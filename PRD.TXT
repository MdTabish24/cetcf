# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Enterprise Educational Portal

## 1. Project Overview
The goal is to build a modern, enterprise-grade educational portal inspired by the structure of NCETIR but with a premium UI/UX, custom company content, and robust backend functionality. The platform will serve as the primary interface for an educational trust/council to manage affiliations, student records, and vocational programs.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Prospective Students:** Looking for job-oriented courses.
- **Institution Owners:** Interested in franchise/affiliation (ASTC).
- **Current Students:** Checking results and verifying certificates.
- **Government/Regulatory Bodies:** Verifying the council's legal standing and affiliations.

## 4. Functional Requirements

### 4.1. Verification Engine (Core Feature)
- **Input:** Roll Number / Enrollment Number.
- **Output:** Dynamic generation of digital marksheets/certificates (view-only).
- **Security:** Rate limiting to prevent database scraping.

### 4.2. Franchise/Affiliation Management
- **Lead Capture:** Detailed form for potential partners.
- **Admin Dashboard:** To track and manage affiliation requests.

### 4.3. Multi-Portal Access
- **Admin Portal:** Manage content, centers, students, and results.
- **Center Portal:** Student registration, fee management, and internal updates.
- **Student Portal (Optional):** Course materials, exam notifications.

### 4.4. Content Management System (CMS)
- Ability to update "Latest Updates" and "Programs" without coding.
- Dynamic addition of new "Affiliations" or "Accreditations".

## 5. Technical Requirements & Modernization (Enterprise Standards)

### 5.1. Modern UI/UX (The "Modern UI" Request)
- **Framework:** React/Next.js for a fast, single-page feel.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS for a clean, professional "Enterprise" look.
- **Components:** 
    - Use **Skeleton Screens** for loading states.
    - **Animated Counters** for statistics.
    - **Responsive Design:** 100% mobile-friendly (critical for students).
    - **Trust Bar:** Instead of a cluttered grid, use a clean, grayscale-to-color hover logo strip.

### 5.2. Backend & Security
- **Database:** PostgreSQL or MySQL for structured student/center data.
- **Authentication:** JWT-based secure login for Admin/Centers.
- **SEO:** Optimized meta-tags for all program pages.

## 6. Content Strategy (Custom Company)
- **Placeholder Strategy:** Use "Company Name" as a variable.
- **Focus:** Shift from generic descriptions to benefit-driven content (e.g., "Become Job Ready in 6 Months" instead of "We provide vocational training").

## 7. Success Metrics
- **Verification Accuracy:** 100% match with database.
- **Load Time:** < 2 seconds for the homepage.
- **Lead Conversion:** Increase in franchise inquiries via the modern form.

## 8. Infrastructure Architecture
- **Hosting Strategy:** VPS (Virtual Private Server) such as Contabo or Hostinger.
- **Why VPS:** The Node.js Express backend heavily utilizes the local filesystem for persistent file storage (`multer` for photos, `pdfkit` for certificates). A VPS guarantees file persistence, avoiding the ephemeral storage limitations (and 4.5MB upload constraints) of serverless platforms like Vercel or Cloud Run.
- **Database:** PostgreSQL installed natively on the VPS or via managed NeonDB.
- **Video Hosting:** Bunny.net (Bunny Stream) to prevent rate-limiting/buffering issues seen with Google Drive, while offering DRM piracy protection at low costs ($0.01/GB).
