FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for CETCF certification?
+
Anyone can apply — students, working professionals, homemakers, or unemployed individuals. No minimum educational qualification required. If you have prior experience (1+ years), use the RPL route to skip training.
What is the difference between Regular and RPL route?
+
Regular route: Enroll at affiliated institute → Train → Exam. RPL route: For 1+ year experienced professionals — directly attempt exam without training.
How is the exam conducted? Is it online?
+
Yes, completely online and automated. Theory: 25 MCQs × 4 marks = 100 marks (30 mins). Practical: 3 photos + 1 video = 100 marks. Total = 200 marks. Pass = 50%.
How do I verify a CETCF certificate?
+
Go to cetcf.org/verify, enter the Certificate ID, and instant verification appears. Or simply scan the QR code on the certificate.
How can my institute get affiliated with CETCF?
+
Apply through Institute Portal → Submit documents → Pay annual fee → Get Center Code within 7 days.
