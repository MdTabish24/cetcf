HR Assistant & Payroll Basics

📚 Sector: Business, Retail & Finance 🎓 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months (8 Weeks / 120 Hours)

Course Overview

Master the fundamentals of Human Resources and Payroll management in just 8 weeks. This certification course equips you with practical skills to manage employee records, process salaries, handle attendance, and support HR operations in Indian organizations. Perfect for those seeking entry-level HR roles in corporate offices, retail chains, and manufacturing units.

This foundation-level course covers essential HR and payroll operations as practiced in Indian companies. You will learn employee onboarding, payroll processing, attendance management, statutory compliance, and basic HR documentation. The course combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on practice using real-world scenarios, spreadsheets, and HR software tools commonly used in India.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior HR or payroll experience required
  • Currently employed in HR role? Apply for RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid government-issued ID proof required (Aadhaar, PAN, Driving License, Passport)
  • Registration fee and exam fee applicable
  • Open to all candidates including persons with disabilities

Modules

Module 1: HR Fundamentals & Organizational Structure

Week 1-2 | 15 Hours

  • Introduction to Human Resource Management — Definition, scope, and importance in Indian organizations
  • HR Functions & Responsibilities — Recruitment, training, compensation, employee relations, and compliance
  • Organizational Structure & Hierarchy — Understanding reporting lines, departments, and roles in typical Indian companies
  • Role of HR Assistant — Daily tasks, responsibilities, and career growth opportunities
  • Indian Labour Laws Overview — Basic understanding of Factory Act, Labour Code, and compliance requirements
  • Types of Organizations — Corporate, SME, Government, NGO, and their HR practices
  • HR Department Structure — Team roles, hierarchy, and collaboration with other departments
  • Workplace Culture & Ethics — Creating positive work environment and maintaining professional conduct
  • Communication Skills for HR — Active listening, written communication, and professional etiquette

Module 2: Recruitment, Selection & Employee Onboarding

Week 2-3 | 18 Hours

  • Recruitment Process in India — Job analysis, vacancy identification, and manpower planning
  • Job Descriptions & Job Specifications — Creating clear job profiles for different roles
  • Advertising & Sourcing — Using job portals (Naukri, Indeed), LinkedIn, and local recruitment methods
  • Screening & Shortlisting — Reviewing CVs, identifying suitable candidates, and initial filtering
  • Interview Process — Types of interviews (HR round, technical, final), conducting and documentation
  • Selection & Offer Letter — Making selection decisions, drafting offer letters, and negotiation basics
  • Employee Onboarding Checklist — Documentation, orientation, system access, and first-day arrangements
  • New Hire Documentation — Collecting Aadhaar, PAN, bank details, emergency contacts, and other forms
  • Induction Training — Introducing company policies, facilities, team members, and role expectations
  • Welcome & Engagement — Creating positive first impression and employee retention from day one
  • Background Verification — Checking references, previous employment, and qualifications
  • Probation Period Management — Monitoring performance, feedback, and confirmation process

Module 3: Payroll Processing & Salary Administration

Week 3-5 | 30 Hours

  • Payroll Basics in India — Understanding salary structure, components, and statutory deductions
  • Salary Components Breakdown — Basic, DA, HRA, conveyance, medical, performance bonus, and other allowances
  • Statutory Deductions — Income Tax (IT), Employee Provident Fund (EPF), Employee State Insurance (ESI), and Professional Tax
  • Gross Salary & Net Salary Calculation — Formula, components, and practical examples
  • Income Tax Slab System — Understanding tax brackets, exemptions, and TDS calculations for FY 2024-25
  • Provident Fund (PF) — EPF contribution (12%), withdrawal rules, and documentation
  • ESI Contribution — Understanding ESI scheme, eligibility, contribution rates, and benefits
  • Professional Tax — State-wise variations, applicability, and calculation
  • Payroll Software Tools — Using Excel, NAVI, Zoho Payroll, and basic HR software for salary processing
  • Monthly Payroll Cycle — Attendance input, leave adjustment, bonus calculation, and salary disbursement
  • Salary Slip Generation — Creating accurate salary slips with all deductions and statutory information
  • Annual Payroll Activities — Bonus, gratuity, leave encashment, and year-end settlements
  • Payroll Reconciliation — Verifying calculations, bank reconciliation, and error correction

Module 4: Attendance, Leave & Time Management

Week 5-6 | 18 Hours

  • Attendance Management Systems — Biometric, card-based, manual registers, and digital apps
  • Recording & Tracking Attendance — Daily mark-in/mark-out, late arrivals, and absenteeism
  • Leave Policy in Indian Companies — Types of leave (casual, sick, earned, privilege, maternity, paternity)
  • Leave Approval Process — Online portals, manager approval workflow, and documentation
  • Leave Calculation & Accrual — Monthly accrual, carryover rules, and leave encashment
  • Attendance Reports — Generating monthly attendance summary, absenteeism reports, and overtime records
  • Shift Management — Understanding shift patterns, night shift allowance, and shift rotation
  • Overtime & Compensation — Calculating overtime pay, compensatory off, and statutory compliance
  • Disciplinary Action for Absenteeism — Warning letters, suspension, and termination procedures
  • Work-From-Home (WFH) & Flexible Work Arrangements — Tracking and documentation in modern organizations
  • Holiday Calendar Management — National holidays, state holidays, and company-specific holidays
  • Attendance Reconciliation — Monthly verification, discrepancy resolution, and payroll integration

Module 5: Employee Records, Compliance & Documentation

Week 6-7 | 20 Hours

  • Employee Records Management — Creating and maintaining personnel files for each employee
  • Personal Information Documentation — Aadhaar, PAN, passport, driving license, and address proof
  • Employment Agreements & Contracts — Understanding employment letter, service agreement, and confidentiality clauses
  • Statutory Registers — Muster roll, wage register, overtime register, and leave register
  • Form 16 & Tax Compliance — Understanding Form 16, TDS certificate, and annual tax filing
  • Provident Fund Documentation — PF nomination, withdrawal forms, and settlement documents
  • ESI Documentation — ESI registration, contribution records, and claim forms
  • Professional Tax Compliance — State-wise requirements and documentation
  • Data Protection & Confidentiality — GDPR awareness, data security, and privacy policy compliance
  • Digital Record Keeping — Using HR software for document storage, backup, and retrieval
  • Audit Trail & Documentation — Maintaining records for statutory audits and compliance checks
  • Exit Documentation — Resignation acceptance, full and final settlement, and employee separation documents

Module 6: Employee Relations, Grievance Handling & HR Operations

Week 7-8 | 19 Hours

  • Employee Relations Fundamentals — Building positive relationships, engagement, and workplace harmony
  • Communication Channels — Email etiquette, internal memos, bulletin boards, and town halls
  • Grievance Handling Process — Receiving complaints, investigation, resolution, and documentation
  • Disciplinary Procedures — Warning letters, suspension, termination, and due process
  • Performance Management Basics — Understanding appraisals, feedback, and performance improvement plans
  • Employee Engagement Activities — Team building, celebrations, recognition programs, and wellness initiatives
  • Conflict Resolution — Mediation, negotiation, and maintaining professional relationships
  • Health & Safety Compliance — Understanding workplace safety, accident reporting, and OSHA requirements
  • Termination & Exit Process — Resignation handling, retirement, retrenchment, and separation formalities
  • Employee Handbook & Policies — Creating, communicating, and maintaining company policies
  • HR Metrics & Analytics — Tracking attrition, retention, cost per hire, and HR KPIs
  • Workplace Harassment Policy — Understanding POSH Act, complaint procedure, and prevention measures

Assessment & Certification

Total Marks: 100 (Pass Percentage: 50%)

Theory (50 Marks): 25 Questions x 2 Marks (Multiple Choice & Short Answer) - 45 minutes

Practical (50 Marks): Candidates must complete real-world HR and payroll tasks using software and documentation

Practical Tasks:

  • Payroll Processing Assignment (15 Marks): Process monthly payroll for 5 sample employees including salary calculation, statutory deductions (IT, EPF, ESI), and generate salary slips using Excel or HR software
  • Employee Onboarding Documentation (12 Marks): Create complete onboarding checklist and documentation package for a new hire including offer letter, employment agreement, and required forms
  • Attendance & Leave Management (10 Marks): Prepare monthly attendance report, calculate leave balance, process leave applications, and generate absenteeism summary for a sample month
  • HR Records & Compliance File (8 Marks): Organize and maintain employee personnel file with all statutory documents, forms, and compliance records in proper order
  • Grievance Resolution Case Study (5 Marks): Handle a sample employee grievance from complaint to resolution, documenting the process and maintaining confidentiality

Learning Outcomes

  • Process monthly payroll accurately including salary calculation, statutory deductions, and salary slip generation
  • Manage employee recruitment, selection, and onboarding with proper documentation and compliance
  • Maintain attendance records, process leave applications, and generate HR reports using software tools
  • Understand and comply with Indian labour laws, statutory requirements, and documentation standards
  • Handle employee grievances, maintain records, and support employee relations activities
  • Use HR software and Excel to manage payroll, attendance, and employee data efficiently
