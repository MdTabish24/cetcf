AI Tools for Everyday Use (ChatGPT etc.)

📚 Sector: Digital & Information Technology 🎓 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 1 Month (4 Weeks / 60 Hours)

Course Overview

Master the essential AI tools transforming how Indians work, learn, and communicate. This foundation course teaches you to leverage ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and other AI platforms for productivity, content creation, and problem-solving in real-world scenarios.

This practical course equips learners with hands-on skills in using modern AI tools for everyday tasks. From writing emails and creating content to automating workflows and learning new skills, you'll discover how AI can enhance your professional and personal life. Designed for the Indian job market, this course emphasizes practical applications in common workplace scenarios.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass (or equivalent)
  • No prior AI or technical experience required
  • Basic computer literacy and internet access needed
  • Already working with AI tools? Apply for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) exam
  • Valid ID proof required at registration (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Passport)
  • Applicable for all candidates across India including rural areas
  • Persons with disabilities welcome with reasonable accommodations

Modules

Module 1: Introduction to AI & Conversational AI Fundamentals

Week 1 | 15 Hours

  • What is Artificial Intelligence? — Understanding AI basics, machine learning, and neural networks in simple terms
  • History and Evolution of AI — From early chatbots to modern LLMs (Large Language Models)
  • Conversational AI Explained — How ChatGPT, Gemini, and similar tools work
  • Large Language Models (LLMs) — Understanding how AI learns from data and generates responses
  • ChatGPT Overview & Registration — Setting up accounts, free vs paid versions, and account management
  • Other Popular AI Tools — Google Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, Claude, and Perplexity AI comparison
  • Ethical Use of AI Tools — Privacy, data security, and responsible AI usage in India
  • AI Limitations and Hallucinations — Understanding when AI makes mistakes and how to verify information
  • Internet Safety & Cybersecurity — Protecting your data when using cloud-based AI tools
  • Indian AI Ecosystem — Government initiatives, Make in India AI projects, and local opportunities

Module 2: Practical ChatGPT Skills for Daily Tasks

Week 1-2 | 15 Hours

  • Crafting Effective Prompts — Writing clear, specific instructions for AI to generate useful responses
  • Professional Email Writing — Using ChatGPT to compose formal emails, applications, and professional correspondence
  • Content Creation & Writing — Generating blog posts, social media captions, product descriptions, and articles
  • Resume & CV Building — Creating ATS-friendly resumes and cover letters with AI assistance
  • Academic Writing Support — Using AI for essay outlines, research summaries, and study notes (ethical usage)
  • Customer Service Responses — Drafting replies to customer complaints and queries
  • Code Generation & Debugging — Using ChatGPT for basic programming help and code explanations
  • Language Translation & Learning — Translating content and learning new languages with AI
  • Brainstorming & Ideation — Using AI for creative ideas for business, projects, and content
  • Data Analysis & Summarization — Extracting insights from text and creating summaries

Module 3: Advanced Prompting Techniques & AI Tool Mastery

Week 2 | 15 Hours

  • Prompt Engineering Fundamentals — Techniques like role-playing, few-shot learning, and chain-of-thought prompting
  • Using System Prompts — Creating custom AI assistants for specific tasks and industries
  • Advanced Prompt Patterns — Problem-solving frameworks, structured output formats, and multi-step instructions
  • ChatGPT Plugins & Integrations — Connecting ChatGPT with web tools, Google Sheets, and productivity apps
  • GPT-4 vs GPT-3.5 — Understanding differences and when to use each version
  • Google Gemini Deep Dive — Features, capabilities, and integration with Google Workspace
  • Microsoft Copilot & Office Integration — Using AI in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Teams
  • Image Generation with AI — Creating visuals using DALL-E, Midjourney alternatives, and text-to-image tools
  • Voice & Speech Features — Using voice input/output with ChatGPT and other AI tools
  • Custom GPTs Creation — Building personalized AI assistants for specific business needs

Module 4: AI Tools for Business & Productivity

Week 3 | 15 Hours

  • Business Automation with AI — Automating routine tasks like data entry, scheduling, and reporting
  • Content Marketing Strategy — Using AI for content calendars, SEO optimization, and keyword research
  • Social Media Management — Scheduling posts, generating captions, and analyzing engagement with AI
  • Email Marketing Automation — Creating email campaigns and personalization using AI tools
  • Market Research & Competitor Analysis — Using AI to gather and analyze business intelligence
  • Financial Planning & Budgeting — AI assistance in financial forecasting and expense tracking
  • Project Management — Using AI for task prioritization, timeline planning, and team coordination
  • Customer Relationship Management (CRM) — AI-powered customer insights and relationship building
  • Sales & Lead Generation — Using AI for sales pitch creation and lead qualification
  • HR & Recruitment — Screening resumes, drafting job descriptions, and interview preparation

Module 5: AI Tools for Learning, Skill Development & Personal Growth

Week 3-4 | 10 Hours

  • Personalized Learning Plans — Creating custom study schedules with AI tutors
  • Exam Preparation Strategy — Using AI for test practice, revision notes, and doubt clearing
  • Skill Development Roadmaps — Planning career growth in AI, coding, digital marketing, and other fields
  • Language Learning with AI — Interactive language learning with ChatGPT and specialized tools
  • Professional Certification Preparation — Using AI for competitive exam coaching (UPSC, SSC, Banking exams)
  • Mental Health & Wellness — AI tools for stress management, meditation guidance, and well-being
  • Hobby Development — Learning music, art, writing, and other creative skills with AI assistance
  • Entrepreneurship & Business Planning — Creating business plans, pitch decks, and startup strategies
  • Networking & Professional Development — Crafting LinkedIn profiles and professional communications

Module 6: Practical Projects, Limitations & Future of AI

Week 4 | 10 Hours

  • Real-World Project 1: Creating a Complete Job Application Package — Resume, cover letter, and interview prep using AI
  • Real-World Project 2: Building a Small Business Marketing Strategy — Content calendar, social posts, and email campaigns
  • Real-World Project 3: Personal Learning Plan — Custom study material for a new skill or certification
  • AI Tool Comparison & Selection — Choosing the right AI tool for different tasks and budgets
  • Fact-Checking & Verification — Methods to verify AI-generated information and avoid misinformation
  • Copyright & Plagiarism Issues — Understanding intellectual property when using AI-generated content
  • Limitations of Current AI Tools — Bias, accuracy issues, and when to use human judgment
  • Future of AI in India — Emerging trends, job opportunities, and skills needed for AI careers
  • Building an AI-Powered Career — Roadmap for pursuing AI-related jobs and entrepreneurship
  • Hands-On Capstone Project — Creating a comprehensive portfolio demonstrating AI tool mastery

Assessment & Certification

Total Marks: 100 (Pass Percentage: 50%)

Theory (50 Marks): 25 Questions x 2 Marks (Multiple Choice, Short Answer) - 45 minutes

Practical (50 Marks): Candidates must complete 3 practical projects demonstrating AI tool proficiency and submit documented evidence

Practical Tasks:

  • Project 1: Professional Communication Package (15 Marks): Create a complete job application package using ChatGPT including: optimized resume, tailored cover letter, and interview preparation notes. Submit screenshots of prompts used and final outputs with explanations.
  • Project 2: Content Creation & Marketing Strategy (15 Marks): Develop a 2-week social media content calendar and 3 different marketing emails using AI tools. Include original prompts, AI outputs, and your refinements with explanations of why changes were made.
  • Project 3: Learning Resource & Skill Development Plan (20 Marks): Create a personalized learning plan for a new skill (coding, language, business skill) using AI. Include study schedule, practice exercises, and revision materials generated with AI, with documentation of tools and prompts used.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand AI fundamentals and how conversational AI tools like ChatGPT work
  • Write effective prompts to get accurate and useful responses from AI tools
  • Use ChatGPT and other AI tools for professional writing, content creation, and communication
  • Apply AI tools to automate business tasks and improve productivity in workplace
  • Create personalized learning plans and develop new skills using AI assistance
  • Evaluate AI-generated content critically, verify information, and use AI ethically and responsibly
