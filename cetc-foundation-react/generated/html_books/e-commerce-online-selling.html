E-Commerce & Online Selling

📚 Sector: Digital & Information Technology 🎓 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months (8 Weeks / 120 Hours)

Course Overview

Master the fundamentals of e-commerce and online selling in India's fastest-growing digital marketplace. This foundation course equips you with essential skills to launch, manage, and scale online businesses across platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, and your own website.

This comprehensive 8-week program covers everything from understanding e-commerce ecosystems and setting up online stores to product listing optimization, digital marketing, payment processing, and customer service. Designed for aspiring entrepreneurs and sales professionals, this course blends theoretical knowledge with practical hands-on training using real Indian e-commerce platforms and tools.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass
  • No prior e-commerce experience required
  • Already running an online business? Apply for direct RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid government ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Driving License) required at registration
  • Basic computer literacy and internet access required
  • Applicable for all Indian citizens and foreign nationals with valid visa
  • No age upper limit — suitable for career changers and retirees

Modules

Module 1: E-Commerce Fundamentals & Market Overview

Week 1 | 15 Hours

  • Introduction to E-Commerce — Definition, evolution, and types (B2C, B2B, C2C, D2C) in Indian context
  • Indian E-Commerce Landscape — Market size, growth trends, major players (Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, Dunzo)
  • E-Commerce Business Models — Understanding marketplace vs. direct-to-consumer models
  • Consumer Behavior in India — Online shopping patterns, payment preferences, regional variations
  • Digital Payment Ecosystem — UPI, e-wallets, net banking, COD adoption in India
  • Legal & Regulatory Framework — GST, FSSAI, e-commerce rules, consumer protection laws
  • Market Research & Opportunity Identification — Finding profitable niches and products
  • Competitive Analysis — Tools and techniques for analyzing competitors on Indian platforms
  • Industry Certifications & Standards — ISO, e-commerce compliance requirements

Module 2: Setting Up Your Online Store & Platform Selection

Week 2 | 15 Hours

  • Choosing Your E-Commerce Platform — Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Shopyfi comparison
  • Registering on Indian Marketplaces — Detailed process for Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho seller accounts
  • Domain Registration & Web Hosting — Namecheap, GoDaddy, Hostinger for Indian sellers
  • Website Design Fundamentals — User experience, mobile optimization, loading speed
  • Payment Gateway Integration — Razorpay, PayU, Instamojo setup and configuration
  • Logistics & Fulfillment Setup — Shipping partners (Delhivery, Ekart, Shiprocket) in India
  • Store Branding & Visual Identity — Logo design, color schemes, brand consistency
  • SSL Certificates & Security — Ensuring customer data protection and trust
  • Analytics Setup — Google Analytics, platform-native dashboards for tracking performance
  • Inventory Management Systems — Stock tracking, warehouse management basics

Module 3: Product Listing Optimization & Content Creation

Week 3 | 15 Hours

  • Product Research & Selection — Identifying profitable products for Indian market
  • Sourcing & Supplier Management — Connecting with manufacturers, wholesalers, dropshipping
  • Product Photography Best Practices — DIY photography, lighting, angles, editing tools
  • Writing Compelling Product Descriptions — SEO-friendly, benefit-focused copy
  • Pricing Strategy — Cost-plus, competitive, psychological pricing for Indian consumers
  • Keyword Research for E-Commerce — Using Ubersuggest, SEMrush, Google Keyword Planner
  • Creating Product Variants — Size, color, material options management
  • Bulk Listing & CSV Upload — Managing large product catalogs efficiently
  • A/B Testing Product Pages — Optimizing conversion rates through testing
  • Managing Product Reviews & Ratings — Encouraging feedback and handling negative reviews
  • Compliance in Product Listings — Accurate specifications, allergen information, certifications

Module 4: Digital Marketing & Customer Acquisition

Week 4 | 15 Hours

  • E-Commerce SEO Basics — On-page optimization, technical SEO, link building for online stores
  • Social Media Marketing for E-Commerce — Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest strategies for selling
  • Content Marketing Strategy — Blogs, videos, infographics to drive organic traffic
  • Email Marketing Campaigns — Building mailing lists, creating promotional sequences
  • Google Shopping & Product Ads — Setting up Google Merchant Center, campaign management
  • Amazon & Flipkart Sponsored Ads — PPC campaigns, bid management, ACOS optimization
  • Influencer & Affiliate Marketing — Partnerships with Indian content creators and bloggers
  • WhatsApp Business for Sales — Using WhatsApp for customer communication and selling
  • Seasonal Campaigns & Festive Marketing — Diwali, Holi, New Year strategies for Indian market
  • Customer Retention & Loyalty Programs — Repeat purchase strategies, rewards systems
  • Analytics & ROI Measurement — Tracking marketing spend, calculating customer acquisition cost

Module 5: Payment Processing, Logistics & Order Management

Week 5-6 | 30 Hours

  • Payment Gateway Operations — Processing transactions, handling failures, refunds
  • Fraud Detection & Prevention — Identifying suspicious orders, chargebacks, dispute resolution
  • Cash on Delivery Management — COD logistics, payment collection, remittance in India
  • International Payments — Handling cross-border transactions, currency conversion
  • Shipping Integration & Automation — Real-time tracking, label generation, carrier selection
  • Warehouse & Fulfillment Operations — Picking, packing, quality checks, returns
  • Order Management System (OMS) — Processing orders, managing backorders, cancellations
  • Return & Refund Management — RMA process, return authorization, restocking procedures
  • Shipping Cost Optimization — Negotiating rates, zone-based pricing, weight optimization
  • Last-Mile Delivery Challenges — Addressing delivery failures, customer expectations in India
  • Inventory Forecasting — Demand planning, stock optimization, preventing stockouts
  • Supplier Coordination — Managing lead times, quality issues, bulk orders

Module 6: Customer Service, Analytics & Business Growth

Week 7-8 | 30 Hours

  • Customer Service Excellence — Handling inquiries, complaints, escalations professionally
  • Multi-Channel Support — Email, chat, phone, social media customer service integration
  • Building Customer Trust — Transparent policies, quality assurance, warranty management
  • Customer Feedback & Review Management — Responding to reviews, improving ratings
  • Business Analytics & KPIs — Conversion rate, AOV, CAC, LTV, profit margin tracking
  • Dashboard Creation & Reporting — Using Google Sheets, Excel, BI tools for insights
  • Scaling Your E-Commerce Business — Growth strategies, expansion to new platforms
  • Marketplace Algorithm Understanding — Amazon A9, Flipkart algorithm for visibility
  • Mobile Commerce (M-Commerce) Optimization — App-based selling, mobile payment trends
  • Subscription Models & Recurring Revenue — Building sustainable income streams
  • Tax Planning & Financial Management — GST compliance, income tax, maintaining records
  • Competitive Benchmarking — Comparing performance with competitors, industry standards

Assessment & Certification

Total Marks: 100 (Pass Percentage: 50%)

Theory (50 Marks): 25 Questions x 2 Marks (Multiple Choice & Short Answer) - 45 minutes

Practical (50 Marks): Hands-on assessment involving setting up and managing an actual online store with real products and transactions

Practical Tasks:

  • Online Store Setup & Configuration (15 Marks): Candidate must set up a complete e-commerce store on chosen platform (Shopify/WooCommerce/Marketplace) with proper branding, payment gateway, and shipping integration. Screenshot documentation required.
  • Product Listing & Optimization (15 Marks): Create and optimize 10 product listings with professional images, SEO-friendly descriptions, pricing, variants, and category placement. Demonstrate keyword research and listing best practices.
  • Digital Marketing Campaign Execution (10 Marks): Design and implement a social media marketing campaign (Instagram/Facebook) and email marketing sequence. Submit campaign strategy, ad creatives, email templates, and analytics screenshots.
  • Order Management & Customer Service Simulation (10 Marks): Handle simulated customer orders including payment processing, order tracking, customer inquiries, and complaint resolution. Demonstrate professional communication and problem-solving.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand e-commerce business models, market dynamics, and regulatory requirements in India
  • Set up and configure a complete online store on multiple platforms with payment and logistics integration
  • Create optimized product listings with professional images and SEO-friendly content
  • Execute digital marketing campaigns across social media, email, and paid advertising channels
  • Manage orders, payments, logistics, and customer service efficiently using industry tools
  • Analyze business metrics, optimize conversion rates, and scale e-commerce operations profitably
