Eyelash & Eyebrow Technician

📚 Sector: Beauty & Lifestyle 🎓 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months (8 Weeks / 120 Hours)

Course Overview

Master the art of professional eyelash extensions and eyebrow designing in just 8 weeks. This foundation course teaches you industry-standard techniques used in salons and beauty studios across India. Get certified and start earning immediately as a skilled beauty technician.

This comprehensive program covers everything from basic anatomy of the eye area to advanced lash extension application, eyebrow shaping, and client care. You will learn both manual and semi-permanent techniques, safety protocols, product knowledge, and business fundamentals. Includes hands-on practice with real clients and certification upon completion.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum age: 14 years (16 years preferred)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass
  • No prior experience required
  • Already working as eyelash/eyebrow technician? Apply for RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid government ID proof required (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Passport)
  • Medical fitness certificate for health & safety compliance
  • Open for all genders and categories

Modules

Module 1: Foundation & Safety Basics

Week 1 | 15 Hours

  • Introduction to Eyelash & Eyebrow Industry in India — career opportunities, market demand, and salon types
  • Anatomy of the Eye Area — eyelid structure, lash growth cycle, eyebrow muscles, and skin types around eyes
  • Health & Safety Standards — hygiene protocols, infection control, sterilization of tools, and workplace safety
  • Cosmetic Regulations in India — understanding FSSAI, DCGI guidelines, and product safety standards
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) — proper use of gloves, masks, aprons, and eye protection
  • Common Eye Conditions & Contraindications — identifying when NOT to perform services (infections, allergies, skin conditions)
  • Client Consultation Skills — asking the right questions, understanding client needs, and managing expectations
  • Workspace Setup & Organization — salon hygiene, tool arrangement, lighting requirements, and ventilation

Module 2: Eyelash Extension Fundamentals

Week 2-3 | 30 Hours

  • Types of Eyelash Extensions — synthetic vs. mink vs. silk, volume lashes, mega volume, and hybrid techniques
  • Lash Extension Products & Tools — understanding adhesives, primers, removers, applicators, and quality standards
  • Natural Lash Assessment — identifying lash thickness, length, strength, and determining suitable extension types
  • Lash Extension Application Techniques — isolation methods, adhesive application, placement angles, and precision
  • Classic Lash Extensions — one-to-one application, duration, maintenance, and pricing strategy in Indian market
  • Volume Lash Extensions — fan creation, weight distribution, and application on sparse natural lashes
  • Aftercare Instructions — client guidance on washing, drying, sleeping position, and product recommendations
  • Common Mistakes & Troubleshooting — preventing lash loss, managing adhesive issues, and correcting application errors
  • Lash Extension Removal Safely — using removers, preventing damage to natural lashes, and client comfort
  • Pricing & Costing — calculating service cost, understanding salon margins, and competitive pricing in your city
  • Building Client Retention — loyalty programs, follow-up care, and upselling complementary services

Module 3: Eyebrow Design & Shaping

Week 4 | 15 Hours

  • Eyebrow Anatomy & Growth Pattern — understanding hair growth, density, and natural arch position
  • Face Shape Analysis — oval, round, square, heart, oblong faces and suitable eyebrow shapes for each
  • Eyebrow Design Methods — threading, waxing, plucking, and microblading basics for eyebrow technicians
  • Eyebrow Shaping Tools — understanding tweezers, razors, threading needles, and waxing equipment
  • Threading Technique — proper hand positioning, thread tension, and creating clean, precise lines
  • Eyebrow Waxing & Tinting — application methods, product selection, and color matching for Indian skin tones
  • Eyebrow Penciling & Makeup — filling sparse areas, creating natural-looking brows, and product recommendations
  • Correcting Over-Plucked Brows — regrowth strategies and temporary solutions for clients with damaged brows
  • Sensitive Skin Management — handling allergies, redness, and post-procedure care for sensitive clients
  • Eyebrow Trends in India — current styles, celebrity influences, and adapting trends for your clientele

Module 4: Semi-Permanent Eyebrow Solutions

Week 5 | 15 Hours

  • Henna Eyebrow Tinting — application techniques, color selection, and longevity expectations
  • Eyebrow Henna Products — understanding natural vs. chemical henna, quality assessment, and sourcing from reliable suppliers
  • Microblading Introduction — understanding the technique, tools, and when to refer clients to certified microblading artists
  • Eyebrow Tint & Lamination — combining tinting with lamination for fuller appearance and longer-lasting results
  • Patch Testing & Allergy Protocols — conducting proper patch tests, documenting allergies, and client safety
  • Color Theory for Eyebrows — understanding undertones, selecting colors for different skin types, and Indian beauty standards
  • Semi-Permanent Eyebrow Maintenance — touch-up schedules, cost structure, and client education
  • Handling Adverse Reactions — managing allergic reactions, burns, and when to seek medical help
  • Product Knowledge — understanding ingredients, shelf life, storage conditions, and supplier verification in India

Module 5: Client Care, Hygiene & Business Skills

Week 6-7 | 30 Hours

  • Client Communication & Consultation — active listening, managing difficult clients, and building trust
  • Record Keeping & Consent Forms — maintaining client records, photography consent, allergy documentation, and legal compliance
  • Sterilization & Sanitization Protocols — understanding autoclave, chemical sterilization, and daily cleaning procedures
  • Infection Control Standards — preventing cross-contamination, managing used materials, and waste disposal
  • Allergies & Sensitivities — identifying common allergens, conducting patch tests, and managing reactions
  • Professional Appearance & Etiquette — dress code, personal hygiene, punctuality, and client-facing behavior
  • Pricing Strategies — understanding cost of materials, labor, salon overhead, and competitive pricing in your region
  • Social Media Marketing for Beauty Services — using Instagram, WhatsApp, and Facebook for client acquisition
  • Booking Systems & Client Management — managing appointments, cancellations, no-shows, and customer database
  • Building Your Personal Brand — creating a portfolio, before-after photos, testimonials, and online presence
  • Upselling & Cross-Selling — recommending complementary services, eyebrow aftercare products, and lash serums
  • Customer Retention Strategies — loyalty programs, referral bonuses, seasonal offers, and repeat bookings

Module 6: Practical Application & Certification

Week 8 | 15 Hours

  • Supervised Practice Sessions — working on live models under expert guidance with feedback
  • Speed & Accuracy Development — improving application time while maintaining quality standards
  • Portfolio Building — creating professional before-after documentation for salon/personal use
  • Mock Client Interactions — role-playing real salon scenarios, handling complaints, and professional communication
  • Quality Assessment Checklist — understanding grading criteria, identifying perfect applications, and self-evaluation
  • Troubleshooting Real Scenarios — addressing common issues faced in actual salon environment
  • Final Practical Exam Preparation — mock exams, time management, and stress handling
  • Certification Requirements — understanding CETCF standards, exam format, and post-certification support

Assessment & Certification

Total Marks: 100 (Pass Percentage: 50%)

Theory (50 Marks): 25 Questions x 2 Marks each (Multiple Choice & Short Answer) - 45 minutes

Practical (50 Marks): Live demonstration of eyelash extension and eyebrow design on models with quality assessment

Practical Tasks:

  • Eyelash Extension Application (25 Marks): Candidate must apply classic lash extensions on both eyes of a live model, demonstrating proper isolation, adhesive application, and placement. Assessment includes speed, precision, client comfort, and lash retention.
  • Eyebrow Design & Shaping (15 Marks): Candidate must analyze face shape, design suitable eyebrow shape, and execute threading or waxing with clean lines. Assessment includes design appropriateness, symmetry, precision, and client satisfaction.
  • Client Consultation & Hygiene Protocol (10 Marks): Candidate must conduct proper client consultation, maintain hygiene standards, demonstrate sterilization procedures, and explain aftercare instructions to examiner.

Learning Outcomes

  • Apply professional eyelash extensions using correct techniques and quality materials
  • Design and shape eyebrows according to face shape and client preferences
  • Maintain strict hygiene, sterilization, and infection control standards
  • Conduct proper client consultation, manage expectations, and handle difficult situations
  • Identify contraindications and manage allergic reactions appropriately
  • Build a sustainable beauty business with pricing, marketing, and client retention strategies
