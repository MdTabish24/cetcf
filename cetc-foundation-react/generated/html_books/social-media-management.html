Social Media Management

📚 Sector: Digital & Information Technology 🎓 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months (8 Weeks / 120 Hours)

Course Overview

Master the art and science of social media management in just 8 weeks. This foundation-level certification equips you with practical skills to manage brands, create engaging content, and build communities across Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and YouTube. Perfect for aspiring digital marketers, entrepreneurs, and professionals looking to establish a career in India's fastest-growing digital sector.

This comprehensive course covers the complete social media management lifecycle—from platform strategy and content creation to community engagement, analytics, and crisis management. You'll learn industry-standard tools, real-world case studies from Indian brands, and hands-on techniques used by successful agencies and in-house teams across India. By course completion, you'll be job-ready for entry-level positions or capable of managing social media for small businesses independently.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum age: 14 years (with parental consent)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior experience in social media management required
  • Basic computer and internet literacy essential
  • Already working as social media professional? Apply for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) exam
  • Valid government-issued ID proof required at registration (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Passport)
  • Email ID and active social media accounts (for practical demonstrations) required
  • Applicable for all candidates across India; special consideration for SC/ST/OBC as per government norms

Modules

Module 1: Foundations of Social Media & Digital Landscape

Week 1 | 15 Hours

  • Evolution of social media globally and in India — understanding platform growth, user demographics, and market trends
  • Social media ecosystem overview — exploring major platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, Pinterest) and their unique audiences
  • Role of social media in business — brand awareness, customer engagement, lead generation, and sales conversion
  • Social media vs. traditional marketing — cost-effectiveness, reach, measurability, and real-time engagement advantages
  • Indian social media market statistics — user base, platform preferences, mobile-first adoption, and regional language trends
  • Digital consumer behavior in India — purchasing patterns, content preferences, trust factors, and seasonal trends
  • Career opportunities in social media management — job roles, skill requirements, and growth potential in Indian agencies and companies
  • Industry certifications and standards — CETCF, Google Digital Garage, Facebook Blueprint, and LinkedIn Learning pathways

Module 2: Platform Mastery & Account Setup

Week 2 | 18 Hours

  • Facebook business setup — creating business pages, configuring settings, understanding page roles, and verification process
  • Instagram for business — converting to business account, setting up Instagram Shopping, bio optimization, and link-in-bio strategies
  • Twitter/X professional accounts — handle selection, verified badge process, profile optimization, and tweet scheduling
  • LinkedIn company pages — creating professional profiles, employee advocacy setup, and B2B content strategies
  • YouTube channel creation — channel branding, thumbnail design, playlist organization, and monetization eligibility
  • TikTok for business — understanding algorithm, creating business account, and leveraging trending sounds and hashtags
  • Pinterest business account — setting up boards, rich pins, and utilizing Pinterest for e-commerce and traffic generation
  • Cross-platform consistency — maintaining brand voice, visual identity, and messaging across all channels
  • Profile optimization best practices — keywords, CTAs, contact information, and conversion-focused design
  • Account security and compliance — two-factor authentication, password management, and data privacy regulations in India

Module 3: Content Creation & Strategy Development

Week 3-4 | 30 Hours

  • Content pillars and themes — identifying brand values, audience interests, and content categories for consistent messaging
  • Editorial calendar creation — monthly planning, seasonal campaigns, product launches, and festive content calendars relevant to India
  • Copywriting for social media — crafting engaging captions, CTAs, headlines, and platform-specific messaging
  • Visual content creation — photography basics, image sizing for each platform, design tools (Canva, Adobe Express), and brand guidelines
  • Video content strategy — short-form vs. long-form, Reels, Stories, YouTube Shorts, and TikTok video best practices
  • Hashtag research and strategy — finding trending hashtags, creating branded hashtags, and hashtag campaigns for Indian audiences
  • User-generated content (UGC) — encouraging customer posts, running contests, and building community-driven content
  • Storytelling techniques — narrative arcs, emotional connection, brand storytelling, and Indian cultural sensitivity
  • Trending content and memes — staying current, participating in trends responsibly, and creating shareable moments
  • Content repurposing — converting blog posts to social snippets, turning videos into multiple formats, and maximizing content ROI
  • Accessibility in content — alt text, captions, inclusive language, and designing for diverse audiences
  • Crisis communication through content — handling negative feedback, transparent messaging, and maintaining brand reputation

Module 4: Community Management & Engagement

Week 5 | 15 Hours

  • Community building fundamentals — fostering loyal followers, creating safe spaces, and building brand advocates
  • Response protocols and guidelines — managing comments, DMs, mentions, and establishing community standards
  • Engagement best practices — replying timely, asking questions, running polls, and creating conversations
  • Influencer collaboration — identifying micro-influencers, partnerships, gifting, and measuring influencer ROI
  • Customer service via social media — handling complaints, resolving issues publicly, and turning customers into promoters
  • Moderation and content filtering — spam management, inappropriate content removal, and community guidelines enforcement
  • Building communities on WhatsApp, Telegram, and Discord — group management, exclusive content, and community events
  • Loyalty programs and rewards — exclusive discounts, early access, gamification, and retention strategies
  • Events and live sessions — hosting Instagram Live, Facebook Live, YouTube Live, and Q&A sessions for audience interaction
  • Sentiment analysis — understanding audience emotions, identifying brand perception, and responding to feedback constructively

Module 5: Analytics, Insights & Performance Measurement

Week 6 | 18 Hours

  • Social media metrics fundamentals — impressions, reach, engagement rate, click-through rate, and conversion metrics
  • Platform-native analytics — Facebook Insights, Instagram Insights, Twitter Analytics, YouTube Analytics, and LinkedIn Analytics
  • Google Analytics for social media — tracking social traffic, conversion funnels, and ROI measurement
  • Third-party analytics tools — Hootsuite, Buffer, Sprout Social, Later, and native Indian tools for small businesses
  • KPI identification and tracking — setting SMART goals, selecting relevant metrics, and aligning with business objectives
  • Audience insights and demographics — understanding follower profiles, behavior patterns, and targeting opportunities
  • Content performance analysis — identifying top-performing posts, optimal posting times, and content gaps
  • Competitor analysis — monitoring competitor strategies, benchmarking performance, and identifying market opportunities
  • Reporting and dashboards — creating monthly reports, visualizing data, and presenting insights to stakeholders
  • A/B testing strategies — testing copy, visuals, posting times, and CTAs to optimize performance
  • ROI calculation for social media — attributing revenue to social campaigns, calculating customer acquisition cost, and justifying budgets
  • Data privacy and compliance — GDPR, India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, and ethical data handling

Module 6: Paid Advertising, Tools & Professional Practice

Week 7-8 | 24 Hours

  • Social media advertising fundamentals — organic vs. paid reach, ad objectives, and budget allocation strategies
  • Facebook Ads Manager — campaign setup, audience targeting, creative optimization, and conversion tracking
  • Instagram advertising — Story ads, Feed ads, Reels ads, Shopping ads, and carousel ads
  • LinkedIn advertising — sponsored content, InMail, lead generation ads, and B2B campaign strategies
  • Twitter/X advertising — promoted tweets, trends, and audience targeting for real-time marketing
  • YouTube advertising — skippable ads, non-skippable ads, bumper ads, and channel memberships
  • TikTok advertising — native ads, branded hashtag challenges, and reaching Gen Z audiences in India
  • Audience segmentation and targeting — demographics, interests, behaviors, lookalike audiences, and custom audiences
  • Budget management and bidding strategies — CPC, CPM, CPA, daily budgets, and cost optimization techniques
  • Ad creative best practices — mobile-first design, compelling copy, clear CTAs, and platform-specific requirements
  • Conversion tracking and pixel implementation — Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics integration, and event tracking
  • Social media management tools — Hootsuite, Buffer, Later, Meta Business Suite, and content scheduling platforms
  • Brand safety and ad policy compliance — avoiding content violations, advertiser-friendly guidelines, and maintaining brand reputation
  • Professional ethics and best practices — transparency, authenticity, avoiding misinformation, and responsible marketing in India

Assessment & Certification

Total Marks: 100 (Pass Percentage: 50%)

Theory (50 Marks): 25 Questions x 2 Marks (Multiple Choice & Short Answer) - 45 minutes

Practical (50 Marks): Candidates must complete real-world social media management projects demonstrating platform proficiency, content creation, analytics interpretation, and campaign execution.

Practical Tasks:

  • Complete Social Media Audit & Strategy Document (15 Marks): Analyze an assigned brand's current social media presence across 3 platforms, identify gaps, and present a 4-week content strategy with editorial calendar, hashtag research, and KPI targets.
  • Content Creation Portfolio (15 Marks): Create and submit 8 original posts (mix of images, videos, captions) optimized for different platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter), demonstrating platform-specific best practices and brand consistency.
  • Analytics & Reporting Assignment (10 Marks): Analyze provided social media campaign data using Google Analytics and platform insights, create a professional dashboard/report with visualizations, and present actionable recommendations for optimization.
  • Paid Campaign Setup & Optimization (10 Marks): Set up a Facebook/Instagram ad campaign for a given product/service, configure audience targeting, create ad creatives, and document the setup process with screenshots and optimization strategy.

Learning Outcomes

  • Successfully create and optimize business profiles on 5+ major social media platforms following best practices
  • Develop comprehensive social media strategies aligned with business goals, including content calendars and KPI targets
  • Produce high-quality, platform-optimized content (text, images, videos) that drives engagement and conversions
  • Analyze social media performance using analytics tools and present data-driven insights and recommendations to stakeholders
  • Execute and optimize paid social media advertising campaigns with proper audience targeting and budget management
  • Manage online communities professionally, handle customer service inquiries, and maintain brand reputation across channels
  • Apply crisis management and ethical practices in social media marketing while complying with Indian regulations
