Tally Prime & GST Accounting

📚 Sector: Digital & Information Technology 🎓 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 3 Months (12 Weeks / 180 Hours)

Course Overview

Master Tally Prime and GST compliance with this industry-focused certification course designed for aspiring accounting professionals in India. Gain practical skills in digital bookkeeping, invoice management, and tax filing required by modern Indian businesses.

This comprehensive program covers Tally Prime software fundamentals, GST accounting procedures, statutory compliance, and real-world business scenarios. Participants will develop proficiency in maintaining digital ledgers, generating financial reports, and managing GST returns for small and medium enterprises across India.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior accounting experience required
  • Basic computer literacy (Windows/Mac navigation, typing)
  • Already working as accountant? Apply for direct RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid ID proof required (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Passport)
  • Applicable for all Indian citizens and foreign nationals with valid work permits
  • No upper age limit — career changers and senior professionals welcome

Modules

Module 1: Fundamentals of Accounting & Tally Prime Introduction

Week 1-2 | 30 Hours

  • Introduction to Tally Prime — features, advantages, and interface overview for Indian businesses
  • Basic accounting principles — debit-credit rules, journal entries, and ledger concepts
  • Chart of Accounts — classification of accounts (Assets, Liabilities, Capital, Income, Expenses) as per Indian standards
  • Company creation in Tally Prime — setting up financial year (April-March), currency, and fiscal settings
  • Master data creation — creating groups and ledgers for various account categories
  • Data entry fundamentals — navigating menus, shortcuts, and daily transaction recording
  • Voucher types in Tally — Journal, Contra, Sales, Purchase, Receipt, Payment vouchers explained
  • Backup and security — protecting data with passwords and regular backup procedures
  • Multi-user setup — understanding user roles and permissions for team-based accounting
  • Customization basics — modifying reports and screens for business-specific needs

Module 2: Day-to-Day Transaction Recording & Inventory Management

Week 3-4 | 30 Hours

  • Purchase transactions — recording supplier invoices, credit notes, and debit notes in Tally Prime
  • Sales transactions — documenting customer invoices, returns, and discounts with GST implications
  • Bank reconciliation — matching bank statements with Tally records and identifying discrepancies
  • Cash management — recording petty cash, imprest accounts, and cash flow statements
  • Inventory setup — creating stock groups, items, units of measurement (kg, liters, pieces, etc.)
  • Stock valuation methods — FIFO, LIFO, and weighted average inventory costing in Tally
  • Goods receipt and issue — recording inward and outward stock movements with batch tracking
  • Stock adjustment — handling inventory write-offs, damages, and stock reconciliation
  • Inventory reports — generating stock summaries, aging analysis, and reorder level reports
  • Inter-warehouse transfers — managing stock movement between multiple business locations
  • Serial number tracking — maintaining records for high-value items and warranty management
  • Barcode integration — using barcode scanning for efficient inventory management

Module 3: GST (Goods and Services Tax) Fundamentals & Compliance

Week 5-6 | 30 Hours

  • GST overview — understanding the 4-tier tax structure (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) and exemptions applicable in India
  • GST registration process — eligibility criteria, online registration, and GSTIN allocation
  • HSN and SAC codes — classification of goods and services for correct GST rate application
  • Input Tax Credit (ITC) — eligibility, claiming, and restrictions under GST rules
  • GST on different transactions — supplies, reverse charge mechanism, and composition scheme
  • Inter-state and intra-state supplies — understanding CGST, SGST, IGST, and cess implications
  • GST on imports and exports — special provisions for international transactions
  • E-way bill — generation, validity, and management for goods movement across states
  • Place of supply rules — determining tax jurisdiction for services and goods
  • GST liability and payment — calculating tax liability, payment deadlines, and penalties for non-compliance
  • Special categories — GST treatment for agriculture, jewelry, and services sector
  • Recent GST amendments — staying updated with latest rule changes and notifications

Module 4: GST Return Filing & Tally Prime Integration

Week 7-8 | 30 Hours

  • GSTR-1 preparation — recording outward supplies and generating monthly returns in Tally
  • GSTR-2A reconciliation — matching supplier invoices with GST portal data
  • GSTR-3B filing — calculating net GST payable, ITC claims, and payment schedules
  • GSTR-4 for composition dealers — simplified return filing for small businesses
  • GSTR-5 for non-resident taxable persons — documentation and compliance requirements
  • GSTR-6 for input service distributors — allocation and tracking of input credit
  • GSTR-7 for tax collectors — TDS and TCS reporting requirements
  • GSTR-8 for e-commerce platforms — managing supplies through digital channels
  • GSTR-9 annual return — preparing annual reconciliation and financial statements
  • Tally GST configuration — setting up HSN codes, tax rates, and return settings
  • Automated GST calculations — using Tally formulas for accurate tax computation
  • Common GST errors and corrections — handling amendments, reversals, and credit notes

Module 5: Financial Reporting & Analysis

Week 9-10 | 30 Hours

  • Trial Balance — verifying accuracy of all ledger entries and identifying posting errors
  • Balance Sheet preparation — assets, liabilities, and equity classification as per Indian Companies Act
  • Profit & Loss Statement — revenue, expenses, and net profit calculation for business performance analysis
  • Cash Flow Statement — direct and indirect methods for analyzing cash movements
  • Ratio analysis — profitability, liquidity, and solvency ratios for business health assessment
  • Tally reports — accessing standard and custom reports for decision-making
  • Comparative analysis — year-on-year and month-on-month performance evaluation
  • Audit trail — maintaining transaction history and ensuring data integrity
  • Reconciliation statements — bank, GST, and inter-company reconciliations
  • Financial statement notes — disclosures required under Indian accounting standards
  • Statutory compliance reports — generating documents for income tax and corporate filings
  • Management reports — cost center analysis, profit center analysis, and departmental performance

Module 6: Practical Implementation & Real-World Scenarios

Week 11-12 | 30 Hours

  • Case study 1 — Setting up a retail business in Tally Prime with GST compliance
  • Case study 2 — Managing multi-location inventory for e-commerce operations
  • Case study 3 — Handling GST return filing for a service-based company
  • Case study 4 — Processing international transactions with export documentation
  • Case study 5 — Managing GST corrections, amendments, and credit note reversals
  • Practical exercise — Creating complete month-end closing and financial statements
  • Payroll integration — recording employee salaries with TDS and statutory deductions
  • Fixed asset management — depreciation, asset registers, and disposal accounting
  • TDS/TCS procedures — withholding tax compliance and quarterly filings
  • Year-end closing procedures — accruals, provisions, and financial statement finalization
  • Data migration and upgrades — transferring data between versions and backup recovery
  • Troubleshooting common issues — resolving posting errors, reconciliation mismatches, and software glitches

Assessment & Certification

Total Marks: 100 (Pass Percentage: 50%)

Theory (50 Marks): 25 Questions x 2 Marks (Multiple Choice, Short Answer, and Scenario-based) - 45 minutes

Practical (50 Marks): Hands-on Tally Prime tasks including transaction recording, GST return filing, and financial report generation

Practical Tasks:

  • Company Setup & Master Data Creation (10 Marks): Create a complete company profile in Tally Prime with Chart of Accounts, stock items, and customer/supplier master data
  • Transaction Recording & Inventory Management (15 Marks): Record 20+ purchase and sales transactions with GST, manage inventory movements, and reconcile stock
  • GST Return Filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B) (15 Marks): Prepare and file monthly GST returns using Tally data, reconcile ITC, and generate compliance reports
  • Financial Statement Generation & Analysis (10 Marks): Generate Trial Balance, Balance Sheet, P&L Statement, and provide business performance analysis with ratios

Learning Outcomes

  • Proficiently operate Tally Prime software for daily accounting transactions and financial management
  • Accurately apply GST rules, rates, and compliance procedures for Indian businesses
  • Prepare and file statutory GST returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B) with confidence
  • Generate accurate financial statements (Balance Sheet, P&L, Cash Flow) as per Indian standards
  • Manage inventory, track stock movements, and perform inventory reconciliation
  • Identify and resolve common accounting errors, reconciliation issues, and data integrity problems
  • Maintain audit trails, ensure data security, and implement backup procedures
  • Provide financial analysis and business insights using Tally reports and ratio analysis
