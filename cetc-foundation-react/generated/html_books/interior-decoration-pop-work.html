Interior Decoration & POP Work

📚 Sector: Construction & Building Trades 🎓 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months (8 Weeks / 120 Hours)

Course Overview

Master the art and science of interior decoration and POP (Plaster of Paris) ceiling work. This foundation course trains you to create beautiful, functional interior spaces using modern techniques and traditional craftsmanship. Perfect for anyone wanting to start a career in India's booming construction and interior design industry.

Learn professional interior decoration skills including wall finishes, POP ceiling installation, false ceiling design, color theory, space planning, and finishing touches. This hands-on course covers both residential and commercial projects, preparing you for immediate employment or self-employment in India's growing construction sector.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum age: 14 years (with parental consent for 14-16 age group)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior experience needed — beginners welcome
  • Already working in interior decoration? Apply for direct RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid government ID proof required (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Passport)
  • Medical fitness certificate may be required for height work
  • Applicable for all candidates regardless of gender, caste, religion, or background

Modules

Module 1: Fundamentals of Interior Decoration & POP Work

Week 1-2 | 20 Hours

  • Introduction to interior decoration industry in India — career opportunities, market demand, growth trends
  • Understanding POP (Plaster of Paris) — composition, types, grades, and applications in construction
  • Safety and personal protective equipment (PPE) — helmets, gloves, masks, safety shoes, eye protection
  • Tools and equipment identification — trowels, floats, mixers, scaffolding, measuring instruments
  • Basic mathematics for construction — measurements, calculations, area and perimeter, material estimation
  • Introduction to interior spaces — residential, commercial, institutional, industrial applications
  • Quality standards and building codes — Indian Standards (IS), National Building Code (NBC) compliance
  • Environmental awareness and sustainable interior practices — eco-friendly materials, waste management
  • Health hazards in POP work — dust control, ventilation requirements, ergonomics
  • Introduction to design principles — balance, proportion, color harmony, space utilization

Module 2: Surface Preparation & Wall Finishing Techniques

Week 2-3 | 18 Hours

  • Wall surface assessment and preparation — cleaning, leveling, moisture checking, crack repair
  • Plastering basics — cement plaster composition, sand grades, water quality, mixing ratios
  • Single coat and double coat plastering techniques — application methods, thickness standards
  • Putty application — types of putty, application tools, finishing smooth surfaces
  • Sanding and smoothing techniques — sandpaper grades, hand sanding vs machine sanding, dust control
  • Paint primer selection and application — types of primers, coverage calculation, application methods
  • Texture finishes — spray texture, roller texture, brush texture, design variations
  • Wall finishes — matte, glossy, semi-gloss finishes, paint quality standards
  • Moisture and water-proofing — dampness identification, waterproofing coatings, prevention measures
  • Common defects and remedies — peeling, cracking, blistering, efflorescence, solutions

Module 3: POP Ceiling Installation & False Ceiling Systems

Week 3-5 | 25 Hours

  • False ceiling systems overview — suspended ceilings, drop ceilings, grid systems, advantages
  • POP ceiling materials — POP boards, gypsum boards, acoustic panels, metal frames, suspension systems
  • Ceiling design and planning — layout drawings, measurement techniques, load calculations
  • Grid and frame installation — metal channel fixing, suspension wire installation, leveling procedures
  • POP board cutting and installation — cutting techniques, joining methods, fastening systems
  • Finishing POP ceilings — joint filling, sanding, painting, texture application
  • Decorative ceiling designs — cove ceilings, coffered ceilings, tray ceilings, curved designs
  • Lighting integration in false ceilings — recessed lights, LED strips, downlights, wiring safety
  • Acoustic ceilings — sound absorption properties, installation methods, maintenance
  • Common ceiling defects and troubleshooting — sagging, cracking, water damage, repair procedures
  • Cost estimation for ceiling work — material quantity calculation, labor estimation, project budgeting
  • Safety in height work — scaffolding safety, fall protection, working at heights regulations

Module 4: Color Theory, Design Principles & Space Planning

Week 5-6 | 18 Hours

  • Color theory fundamentals — primary, secondary, tertiary colors, color wheel, color psychology
  • Color combinations — complementary, analogous, triadic, monochromatic color schemes
  • Color selection for different spaces — living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens, bathrooms, commercial spaces
  • Lighting effects on colors — natural light, artificial light, color rendering index (CRI), light temperature
  • Space planning principles — traffic flow, furniture arrangement, scale and proportion
  • Small space optimization — visual tricks, storage solutions, multi-functional design
  • Design styles — modern, traditional, minimalist, industrial, eclectic, fusion styles
  • Texture and material selection — wall textures, flooring materials, fabric combinations
  • Creating mood and atmosphere — warm vs cool spaces, formal vs casual designs
  • Client consultation and requirement gathering — understanding client needs, budget planning, timeline

Assessment & Certification

Total Marks: 100 (Pass Percentage: 50%)

Theory (50 Marks): 25 Questions x 2 Marks (Multiple Choice and Short Answer) - 45 minutes

Practical (50 Marks): Hands-on demonstration of POP ceiling installation, wall finishing, and decorative application with quality and time assessment

Practical Tasks:

  • POP Ceiling Installation & Finishing (20 Marks): Candidate must install a false ceiling with POP boards on a 4x4 feet grid, including proper joint filling, sanding, and painting. Assessment includes accuracy of installation, leveling, joint finishing quality, and overall appearance.
  • Wall Finishing & Texture Application (15 Marks): Candidate must prepare a wall surface, apply putty, sand smoothly, apply primer and paint with chosen texture finish on a 2x3 feet wall section. Assessment includes surface smoothness, texture uniformity, paint coverage, and professional finish.
  • Decorative Design Implementation (15 Marks): Candidate must design and execute a decorative element such as a painted mural, stencil design, or decorative finish on a 2x2 feet surface. Assessment includes design creativity, color harmony, precision, and artistic quality.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand and apply safety standards and best practices in interior decoration and POP work
  • Prepare surfaces and execute professional wall finishes including plastering, putty, and painting
  • Install and finish false ceilings with POP boards, grid systems, and decorative designs
  • Apply color theory and design principles to create aesthetically pleasing interior spaces
  • Execute decorative finishes and specialty work including textures, murals, and artistic elements
  • Plan, estimate costs, and manage interior decoration projects from conception to completion
