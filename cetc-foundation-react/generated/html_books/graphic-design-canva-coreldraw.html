Graphic Design — Canva & CorelDRAW

📚 Sector: Digital & Information Technology 🎓 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months (8 Weeks / 120 Hours)

Course Overview

Master modern graphic design using industry-standard tools Canva and CorelDRAW. This foundation course equips you with practical skills to create professional marketing materials, social media graphics, and print designs—essential for today's digital-first businesses across India.

This comprehensive 120-hour program covers design fundamentals, typography, color theory, and hands-on training in both Canva (cloud-based, user-friendly) and CorelDRAW (professional vector graphics). You'll create real-world projects including social media posts, brochures, logos, and marketing collateral while understanding Indian design market demands and client expectations.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior graphic design experience required
  • Basic computer literacy (familiarity with Windows/Mac operating systems)
  • Access to computer with Canva account (free or paid) and CorelDRAW installation
  • Already working as a designer? Apply for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) exam
  • Valid government-issued ID proof required at registration (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, or Voter ID)
  • Applicable for all candidates including school students, unemployed youth, and working professionals

Modules

Module 1: Design Fundamentals & Industry Introduction

Week 1 | 15 Hours

  • Introduction to graphic design industry in India — market overview, job roles, and growth opportunities
  • Design principles — balance, contrast, emphasis, movement, pattern, and unity in visual communication
  • Understanding color theory — RGB vs CMYK color modes, color psychology, and cultural color preferences in Indian markets
  • Typography basics — font families, serif vs sans-serif, font pairing, and readability standards
  • Composition and layout principles — rule of thirds, golden ratio, white space, and grid systems
  • Design for different mediums — digital vs print, resolution requirements (72 dpi vs 300 dpi), and file formats
  • Indian design aesthetics — incorporating traditional motifs, regional styles, and cultural sensitivities
  • Client communication and design briefs — understanding requirements, feedback loops, and revision management
  • Professional design ethics and copyright — intellectual property, licensing, and avoiding plagiarism

Module 2: Canva Mastery for Quick Design Solutions

Week 2 | 15 Hours

  • Canva interface and workspace setup — navigation, tools, templates, and customization options
  • Creating social media graphics — Instagram posts, Stories, Reels covers, Facebook banners, and LinkedIn templates
  • Designing marketing collateral — posters, flyers, brochures, and business cards using Canva templates
  • Text and typography in Canva — font selection, styling, effects, and creating visual hierarchy
  • Working with images and illustrations — stock photos, uploading custom images, removing backgrounds, and image editing
  • Creating presentations and infographics — slide design, data visualization, and animated presentations
  • Canva Pro features and premium assets — accessing premium templates, fonts, stock images, and brand kit creation
  • Exporting and file management — downloading in various formats (PNG, PDF, MP4), quality settings, and cloud storage
  • Practical project: Create a complete social media campaign (5 posts) for a fictional Indian startup

Module 3: CorelDRAW Professional Vector Graphics — Part 1

Week 3-4 | 30 Hours

  • CorelDRAW workspace fundamentals — interface, toolbars, docking panels, and customization for efficiency
  • Vector vs raster graphics — understanding paths, nodes, curves, and why vectors are essential for professional design
  • Drawing tools and shape creation — rectangle, circle, polygon, star, and Bezier curve tools for precise shapes
  • Object manipulation and transformation — selecting, moving, rotating, scaling, skewing, and alignment tools
  • Working with paths and nodes — node editing, curve adjustment, combining shapes, and creating complex designs
  • Fill and stroke properties — solid colors, gradients, patterns, transparency, and outline customization
  • Text tools in CorelDRAW — artistic text vs paragraph text, text formatting, special effects, and text on path
  • Working with layers and objects — organizing designs, managing object hierarchy, and grouping elements
  • Color management in CorelDRAW — color palettes, spot colors, CMYK separation, and preparing for print
  • Creating logos and brand elements — logo design principles, scalability, and versatility across applications
  • Practical project: Design a professional logo for an Indian restaurant or e-commerce brand

Module 4: CorelDRAW Professional Vector Graphics — Part 2

Week 5 | 15 Hours

  • Advanced drawing techniques — combining shapes, Boolean operations (union, subtract, intersect), and creating custom shapes
  • Perspective and distortion tools — applying 3D effects, envelope distortion, and creating depth in designs
  • Special effects and filters — drop shadow, blur, glow, bevel, and artistic effects for professional polish
  • Working with symbols and libraries — creating reusable design elements, managing symbol libraries, and batch editing
  • Creating patterns and textures — custom pattern fills, texture creation, and seamless pattern design
  • Designing for print — CMYK color separation, bleed and margins, crop marks, and preparing files for commercial printing
  • Creating business collateral — letterheads, envelopes, business cards, and maintaining consistent branding
  • Practical project: Design complete stationery set (letterhead, business card, envelope) for a professional service in India
  • Exporting and file formats — saving as CDR, exporting to PDF, EPS, SVG, and preparing files for different purposes

Module 5: Real-World Design Projects & Market Applications

Week 6-7 | 30 Hours

  • Designing for Indian e-commerce — product graphics, marketplace thumbnails, promotional banners, and mobile-first design considerations
  • Social media marketing design — Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, WhatsApp Business graphics optimized for Indian audiences
  • Print design for Indian market — wedding invitations with cultural elements, festival posters, and regional language typography
  • Packaging design basics — label design, product box layouts, and considerations for Indian retail environments
  • Web graphics and UI elements — buttons, icons, headers, and designing graphics for websites and mobile apps
  • Advertising and promotional materials — banner ads, Google Ads design, print advertisements for Indian publications
  • Creating design mockups — using templates to present designs on devices, packaging, and print materials
  • Brand identity systems — creating cohesive branding guidelines, style guides, and maintaining consistency across materials
  • Design for small businesses — affordable design solutions, quick turnarounds, and scaling designs for different platforms
  • Portfolio building — selecting best work, presenting designs professionally, and creating a digital portfolio
  • Practical projects: Create 3-4 complete design projects including social media campaign, print collateral, and packaging design

Module 6: Professional Practice, Business Skills & Career Development

Week 8 | 15 Hours

  • Freelance graphic design — platforms like Fiverr, Upwork, 99designs, and Indian alternatives like Truelancer and Toptal
  • Pricing design services — understanding market rates in India, calculating project costs, and value-based pricing
  • Client management and project workflow — proposal writing, contracts, scope management, and handling revisions
  • Building a design business — registering as freelancer/entrepreneur, GST compliance in India, and invoicing
  • Time management and productivity — managing multiple projects, meeting deadlines, and using design tools efficiently
  • Staying updated with design trends — following Indian designers, design communities, and continuous learning resources
  • Design software alternatives and comparisons — Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Affinity Designer, and when to use each
  • Soft skills for designers — communication, negotiation, feedback handling, and building client relationships
  • Creating a professional portfolio — case studies, before-after presentations, testimonials, and online presence (Behance, Dribbble)
  • Job search strategies — freelance platforms, design agencies in India, corporate design roles, and startup opportunities
  • Capstone project — comprehensive design project demonstrating all learned skills

Assessment & Certification

Total Marks: 100 (Pass Percentage: 50%)

Theory (50 Marks): 25 Questions × 2 Marks (Multiple Choice & Short Answer) - 45 minutes

Practical (50 Marks): Candidates must complete 3 design projects demonstrating mastery of Canva and CorelDRAW with practical application

Practical Tasks:

  • Social Media Campaign Design (15 Marks): Create a complete social media marketing campaign (minimum 5 graphics) using Canva for an Indian brand/business including Instagram posts, Stories, and promotional graphics. Must demonstrate understanding of platform specifications, color psychology, and typography.
  • Professional Logo & Branding Design (18 Marks): Design a professional logo using CorelDRAW for an Indian business (restaurant, retail, service) with 2-3 variations and color applications. Must show vector proficiency, scalability across sizes, and application on business collateral (business card mockup).
  • Print Collateral Design Project (17 Marks): Create a complete print design project (brochure, poster, or packaging) using CorelDRAW with proper CMYK color separation, bleed marks, and print specifications. Must include typography, imagery, and professional layout following Indian design standards.

Learning Outcomes

  • Design professional graphics using Canva for quick, social media, and marketing materials with understanding of platform capabilities and limitations
  • Create vector-based designs in CorelDRAW demonstrating mastery of drawing tools, object manipulation, and professional design techniques
  • Apply design fundamentals including color theory, typography, composition, and layout principles to create visually appealing and functional designs
  • Prepare designs for both digital and print media with proper color modes (RGB/CMYK), resolution, bleed, and file format specifications
  • Build professional portfolios and establish freelance/business presence with understanding of Indian design market, pricing, and client management
  • Demonstrate cultural sensitivity and awareness of Indian design aesthetics, regional preferences, and local market requirements in design projects
