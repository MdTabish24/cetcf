Yoga Teacher — Foundation Level

📂 Sector: Sports, Fitness & Recreation 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 3 Months 🕐 Hours: 180 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Learn to teach authentic yoga and become a certified Yoga Teacher with Foundation-level expertise. This 12-week intensive program covers yoga philosophy, asanas, pranayama, and teaching methodology aligned with Indian wellness traditions and modern fitness standards. Perfect for fitness enthusiasts, career-changers, and wellness professionals in India.

Module 1: Yoga Philosophy, History & Fundamentals

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to Yoga — Definition, meaning, and significance in modern India
  • History of Yoga — Ancient origins, Vedic period, classical yoga texts (Yoga Sutras, Bhagavad Gita)
  • Eight Limbs of Yoga (Ashtanga) — Yama, Niyama, Asana, Pranayama, Pratyahara, Dharana, Dhyana, Samadhi
  • Different Yoga Schools — Hatha, Vinyasa, Ashtanga, Iyengar, Kundalini, and their characteristics
  • Yoga in Indian Culture — Role in Ayurveda, wellness traditions, and spiritual practices
  • Modern Yoga Movement in India — From ancient practice to contemporary fitness industry
  • Yoga and Health Benefits — Scientific understanding of physical, mental, and emotional benefits
  • Yoga Ethics for Teachers — Professional conduct, student safety, and responsibility

Module 2: Human Anatomy, Physiology & Body Mechanics for Yoga

📚 Topics Covered

  • Basic Human Anatomy — Skeletal system, bones, joints, and their functions in yoga
  • Muscular System — Major muscle groups, how they work during asanas, and flexibility
  • Spine and Vertebral Column — Structure, cervical, thoracic, lumbar regions, and safe alignment
  • Joint Types and Movement — Ball-and-socket, hinge joints, and their role in yoga postures
  • Respiratory System — Lungs, diaphragm, breathing mechanics, and pranayama benefits
  • Nervous System Basics — Central and peripheral nervous system, stress response, and yoga benefits
  • Cardiovascular System — Heart, blood circulation, and yoga's impact on heart health
  • Body Alignment Principles — Proper posture, weight distribution, and injury prevention in asanas
  • Common Postural Problems — Kyphosis, lordosis, scoliosis, and yoga modifications
  • Contraindications and Limitations — Understanding when students should avoid certain poses

Module 3: Asanas (Yoga Postures) — Beginner to Intermediate Level

📚 Topics Covered

  • Standing Asanas — Tadasana, Uttanasana, Virabhadrasana I & II, Trikonasana, Vrksasana, proper alignment and modifications
  • Seated Asanas — Sukhasana, Baddha Konasana, Paschimottanasana, Ardha Matsyendrasana, and benefits
  • Forward Bends — Uttanasana, Paschimottanasana, Adho Mukha Svanasana, safe progression and variations
  • Backward Bends — Bhujangasana, Urdhva Mukha Svanasana, Dhanurasana, Ustrasana, and counter-poses
  • Twists — Parivrtta Trikonasana, Ardha Matsyendrasana, Bharadvajasana, spinal health benefits
  • Balance Poses — Natarajasana, Ardha Chandrasana, Vrksasana, building focus and stability
  • Inversions — Adho Mukha Svanasana, Prasarita Padottanasana, Sirsasana preparation, and safety
  • Core Strengthening Asanas — Navasana, Plank variations, boat pose, building strength for practice
  • Hip Openers — Pigeon pose, Gomukhasana, Eka Pada Rajakapotasana prep, flexibility development
  • Restorative and Relaxation Poses — Savasana, Balasana, Supta Baddha Konasana, recovery and integration
  • Sequencing Asanas — Creating balanced sequences, warm-up, peak poses, cool-down structure
  • Modifications and Props — Using blocks, straps, bolsters, blankets for different body types and abilities

Module 4: Pranayama (Breathing Techniques) & Meditation

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to Pranayama — Prana concept, energy flow, and breathing science
  • Natural Breathing vs. Yogic Breathing — Differences, benefits, and practice methods
  • Dirga Pranayama (Three-Part Breath) — Abdominal, thoracic, clavicular breathing techniques
  • Nadi Shodhana (Alternate Nostril Breathing) — Balancing left and right energy channels, practice steps
  • Ujjayi Pranayama (Ocean Breath) — Sound breathing, calming effect, and applications
  • Kapalabhati (Skull-Shining Breath) — Cleansing breath, energizing technique, and precautions
  • Bhastrika (Bellows Breath) — Warming and energizing practice, proper timing and safety
  • Introduction to Meditation — Concept, benefits, and different meditation styles
  • Mindfulness Meditation — Body scan, present moment awareness, and stress reduction
  • Mantra Meditation — Using mantras (Om, affirmations), chanting, and mental focus
  • Guided Visualization — Creating mental imagery, relaxation techniques, and healing visualization
  • Meditation for Beginners — Posture, duration, common challenges, and progression

Module 5: Teaching Methodology, Class Management & Student Safety

📚 Topics Covered

  • Yoga Teacher Responsibilities — Duty of care, student safety, and professional standards
  • Class Structure and Planning — Warm-up, main practice, cool-down, and timing management
  • Creating Inclusive Classes — Adapting for beginners, intermediate, advanced, and mixed-level students
  • Cueing and Verbal Instruction — Clear communication, alignment cues, safety warnings, and language
  • Demonstration Skills — Safe demonstration, showing modifications, and using mirrors effectively
  • Hands-On Adjustments — When to adjust, how to touch respectfully, consent, and proper technique
  • Managing Different Student Types — Beginners, athletes, seniors, pregnant women, injured students
  • Space and Equipment Management — Studio setup, mat arrangement, ventilation, temperature, cleanliness
  • Injury Prevention and Management — Common yoga injuries, prevention strategies, when to refer to doctors
  • Building Student Confidence — Encouragement, positive feedback, celebrating progress, and motivation
  • Handling Difficult Situations — Managing disruptive students, addressing complaints, maintaining professionalism
  • Online Class Teaching — Virtual instruction, screen positioning, engagement techniques, and technical setup

Module 6: Career Development, Business Skills & Wellness Integration

📚 Topics Covered

  • Yoga Career Pathways in India — Gym instructor, wellness center teacher, corporate yoga, private practice
  • Starting Your Yoga Business — Registration, licensing, insurance, studio setup, and legal requirements
  • Marketing Your Yoga Services — Social media, local promotion, word-of-mouth, online presence, and branding
  • Pricing and Client Management — Setting class fees, package deals, managing bookings, and client retention
  • Building a Client Base — Attracting students, retention strategies, community building, and testimonials
  • Continuing Education — Advanced certifications, specializations (yoga for kids, seniors, therapy)
  • Integration with Wellness Industry — Ayurveda, nutrition, fitness, holistic health approach
  • Professional Networking — Yoga associations, community groups, workshops, and collaboration opportunities
  • Maintaining Your Own Practice — Self-care, preventing burnout, personal development, and authenticity
  • Understanding Indian Yoga Market — Growing demand, corporate wellness, school programs, and opportunities

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years (18+ preferred for professional teaching)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass
  • No prior yoga experience required; beginners welcome
  • Basic fitness level required (ability to practice asanas)
  • Already working as a yoga teacher? Apply for RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid ID proof required at registration (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Driving License)
  • Medical fitness certificate recommended (especially for those with health conditions)
  • Commitment to attend minimum 80% of classes (mandatory attendance)
  • Applicable for all Indian citizens and foreign nationals with valid visa

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the philosophy, history, and principles of yoga as an Indian wellness practice
  • Demonstrate correct performance of 20+ foundational asanas with proper alignment and breathing
  • Practice and teach 5+ pranayama techniques safely and effectively
  • Guide students through meditation and relaxation techniques
  • Design and teach balanced, safe yoga classes for beginner-level students
  • Apply basic anatomy and injury prevention knowledge to yoga teaching
  • Manage a yoga class professionally with clear communication and student safety
  • Build a sustainable yoga teaching career in India's growing wellness industry
