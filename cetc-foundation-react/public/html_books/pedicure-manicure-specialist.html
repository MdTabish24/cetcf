Module 1: Fundamentals of Nail Care & Anatomy

📚 Topics Covered

  • Nail structure and anatomy — Understanding nail plate, nail bed, cuticle, lunula, and free edge; how nails grow and factors affecting nail health
  • Nail types and conditions — Identifying healthy nails, brittle nails, weak nails, fungal infections, psoriasis, and common nail disorders in Indian climate
  • Skin anatomy of hands and feet — Structure of epidermis, dermis, and subcutaneous layers; understanding pressure points and sensitive areas
  • Common nail problems and solutions — Addressing peeling nails, discoloration, ridges, white spots, and hangnails; when to refer clients to dermatologists
  • Nail growth cycle — Anagen, catagen, and telogen phases; why nails grow faster in summer; impact of nutrition and health on nail growth
  • Contraindications and safety — Identifying when NOT to perform services; recognizing infections, open wounds, and skin conditions requiring medical attention
  • Product ingredients and chemistry — Understanding base coats, top coats, nail polish, gels, acrylics, and natural nail care products used in India
  • Health and wellness connection — How nail health reflects overall health; vitamin deficiencies and their nail symptoms; importance of hydration and nutrition

Module 2: Sanitation, Sterilization & Safety Protocols

📚 Topics Covered

  • Salon hygiene standards — Setting up clean, hygienic workstations; daily cleaning routines; maintaining salon environment as per Indian beauty salon guidelines
  • Hand hygiene and personal protective equipment — Proper handwashing techniques, wearing gloves, masks, aprons; preventing cross-contamination between clients
  • Tool sterilization methods — Autoclaving, chemical disinfection (using barbicide and sanitizing solutions); proper storage of sterilized instruments; frequency of sterilization
  • Disinfection vs sterilization — Understanding differences; which tools need sterilization vs disinfection; complying with Indian salon regulations
  • Waste management and disposal — Proper disposal of nail clippings, cotton, contaminated materials; following environmental and health guidelines
  • Client health screening — Pre-service consultation to identify contraindications; maintaining client health records; documentation practices
  • Bloodborne pathogen awareness — Understanding transmission risks; protocols if accidental cuts occur; first aid for minor injuries during service
  • Salon licensing and compliance — Understanding local beauty salon registration requirements in India; GST and business compliance; client consent forms
  • Personal health and grooming — Maintaining professional appearance; staying healthy to avoid transmitting infections; mental health awareness in high-stress salon environment
  • Emergency procedures — Basic first aid; handling allergic reactions to nail products; knowing when to call medical help; emergency contact protocols

Module 3: Manicure Services & Techniques

📚 Topics Covered

  • Manicure service types — Basic manicure, French manicure, gel manicure, acrylic nails, ombre nails, nail art designs; understanding Indian client preferences and seasonal trends
  • Tools and equipment for manicure — Nail files (metal, glass, crystal), buffers, cuticle pushers, nail clippers, dremels, UV/LED lamps; selecting right tools for different nail types
  • Step-by-step basic manicure procedure — Greeting client, hand soaking, nail shaping, cuticle care, hand massage, base coat, polish application, top coat; timing and efficiency
  • Nail shaping techniques — Understanding nail shapes (square, oval, almond, coffin, stiletto); choosing appropriate shapes for hand size and client lifestyle; using proper filing angles
  • Cuticle care and management — Pushing back vs cutting cuticles; using cuticle oil and creams; treating dry, damaged, or infected cuticles; preventing ingrown nails
  • Hand and arm massage techniques — Pressure points on hands and forearms; relaxation strokes; improving circulation; making clients feel pampered; massage duration and pressure
  • Polish application techniques — Achieving smooth, even coats; preventing bubbles and streaks; managing wet polish; drying techniques; polish removal and reapplication
  • Gel and acrylic application — Understanding UV-cured gels vs LED gels; application process; curing times; removal techniques; aftercare instructions for extended wear
  • Nail art and design — Basic designs (stripes, dots, ombre); using nail art tools and brushes; applying decals and gems; creating trending designs popular in India
  • Color selection and matching — Understanding skin undertones (warm, cool, neutral); recommending colors based on occasion, season, and client preference; trending colors in Indian market
  • Problem-solving during service — Fixing chips, managing weak nails, dealing with sensitive clients, addressing client dissatisfaction; building client loyalty
  • Aftercare and maintenance advice — How long manicure lasts; tips to prevent chipping; product recommendations; scheduling follow-up appointments; upselling services

Module 4: Pedicure Services & Techniques

📚 Topics Covered

  • Pedicure service types — Basic pedicure, medical pedicure, gel pedicure, spa pedicure, foot reflexology pedicure; understanding Indian climate impact on foot care
  • Foot and leg anatomy — Foot structure, arches, pressure points, reflexology zones; common foot problems in hot Indian climate (cracking, fungal infections, calluses)
  • Tools and equipment for pedicure — Foot files, pumice stones, foot scrapers, toe separators, pedicure tubs, foot spa machines; selecting appropriate tools for different foot conditions
  • Foot soaking and softening — Choosing water temperature; using foot soaks with salts, essential oils, and herbs; softening calluses and dead skin; duration and benefits
  • Callus and dead skin removal — Using foot files and scrapers safely; avoiding over-removal; treating diabetic feet with care; managing thick, hardened skin
  • Toenail care procedures — Cutting toenails straight across to prevent ingrown nails; filing edges; managing thick or fungal nails; proper nail length and shape
  • Foot massage techniques — Pressure point massage, reflexology basics; calf massage; improving circulation; relaxation strokes; managing client comfort on pedicure chairs
  • Exfoliation and skin treatment — Using foot masks, scrubs, and peels; treating dry, cracked heels; managing fungal issues; recommending home care products
  • Polish application on toenails — Applying base coat, polish, and top coat to toenails; managing larger nail surface area; preventing smudging during drying
  • Pedicure chair operation — Setting up pedicure tub; adjusting temperature and jets; maintaining cleanliness between clients; troubleshooting equipment issues
  • Foot health conditions — Identifying athlete's foot, nail fungus, bunions, corns, warts; knowing when to refer to podiatrist; contraindications for pedicure service
  • Aftercare and foot care advice — Keeping feet dry and clean; using foot powders; wearing appropriate footwear; scheduling regular pedicures; preventing fungal infections in monsoon season
