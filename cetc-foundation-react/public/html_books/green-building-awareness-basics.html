Green Building Awareness Basics

📂 Sector: Environmental & Green Skills 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months 🕐 Hours: 120 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1500
Learn the fundamentals of green building practices and sustainable construction in India. This foundation-level course equips you with essential knowledge about eco-friendly building materials, energy efficiency, water conservation, and waste management in construction. Perfect for aspiring construction professionals, site supervisors, and environmental enthusiasts looking to enter the growing green building sector.

Module 1: Introduction to Green Buildings and Sustainability Concepts

📚 Topics Covered

  • What is a Green Building — definition, principles, and global context in India
  • Sustainability in Construction — environmental, social, and economic pillars
  • Climate Change and Building Sector — carbon footprint and emissions reduction
  • Indian Green Building Movement — history, growth, and current trends
  • Benefits of Green Buildings — cost savings, health benefits, and environmental impact
  • Green vs Traditional Buildings — comparative analysis with Indian examples
  • Stakeholders in Green Building — architects, contractors, developers, and end-users
  • Government Policies and Incentives — subsidies and tax benefits for green construction in India
  • Role of Certification Bodies — IGBC, GRIHA, and their importance
  • Career Opportunities in Green Building Sector — emerging job roles in India

Module 2: Green Building Rating Systems and Certifications

📚 Topics Covered

  • LEED Certification System — overview, rating levels, and application in India
  • GRIHA Rating System — Indian Green Rating for Integrated Habitat Assessment
  • Indian Green Building Council (IGBC) Standards — national benchmarks and requirements
  • BREEAM and Other International Standards — comparative understanding
  • Certification Process — documentation, assessment, and compliance procedures
  • Rating Criteria and Points System — how buildings earn green certification
  • Case Studies of Certified Buildings in India — Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi examples
  • Cost-Benefit Analysis of Certification — investment vs long-term savings
  • Maintenance of Green Certification — post-construction compliance and monitoring
  • Digital Tools for Certification — software and platforms used in Indian projects
  • Challenges in Indian Green Certification — local barriers and solutions
  • Future of Green Ratings in India — emerging standards and improvements

Module 3: Sustainable Materials and Resource Management

📚 Topics Covered

  • Eco-Friendly Building Materials — bamboo, recycled steel, and local materials
  • Locally Sourced Materials in India — reducing transportation emissions
  • Recycled and Reclaimed Materials — sourcing and applications in construction
  • Low-VOC Paints and Finishes — indoor air quality and health benefits
  • Sustainable Cement and Concrete — fly ash, slag, and green alternatives
  • Green Insulation Materials — natural fibers, recycled content options
  • Sustainable Flooring Options — bamboo, cork, recycled tiles in Indian context
  • Material Certification and Standards — understanding eco-labels and certifications
  • Waste Reduction in Material Selection — minimizing construction waste
  • Cost Comparison of Green Materials — pricing trends in Indian market
  • Local Suppliers and Green Material Networks — finding sustainable sources
  • Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) — evaluating material environmental impact

Module 4: Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy Systems

📚 Topics Covered

  • Building Energy Consumption — understanding loads and inefficiencies
  • Energy Audit Basics — identifying energy-saving opportunities
  • HVAC Systems — efficient heating, ventilation, and air conditioning design
  • LED Lighting and Smart Controls — reducing electricity consumption
  • Building Envelope Efficiency — insulation, windows, and thermal performance
  • Solar Energy Systems in India — PV panels, solar water heating, and installation
  • Wind Energy Integration — small-scale wind systems for buildings
  • Energy Storage Solutions — batteries and thermal storage technologies
  • Smart Building Technologies — IoT sensors and energy management systems
  • Passive Design Strategies — natural ventilation and daylighting in Indian climate
  • Energy Performance Standards — BEE ratings and compliance in India
  • Cost Analysis of Energy Systems — ROI and payback periods for renewable installations

Module 5: Water Management and Indoor Environmental Quality

📚 Topics Covered

  • Water Conservation Strategies — reducing consumption in buildings
  • Rainwater Harvesting Systems — design, installation, and maintenance in India
  • Greywater Recycling — treatment and reuse in buildings
  • Low-Flow Fixtures and Smart Metering — water-efficient plumbing
  • Wastewater Treatment Systems — on-site treatment solutions
  • Stormwater Management — permeable pavements and retention ponds
  • Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) Standards — ventilation and pollution control
  • Thermal Comfort and Acoustics — creating healthy indoor environments
  • Natural Lighting and Daylighting Design — reducing artificial light dependency
  • Moisture Control and Mold Prevention — building science basics
  • Indoor Environmental Monitoring — sensors and quality assessment
  • Health and Wellness Standards — WELL Building Standard and Indian adaptations

Module 6: Green Building Construction Practices and Waste Management

📚 Topics Covered

  • Green Construction Site Management — planning and organization
  • Construction Waste Segregation — categories and disposal methods in India
  • Dust and Pollution Control — measures during construction
  • Safety and Health on Green Sites — worker protection and standards
  • Supply Chain Management — sustainable procurement practices
  • Green Laborer Training — upskilling construction workers for green projects
  • Quality Assurance in Green Construction — testing and verification procedures
  • Construction Sequencing for Minimal Impact — planning and scheduling
  • Biodiversity Protection During Construction — environmental safeguards
  • Commissioning and Handover — ensuring systems perform as designed
  • Operations and Maintenance Manuals — training building managers
  • Monitoring and Reporting — post-construction performance tracking and documentation

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior experience required
  • Open to construction workers, site supervisors, engineers, and environmental professionals
  • Currently employed? Apply for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) exam for direct certification
  • Valid government ID proof required at registration (Aadhaar, PAN, or Voter ID)
  • Applicable for all Indian citizens and registered foreign nationals
  • Mobile number and email required for communication and certification

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Understand fundamental principles of green buildings and sustainability in construction
  • Identify and explain major green building rating systems (LEED, GRIHA, IGBC) used in India
  • Select and specify eco-friendly building materials with knowledge of environmental impact
  • Design and evaluate energy-efficient systems including renewable energy integration
  • Plan and implement water conservation and management strategies in buildings
  • Apply green construction practices and waste management protocols on building sites
