Social Media Influencer Marketing

📂 Sector: Media, Photography & Content Creation 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months 🕐 Hours: 120 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1500
Master the art and science of social media influence in India's fastest-growing digital economy. This foundation course trains you to build personal brands, create viral content, and monetize your social media presence across Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, and emerging platforms.

Module 1: Fundamentals of Social Media & Influencer Ecosystem

📚 Topics Covered

  • Social Media Landscape in India — Understanding Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn, and emerging platforms like Koo and ShareChat
  • What is an Influencer? — Definition, types (Nano, Micro, Macro, Mega influencers), and tier-based earning models in Indian context
  • Influencer Marketing vs Traditional Advertising — Cost-benefit analysis, ROI comparison, why brands prefer influencers over TV ads in 2024
  • The Creator Economy in India — Growth statistics, market size, opportunities, and future trends from 2024-2026
  • Platform Algorithms Explained — How Instagram algorithm works, YouTube recommendation system, TikTok FYP, and content visibility factors
  • Audience Psychology & Consumer Behavior — Why people follow influencers, trust factors, engagement psychology, and decision-making triggers
  • Legal & Ethical Framework — FTC guidelines, ASCI code for influencers, disclosure rules, fake followers, and compliance in India
  • Influencer vs Creator vs Content Creator — Key differences, career paths, and income models for each role

Module 2: Personal Branding & Audience Building

📚 Topics Covered

  • Finding Your Niche — Identifying passions, market demand, competition analysis, and choosing profitable niches (fitness, finance, beauty, tech, food, travel in India)
  • Building Your Personal Brand — Creating brand identity, values, unique selling proposition (USP), and brand voice that resonates with Indian audiences
  • Profile Optimization for All Platforms — Bio writing, profile picture selection, cover photo design, link-in-bio strategies, and CTAs (Call-to-Action)
  • Content Pillars & Content Calendar — Planning 30-60-90 day strategies, content themes, posting schedules, and seasonal content planning
  • Organic Growth Strategies — Hashtag research, community engagement, collaborations, cross-platform promotion, and growth hacking techniques
  • Paid Growth vs Organic Growth — When to use ads, Facebook Ads Manager, Instagram promotion, budget allocation, and ROI tracking
  • Audience Analytics & Insights — Understanding follower demographics, engagement rates, best posting times, and audience behavior patterns
  • Building Genuine Community — Creating engaged followers vs vanity metrics, fostering loyalty, responding to comments, and creating fan communities
  • Competitor Analysis — Tracking competitor strategies, benchmarking performance, identifying market gaps, and staying ahead in your niche

Module 3: Content Creation & Visual Storytelling

📚 Topics Covered

  • Photography Fundamentals for Social Media — Composition, lighting, framing, smartphone photography tips, and how to shoot content that performs well
  • Video Content Creation — Planning videos, scripting, filming techniques, editing basics, and optimizing videos for different platforms
  • Content Formats That Drive Engagement — Reels, Shorts, Stories, Carousel posts, Live sessions, IGTV, and platform-specific content strategies
  • Storytelling & Narrative Techniques — Creating emotional connections, hero's journey framework, behind-the-scenes content, and authentic storytelling
  • Visual Design & Aesthetics — Color theory, typography, grid layouts, consistent visual branding, and using tools like Canva, Adobe Express
  • Trending Content & Viral Mechanics — Understanding trends, trending sounds, challenges, memes, and how to create viral-worthy content
  • Tools & Software for Content Creation — Smartphone apps (CapCut, InShot, Snapseed), desktop tools (Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro), and free alternatives
  • Audio & Music for Content — Copyright-free music sources, royalty-free platforms, sound effects, podcast integration, and audio branding
  • Content Calendars & Batch Creation — Planning content, filming in batches, scheduling posts, maintaining consistency, and managing multiple platforms
  • Quality vs Quantity — Understanding the balance, posting frequency, engagement rates, and how often to post on each platform
  • Captions & Copywriting for Social Media — Writing compelling captions, CTAs, emojis usage, hashtag integration, and copywriting psychology

Module 4: Monetization Strategies & Brand Partnerships

📚 Topics Covered

  • Monetization Models — Ad revenue sharing, sponsored content, affiliate marketing, selling digital products, and subscription-based income
  • YouTube Partner Program — Eligibility criteria (1000 subscribers, 4000 watch hours), AdSense setup, CPM rates in India (₹50-500 per 1000 views), and revenue optimization
  • Instagram Monetization — Reels bonus program, brand collabs, Instagram Shopping, IGTV ads, and direct creator payments
  • TikTok Creator Fund & Rewards — Eligibility, earnings per view, bonus programs, and growth strategies for TikTok monetization
  • Sponsored Content & Brand Deals — Types of partnerships, negotiating rates, contracts, deliverables, and working with brand managers
  • Influencer Marketing Platforms — Working with Billo, Qoraa, Klear, AspireIQ, and Indian platforms connecting creators with brands
  • Affiliate Marketing — Commission structures, affiliate networks (Amazon Associates, Flipkart Affiliate), promoting products authentically, and tracking links
  • Digital Products & Online Courses — Creating and selling e-books, courses, presets, templates, and digital downloads to your audience
  • Sponsorship Packages — Creating tier-based offerings (starter, professional, premium), pricing strategies, and packaging services
  • Contract Negotiation & Rates — Understanding market rates, negotiating with brands, calculating fair pricing, and protecting yourself legally
  • Building Long-term Brand Relationships — Repeat collaborations, ambassador programs, and creating win-win partnerships with brands

Module 5: Analytics, Metrics & Performance Optimization

📚 Topics Covered

  • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) — Impressions, reach, engagement rate, click-through rate (CTR), conversion rate, and ROI calculation
  • Platform Analytics Tools — Instagram Insights, YouTube Analytics, TikTok Analytics, Twitter Analytics, and interpreting data dashboards
  • Engagement Rate Calculation — Formula, benchmarking against industry standards, and improving engagement metrics
  • Audience Growth Metrics — Follower growth rate, retention rate, churn rate, and analyzing growth patterns over time
  • Content Performance Analysis — Identifying top-performing content, analyzing what works, A/B testing, and optimizing based on data
  • Conversion Tracking — Setting up UTM parameters, link tracking, using tools like Bitly, Google Analytics, and measuring actual sales from content
  • Attribution Models — Understanding how content leads to conversions, multi-touch attribution, and crediting the right content pieces
  • Reporting & Dashboards — Creating monthly performance reports, presenting data to brands, and using tools like Google Data Studio
  • Competitor Benchmarking — Comparing metrics with competitors, identifying strengths and weaknesses, and setting realistic targets
  • Optimization Strategies — Testing posting times, content formats, hashtags, captions, and continuously improving based on data insights
  • Tools for Analytics — Third-party tools like Social Blade, Later, Buffer, Hootsuite, and native platform analytics interpretation

Module 6: Advanced Strategy, Scaling & Business Management

📚 Topics Covered

  • Building a Personal Brand Business — Registering as self-employed, GST registration, taxation for creators, and financial planning
  • Team Building & Delegation — Hiring editors, managers, virtual assistants, and scaling your content production
  • Multi-Platform Strategy — Repurposing content, cross-promotion, platform diversification, and managing presence on 5+ platforms
  • Crisis Management & Reputation — Handling negative comments, managing PR issues, apologies, and protecting your personal brand
  • Collaboration Strategies — Working with other creators, duets, joint projects, shoutouts, and network building
  • Niche Expansion & Diversification — Introducing new content types, entering new markets, and keeping content fresh without losing core audience
  • Influencer Agencies & Management — Understanding agency models, signing with agencies vs staying independent, and negotiating agency contracts
  • International Growth & Global Audience — Expanding beyond India, understanding different markets, localization, and global monetization
  • Emerging Platforms & Future Trends — Staying updated with new platforms, Web3 and NFTs for creators, metaverse opportunities, and AI in content creation
  • Content Sustainability & Burnout Prevention — Maintaining consistency long-term, avoiding creator burnout, taking breaks, and work-life balance
  • Building Passive Income Streams — Creating evergreen content, selling digital products, licensing content, and building sustainable income
  • Advanced Negotiation & Business Skills — Contract review, rate negotiation, managing multiple brands, and building a sustainable influencer business

  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior experience needed - course is designed for beginners
  • Already working as a content creator? Apply for Direct RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid photo ID proof required (Aadhaar, PAN, School ID, Passport) at registration
  • Basic smartphone or computer required for content creation and course access
  • Stable internet connection required for online learning and practical submissions
  • Applicable for all candidates - no age, gender, or background restrictions
