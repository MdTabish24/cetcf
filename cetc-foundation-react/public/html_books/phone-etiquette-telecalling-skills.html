Phone Etiquette & Telecalling Skills

📂 Sector: Spoken Languages & Communication 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 1 Month 🕐 Hours: 60 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the art of professional phone communication and telecalling with this intensive 4-week foundation course. Learn proven techniques to handle customer calls, manage objections, and build rapport over the phone—essential skills for success in India's growing BPO, customer service, and sales sectors.

Module 1: Fundamentals of Phone Communication & Professional Etiquette

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to phone communication — importance in modern business, role in customer service and sales
  • Professional telephone etiquette basics — greeting standards, tone of voice, pace and clarity requirements
  • Understanding the psychology of voice — how tone conveys emotion, confidence, and professionalism over phone
  • Active listening skills — techniques to listen effectively without visual cues, note-taking during calls
  • Proper phone handling procedures — holding phone correctly, microphone positioning, background noise management
  • Professional language standards — avoiding slang, using appropriate vocabulary, maintaining formality levels
  • Cultural sensitivity in Indian context — understanding regional preferences, respecting local customs in communication
  • First impression over phone — creating positive impact within first 5 seconds, setting call tone
  • Personal grooming and workspace setup — how physical environment affects voice quality and confidence
  • Recording and documentation basics — understanding call recording policies, confidentiality, and data protection

Module 2: Inbound Call Handling & Customer Service Excellence

📚 Topics Covered

  • Inbound call reception techniques — answering protocols, company name pronunciation, self-introduction standards
  • Call routing and transfer procedures — identifying caller needs, transferring to correct department, maintaining professionalism during transfer
  • Handling customer inquiries — understanding customer pain points, asking clarifying questions, providing accurate information
  • Managing difficult and angry customers — de-escalation techniques, empathy statements, converting complaints to opportunities
  • Complaint resolution strategies — documenting issues, offering solutions, following up appropriately
  • Building customer rapport — finding common ground, showing genuine interest, creating positive relationships
  • Handling confused or uncertain customers — patience techniques, breaking down complex information, confirming understanding
  • Call documentation and CRM basics — entering call details accurately, following company protocols, maintaining records
  • Multi-tasking during calls — managing computer systems, accessing information, providing quick solutions
  • Closing inbound calls professionally — summarizing action items, confirming next steps, warm goodbye

Module 3: Outbound Telecalling Techniques & Sales Skills

📚 Topics Covered

  • Telecalling fundamentals — understanding outbound call objectives, target audience identification, call planning
  • Prospecting and lead qualification — identifying potential customers, assessing buying capability, prioritizing leads
  • Cold calling techniques — overcoming rejection, opening statements that work, building initial interest
  • Sales pitch development — structuring your message, highlighting benefits over features, adapting to customer type
  • Objection handling framework — anticipating common objections, responding confidently, turning objections into closing opportunities
  • Questioning techniques — open-ended vs closed questions, probing deeper, understanding customer needs
  • Building urgency and interest — creating value perception, using scarcity principles, maintaining momentum
  • Closing techniques — recognizing buying signals, asking for commitment, handling final objections
  • Follow-up strategies — scheduling callbacks, maintaining contact lists, nurturing leads over time
  • Telecalling ethics and compliance — understanding TRAI regulations, Do Not Call (DNC) registry, honest representation

Module 4: Communication Skills & Language Mastery for Phone

📚 Topics Covered

  • English pronunciation for non-native speakers — common Indian English challenges, phonetic practice, accent neutralization basics
  • Hinglish communication — effective code-switching, when to use Hindi vs English, maintaining professionalism in mixed language calls
  • Vocabulary building for telecalling — industry-specific terms, product-related words, professional phrases
  • Grammar and sentence structure — using correct tenses, subject-verb agreement, avoiding common mistakes in real-time speech
  • Confidence building in English — overcoming anxiety, practicing regularly, managing nervousness during calls
  • Pace and intonation — speaking at appropriate speed, using emphasis effectively, varying tone to maintain interest
  • Silence and pauses — using strategic pauses, allowing customer to respond, managing awkward silences
  • Non-verbal communication over phone — conveying enthusiasm through voice, showing attention, building trust through tone

Module 5: Handling Complex Scenarios & Emotional Intelligence

📚 Topics Covered

  • Emotional intelligence fundamentals — understanding emotions, managing stress, reading customer emotions through voice
  • Stress management during calls — breathing techniques, pressure handling, maintaining composure under criticism
  • Conflict resolution strategies — identifying conflict early, using neutral language, finding win-win solutions
  • Handling aggressive customers — maintaining calm, using validation techniques, knowing when to escalate
  • Dealing with language barriers — simplifying language, using visual aids (if video call), confirming understanding
  • Managing multiple calls and interruptions — context switching, maintaining quality, preventing call fatigue
  • Privacy and confidentiality concerns — handling sensitive information, reassuring customers, following data protection
  • Technical troubleshooting during calls — managing poor connections, using alternative communication, maintaining professionalism
  • Handling competitor inquiries — responding diplomatically, highlighting own strengths, maintaining company reputation
  • Personal boundaries and time management — managing work stress, avoiding burnout, maintaining work-life balance

Module 6: Advanced Telecalling & Professional Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Advanced sales techniques — consultative selling, solution-based approach, understanding customer business needs
  • Building long-term customer relationships — loyalty building, repeat business development, customer retention strategies
  • Upselling and cross-selling — identifying additional needs, presenting complementary products, maximizing customer value
  • Call analytics and performance metrics — understanding KPIs, tracking own performance, improving conversion rates
  • Using call scripts effectively — when to use scripts, personalizing scripts, maintaining authenticity while using templates
  • Industry-specific communication — understanding different sectors (BFSI, e-commerce, telecom, insurance), adapting approach
  • International calling etiquette — understanding time zones, cultural differences, appropriate greeting styles for different countries
  • Building personal brand as telecaller — developing reputation, networking in industry, creating career advancement opportunities
  • Technology tools for telecalling — using CRM systems, dialer software, email integration, productivity tools
  • Continuous improvement and feedback — receiving constructive criticism, self-assessment, ongoing skill development, certification paths
  • Interview preparation for telecalling roles — presenting yourself professionally, discussing experience, handling salary negotiations
  • Entrepreneurship in communication services — starting own consultancy, freelance telecalling, online coaching opportunities

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years (or as per local education board norms)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior experience required
  • Basic English or Hindi speaking ability required
  • Comfortable with telephone and computer systems
  • Already working in telecalling? Apply for Direct RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid ID proof required at registration (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID, or Passport)
  • Mobile phone or landline access required for practice and assessment
  • Applicable for all Indian citizens and registered foreign nationals
  • No upper age limit - career changers welcome

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Demonstrate professional phone etiquette and communication standards in both inbound and outbound call scenarios
  • Handle customer inquiries, complaints, and objections confidently using proven de-escalation and resolution techniques
  • Conduct effective cold calls and telecalling with proper prospecting, pitch delivery, and closing techniques
  • Communicate clearly in English with proper pronunciation, grammar, and appropriate pace and intonation
  • Build rapport and emotional connections with customers over the phone through active listening and empathy
  • Maintain professionalism and confidentiality while managing complex customer interactions and stress situations
  • Use CRM systems and call documentation tools to track customer interactions and improve performance metrics
  • Apply sales and upselling techniques to maximize customer value and business opportunities
