Business Communication Skills

📂 Sector: Spoken Languages & Communication 📊 Level: Intermediate ⏱️ Duration: 3 Months 🕐 Hours: 180 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master professional communication in English and Hindi for the modern Indian workplace. This course teaches you how to speak confidently in meetings, write professional emails, handle client calls, and present ideas effectively—skills that every business professional needs to succeed.

Module 1: Foundations of Business Communication

📚 Topics Covered

  • Communication basics and importance in business — understanding why clear communication matters for career growth
  • Verbal and non-verbal communication — body language, eye contact, facial expressions, and gestures in professional settings
  • Listening skills for business — active listening techniques to understand clients and colleagues better
  • Communication barriers and how to overcome them — dealing with accents, noise, cultural differences in Indian workplaces
  • Building confidence in speaking — overcoming nervousness and fear of public speaking
  • Professional attitude and etiquette — dress code, punctuality, respect, and workplace behavior
  • Self-introduction and personal branding — creating a strong first impression in interviews and meetings
  • Understanding audience and context — tailoring your communication for different situations and people

Module 2: Professional English Speaking

📚 Topics Covered

  • Pronunciation and accent improvement — correct pronunciation of common business words and Indian English variations
  • Business vocabulary building — essential words and phrases used in offices, meetings, and client interactions
  • Formal vs informal English — knowing when to use formal language and when casual speech is appropriate
  • Speaking in meetings and discussions — contributing ideas, disagreeing politely, and asking questions professionally
  • Telephone etiquette and skills — answering calls professionally, taking messages, and handling difficult callers
  • Small talk and networking — starting conversations at business events and building professional relationships
  • Handling difficult conversations — managing complaints, conflicts, and sensitive topics professionally
  • Presentation skills basics — structuring talks, using voice effectively, and managing presentation anxiety
  • Grammar essentials for business — common mistakes and how to correct them in spoken English

Module 3: Professional Writing and Email Communication

📚 Topics Covered

  • Business email writing — structure, tone, and formatting of professional emails
  • Email etiquette and dos-and-donts — subject lines, greetings, closings, and reply protocols
  • Writing professional letters — formal letters for complaints, requests, and business proposals
  • Memo and report writing — creating clear internal communications and business documents
  • Writing for clarity and brevity — avoiding jargon, keeping sentences short, and organizing ideas logically
  • Proofreading and editing skills — catching errors and improving quality before sending documents
  • Professional tone and language — maintaining respect and professionalism in written communication
  • Writing persuasive messages — convincing clients and managers through effective written communication
  • Digital communication etiquette — WhatsApp, SMS, and chat protocols in business contexts
  • Handling written complaints and feedback — responding professionally to criticism and negative feedback

Module 4: Presentations and Public Speaking

📚 Topics Covered

  • Presentation planning and preparation — choosing topics, researching, and organizing content
  • Creating effective visual aids — using slides, charts, and graphics to support your message
  • Presentation structure — introduction, body, conclusion, and transitions that keep audience engaged
  • Delivery techniques — pace, volume, pauses, and emphasis for maximum impact
  • Handling nervousness and stage fright — breathing techniques, practice methods, and confidence building
  • Audience engagement — asking questions, encouraging participation, and reading audience reactions
  • Handling Q&A sessions — answering questions confidently, handling difficult questions, and managing time
  • Using technology effectively — PowerPoint, video, and online presentation tools in business
  • Presenting data and numbers — explaining statistics, charts, and financial information clearly
  • Cultural sensitivity in presentations — respecting diverse audiences in Indian business environment

Module 5: Interpersonal and Client Communication

📚 Topics Covered

  • Building professional relationships — networking, maintaining connections, and showing genuine interest
  • Client communication and customer service — understanding client needs and providing excellent service
  • Negotiation skills — discussing terms, finding common ground, and reaching agreements
  • Conflict resolution and difficult conversations — managing disagreements professionally and finding solutions
  • Feedback and performance discussions — giving and receiving feedback constructively
  • Cross-cultural communication — working with diverse teams and respecting cultural differences
  • Emotional intelligence in communication — understanding emotions and responding appropriately
  • Building trust and credibility — being honest, reliable, and professional in all interactions
  • Remote and virtual communication — video calls, online meetings, and digital professionalism
  • Gender sensitivity and inclusive communication — respectful language and avoiding stereotypes

Module 6: Practical Application and Interview Skills

📚 Topics Covered

  • Job interview preparation — researching companies, preparing answers, and practicing responses
  • Interview communication strategies — making eye contact, speaking clearly, and managing anxiety
  • Answering common interview questions — questions about strengths, weaknesses, experience, and goals
  • Behavioral interview techniques — using STAR method to answer situation-based questions
  • Handling difficult interview questions — addressing gaps, failures, and sensitive topics professionally
  • Group discussion and GD skills — participating actively, listening, and contributing meaningfully
  • Salary negotiation communication — discussing compensation professionally and confidently
  • Post-interview follow-up — thank you emails and maintaining professional contact
  • Industry-specific communication — understanding terminology and communication styles in different sectors
  • Continuous improvement — getting feedback, practicing regularly, and developing communication skills over time

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass (or equivalent)
  • No prior experience needed—beginners welcome
  • Already working in communication field? Apply for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) exam
  • Valid ID proof required at registration (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Driving License)
  • Applicable for all candidates across India
  • English language proficiency: Basic understanding of English helpful but not mandatory

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Speak confidently and clearly in professional English with improved pronunciation and vocabulary
  • Write professional emails, letters, and business documents following proper format and etiquette
  • Present ideas effectively using visual aids and handling audience questions with confidence
  • Communicate professionally with clients, colleagues, and managers in various business scenarios
  • Handle difficult conversations, conflicts, and feedback with emotional intelligence and professionalism
  • Succeed in job interviews by answering questions effectively and making strong first impressions
