Paper Craft & Gift Wrapping

📂 Sector: Handicrafts & Creative Arts 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 1 Month 🕐 Hours: 60 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the art of creating beautiful paper crafts and professional gift wrapping designs that turn ordinary moments into memorable experiences. This foundation course teaches you traditional and modern techniques to create handmade gifts, decorative items, and custom wrapping solutions for retail and personal use.

Module 1: Paper Craft Fundamentals & Material Science

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to Paper Craft — History, types, and applications in India
  • Paper types and quality — Tissue, cardstock, handmade, recycled, specialty papers
  • Tools and equipment — Scissors, cutters, rulers, scoring tools, adhesives, embossing machines
  • Safety protocols — Proper handling, storage, and workspace organization
  • Paper selection for different projects — Weight, texture, finish, and color matching
  • Adhesive selection and application — Glue types, tape, paste, and bonding techniques
  • Basic paper folding techniques — Origami basics, accordion folds, valley and mountain folds
  • Color theory and design principles — Complementary colors, balance, contrast in paper crafts
  • Recycling and sustainable paper craft — Upcycling materials, eco-friendly practices
  • Cost calculation and material sourcing — Finding suppliers, bulk buying, pricing materials

Module 2: Greeting Cards & Decorative Paper Items

📚 Topics Covered

  • Greeting card basics — Dimensions, card stock selection, folding techniques
  • Birthday card designs — Age-specific themes, number designs, personalization methods
  • Festival cards — Diwali, Holi, Christmas, Eid, and regional festival-themed cards
  • Occasion cards — Wedding, engagement, baby shower, anniversary, congratulations cards
  • Handmade envelope creation — Sizing, folding, decorating, sealing techniques
  • Layering and dimensional cards — Creating depth, pop-up elements, 3D effects
  • Stamping and embossing — Ink pads, rubber stamps, heat embossing techniques
  • Embellishment techniques — Ribbons, beads, sequins, glitter, dry flowers, metallic accents
  • Calligraphy and hand lettering — Basic strokes, greeting messages, personalized text
  • Batch production methods — Creating multiple cards efficiently for retail sales
  • Packaging and presentation — Gift wrapping cards, adding protective sleeves

Module 3: Paper Flowers & Decorative Craft Items

📚 Topics Covered

  • Paper flower types — Roses, tulips, dahlias, chrysanthemums, peonies, sunflowers
  • Flower-making techniques — Petal cutting, curling, layering, assembly methods
  • Stem and leaf creation — Wire wrapping, paper stem rolling, leaf shaping
  • Bouquet arrangement — Color combinations, height variation, filler flowers
  • Decorative boxes and gift containers — Folding templates, box assembly, lid designs
  • Paper lanterns and hanging decorations — Festival lanterns, ceiling hangings, wall art
  • Paper quilling — Coiling, shaping, gluing, creating patterns and designs
  • Scrapbooking basics — Page layouts, photo arrangement, embellishment placement
  • Paper mache and pulp crafts — Mixing, molding, drying, painting techniques
  • Seasonal decorations — Wall hangings, table centerpieces, window displays
  • Custom gift boxes — Measuring, cutting, assembling, interior lining

Module 4: Professional Gift Wrapping Techniques

📚 Topics Covered

  • Wrapping paper selection — Thickness, pattern, texture, seasonal themes
  • Basic wrapping method — Measuring paper, centering gift, folding, taping technique
  • Advanced wrapping — Irregular shapes, fragile items, luxury presentation
  • Ribbon and bow techniques — Tying bows, ribbon curling, multi-ribbon combinations
  • Themed wrapping designs — Birthday, wedding, corporate, festival-specific wrapping
  • Luxury gift wrapping — Kraft paper, tissue paper layering, premium finishes
  • Corporate gifting standards — Branding, logo placement, professional presentation
  • Gift bag assembly and decoration — Sizing, tissue paper arrangement, handle design
  • Eco-friendly wrapping alternatives — Fabric wraps, newspaper, recycled materials
  • Speed wrapping for retail — Efficient techniques, time management, quality consistency
  • Wrapping specialty items — Bottles, jewelry boxes, irregular shapes, multiple items

Module 5: Design, Branding & Business Skills

📚 Topics Covered

  • Design principles for paper crafts — Layout, typography, visual hierarchy, white space
  • Color psychology in gifting — Cultural preferences in India, occasion-specific colors
  • Trend analysis and seasonal planning — Festival calendars, market demands, trend forecasting
  • Branding your craft products — Logo design, packaging labels, business identity
  • Photography and product display — Lighting, angles, background, online presentation
  • Pricing strategy — Cost analysis, profit margins, competitive pricing in Indian market
  • Customer preferences and feedback — Understanding market needs, customization requests
  • Quality control standards — Consistency, durability, presentation quality checks
  • Inventory management — Stock tracking, material storage, waste reduction
  • Marketing basics — Social media content, local promotion, word-of-mouth strategies

Module 6: Entrepreneurship & Income Generation

📚 Topics Covered

  • Business model options — Retail shop, online store, home-based business, event services
  • Registration and legal requirements — GST registration, business license, tax compliance
  • Startup investment and funding — Initial capital calculation, cost breakdown, funding sources
  • Online selling platforms — Flipkart, Amazon, Etsy, Instagram, personal website setup
  • Wholesale and bulk orders — Supplier agreements, minimum order quantities, pricing
  • Event and party services — Wedding decoration, corporate events, festival celebrations
  • Corporate gifting contracts — B2B sales, bulk orders, customization for companies
  • Social media marketing — Instagram reels, Pinterest, Facebook business pages, hashtag strategy
  • Customer service and retention — Communication, delivery, returns, building loyalty
  • Scaling production — Hiring assistance, outsourcing, maintaining quality at volume

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass (or equivalent)
  • No prior experience required — open to all candidates
  • Already working in paper craft? Apply for direct RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid ID proof required — Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Driving License
  • Applicable for all candidates including homemakers, students, working professionals, and entrepreneurs
  • No language barrier — course available in Hindi and English

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Demonstrate proficiency in selecting appropriate paper types and materials for different craft projects
  • Create professional-quality greeting cards, paper flowers, and decorative items using traditional and modern techniques
  • Apply advanced gift wrapping methods for various shapes, sizes, and occasions with luxury presentation standards
  • Design custom paper craft products aligned with market trends, cultural preferences, and customer requirements
  • Establish and operate a paper craft business including pricing, marketing, and customer management
  • Produce handmade crafts at commercial scale while maintaining quality consistency and sustainability practices
