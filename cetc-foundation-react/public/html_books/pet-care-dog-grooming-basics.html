Pet Care & Dog Grooming Basics

📂 Sector: Child Care & Domestic Services 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 1 Month 🕐 Hours: 60 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the essentials of pet care and dog grooming with this foundation-level certification course designed for aspiring pet care professionals in India. Learn practical skills in dog handling, hygiene, grooming techniques, and pet health management to launch a rewarding career in the growing pet care industry.

Module 1: Introduction to Pet Care & Dog Grooming Industry

📚 Topics Covered

  • Pet care industry overview in India — growth, opportunities, and market trends in urban and rural areas
  • Types of dog grooming services — basic grooming, show grooming, therapeutic grooming, and specialized services
  • Career opportunities in pet care sector — salons, kennels, veterinary clinics, home-based services, and entrepreneurship
  • Professional standards and ethics in pet grooming — animal welfare, client confidentiality, and workplace safety
  • Understanding different dog breeds — characteristics, temperament, coat types, and grooming requirements for Indian breeds
  • Health and hygiene regulations — FSSAI guidelines for pet care facilities, sanitation standards, and disease prevention
  • Business basics for pet care professionals — pricing strategies, customer service, and building a client base in India
  • Introduction to grooming tools and equipment — types, uses, maintenance, and cost-benefit analysis

Module 2: Dog Anatomy, Health & Behavior Fundamentals

📚 Topics Covered

  • Basic dog anatomy — skeletal structure, muscle groups, skin layers, and coat composition relevant to grooming
  • Common health issues in dogs — skin conditions, ear infections, dental problems, and parasite-related issues in Indian climate
  • Recognizing signs of illness — symptoms, behavioral changes, and when to refer clients to veterinarians
  • Dog behavior and psychology — understanding stress signals, body language, aggression, and anxiety during grooming
  • Handling techniques for different temperaments — calm dogs, anxious dogs, aggressive dogs, and puppies safely
  • Age-specific care — grooming requirements for puppies, adult dogs, senior dogs, and special needs dogs
  • Vaccination and health records — importance of vaccination certificates, health documentation, and disease prevention
  • Nutrition basics for pet owners — diet types, feeding schedules, and nutritional needs for different dog sizes and ages
  • Reproductive health awareness — spaying, neutering, and their impact on grooming and behavior
  • Emergency response basics — handling injuries, heatstroke, choking, and first aid awareness for pet groomers

Module 3: Pre-Grooming Assessment & Dog Bathing Techniques

📚 Topics Covered

  • Pre-grooming consultation — client interview, dog assessment, discussing expectations, and setting realistic outcomes
  • Skin and coat assessment — identifying skin conditions, allergies, matting, parasites, and appropriate treatment recommendations
  • Temperature and vital signs checking — body temperature assessment and recognizing heat stress in dogs
  • Bathing water temperature and pH balance — using lukewarm water, appropriate shampoo selection, and water quality considerations
  • Shampoo selection guide — understanding different shampoo types for Indian climate, coat conditions, and skin sensitivities
  • Step-by-step bathing procedure — wetting, shampooing, rinsing, and preventing water in ears and eyes
  • Drying techniques — towel drying, blow-drying with proper temperature control, and hand-drying methods
  • Handling dogs during bathing — restraint techniques, safety measures, and managing anxious or aggressive dogs in water
  • Bathing safety protocols — non-slip surfaces, proper drainage, ventilation, and preventing slips and falls
  • Post-bath care — conditioning treatments, detangling, and preparing coat for grooming

Module 4: Grooming Techniques: Trimming, Styling & Maintenance

📚 Topics Covered

  • Nail trimming techniques — identifying quick location, using guillotine and scissor clippers, and handling resistant dogs
  • Ear cleaning procedures — safe ear canal cleaning, identifying infections, and using appropriate cleaning solutions
  • Paw pad and interdigital care — trimming hair between toes, checking for injuries, and maintaining healthy paws
  • Facial grooming — eye area cleaning, tear stain removal, beard trimming, and safe handling around sensitive areas
  • Dental care basics — tooth brushing techniques, tartar removal awareness, and recognizing dental problems
  • Tail grooming and anal gland expression — safe techniques, identifying problems, and when to refer to veterinarians
  • Scissor work fundamentals — holding scissors correctly, cutting techniques, and safety practices to prevent injuries
  • Clipper operation and maintenance — understanding clipper grades, blade maintenance, sterilization, and preventing clipper burn
  • Breed-specific grooming styles — Indian popular breeds (Indie dogs, Pomeranians, Labradors, German Shepherds) and their grooming needs
  • Styling for different occasions — regular maintenance cuts, show-style grooming, and seasonal adjustments for Indian weather
  • Hand-stripping and plucking techniques — when and how to use these methods for specific breeds and coat types
  • De-matting and de-shedding — techniques for removing mats, managing seasonal shedding, and preventing future matting

Module 5: Grooming Tools, Equipment & Workplace Safety

📚 Topics Covered

  • Grooming tool identification — clippers, scissors, combs, brushes, nail trimmers, and specialized grooming equipment
  • Tool maintenance and sterilization — cleaning protocols, disinfection procedures, sharpening, and storage best practices
  • Grooming table setup and safety — proper height adjustment, non-slip surfaces, restraint systems, and ergonomic positioning
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE) — aprons, gloves, masks, and footwear for maintaining hygiene and preventing infections
  • Workplace hygiene standards — cleaning grooming areas, disposing waste properly, and preventing cross-contamination
  • Water and electrical safety — safe electrical appliance use, water drainage, and preventing electrical hazards in wet environments
  • Lifting and handling safety — proper body mechanics to prevent back injuries, safe dog lifting techniques, and ergonomic practices
  • Chemical safety — handling shampoos, conditioners, disinfectants, and preventing allergic reactions and skin irritation
  • Noise and ventilation management — managing noise from dryers, ensuring proper air circulation, and protecting hearing
  • Accident prevention and incident reporting — identifying hazards, reporting accidents, and maintaining safe work practices

Module 6: Client Communication, Business Skills & Professional Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Effective client communication — listening skills, explaining procedures clearly, managing expectations, and handling complaints professionally
  • Pricing and quotation preparation — calculating costs, creating service packages, and discussing charges transparently with clients
  • Record keeping and documentation — maintaining client and dog profiles, health records, grooming history, and appointment management
  • Building customer relationships — loyalty programs, follow-up care, referral systems, and creating repeat business
  • Home-based pet care business setup — licensing requirements in India, space planning, investment needed, and regulatory compliance
  • Online presence and marketing — social media for pet care services, before-after photos, client testimonials, and digital marketing basics
  • Time management and scheduling — managing multiple appointments, estimating grooming time, and optimizing daily schedules
  • Continuing education and skill development — staying updated with latest grooming trends, online courses, and professional certifications available in India

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years (16 years preferred for better physical capability)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior experience required — open to all interested candidates
  • Physical fitness — ability to stand for extended periods, lift up to 20 kg, and handle dogs safely
  • Animal love and patience — genuine interest in working with dogs and animal welfare
  • Valid ID proof required at registration (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID, or Driving License)
  • Already working in pet care? Apply for Direct RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Medical fitness — free from allergies to dog hair/dander (medical certificate may be required)
  • Applicable for all candidates across India — urban and rural areas

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Understand dog anatomy, health basics, and behavior to provide safe and appropriate grooming care
  • Perform complete dog bathing, drying, and pre-grooming assessment with proper safety protocols
  • Execute professional nail trimming, ear cleaning, paw care, and facial grooming techniques
  • Operate and maintain grooming tools and equipment safely with proper sterilization practices
  • Communicate effectively with clients, manage expectations, and provide professional customer service
  • Establish and manage a home-based or salon-based pet care business with proper documentation and pricing
