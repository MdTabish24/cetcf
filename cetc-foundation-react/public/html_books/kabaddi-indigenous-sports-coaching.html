📚 Topics Covered

  • History and evolution of Kabaddi in India — Origin from ancient civilizations, regional variations, cultural significance
  • Kabaddi rules and regulations — Official AKFI rules, court dimensions, team composition, scoring system
  • Game variations — Pro Kabaddi format, circle Kabaddi, beach Kabaddi, regional styles (Tamil, Bengali, Punjabi)
  • Other indigenous sports overview — Kho-Kho, Lezium, Kushti, Mallakhamb basics and coaching principles
  • Kabaddi terminology — Raider, defender, bonus line, raid, tackle, out, technical terms in Hindi/regional languages
  • Safety rules and injury prevention — Common injuries in Kabaddi, prevention strategies, first aid basics
  • Equipment and court setup — Mat requirements, boundary markings, player uniforms, safety gear specifications
  • Kabaddi in professional leagues — Pro Kabaddi League structure, state tournaments, career opportunities for coaches
  • International Kabaddi standards — Asian Games format, world championships, rules variations globally
  • Coaching ethics and responsibilities — Player welfare, fair play, anti-doping awareness, duty of care

  • Raid variations — Left-side raids, right-side raids, center raids, circle raids, advanced raiding patterns
  • Defending fundamentals — Blocking techniques, ankle hold, waist hold, shoulder hold, group defense formation
  • Tackle execution — Proper tackling position, timing, body mechanics, safety in tackles
  • Footwork and movement — Kabaddi-specific footwork, directional movement, balance maintenance, court awareness
  • Breathing and chanting — Continuous chanting technique, breath control, maintaining 'kabaddi' sound during raids
  • Defensive formations — Three-point formation, four-point formation, corner defense, flexible defensive strategies
  • Offensive strategies — Raid patterns, player positioning, timing attacks, using bonus lines effectively
  • Transition play — Quick transitions from defense to offense, counter-attack opportunities, team coordination
  • Position-specific skills — Raider roles, defender roles, corner player roles, all-rounder development
  • Game reading and decision-making — Analyzing opponent tactics, adjusting strategies, in-game decision making

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass
  • No prior Kabaddi experience required
  • Physical fitness: Able to demonstrate basic Kabaddi movements
  • Already coaching Kabaddi? Apply for direct RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid ID proof required at registration (Aadhar, Passport, Voter ID, Driving License)
  • Medical fitness certificate recommended (non-mandatory for foundation level)
  • Applicable for all genders and regions across India
