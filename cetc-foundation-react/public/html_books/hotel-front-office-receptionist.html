Hotel Front Office & Receptionist

📂 Sector: Food, Catering & Hospitality 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months 🕐 Hours: 120 💰 Exam Fee: ₹500
Master the art of creating exceptional guest experiences at hotel front desks. This foundation course trains you to become a professional receptionist, handling check-ins, guest queries, reservations, and complaints with confidence and courtesy. Ideal for anyone seeking a career in India's booming hospitality sector.

Module 1: Fundamentals of Hotel Front Office & Guest Service

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to Hotel Industry in India — Understanding hotel classifications, star ratings, and organizational structure in Indian hotels
  • Role of Front Office Department — Key responsibilities of receptionists, front desk staff, and guest relations officers
  • Guest Service Philosophy — Creating positive guest experiences, service standards, and hospitality principles
  • Hotel Department Coordination — Communication between front office, housekeeping, kitchen, and maintenance departments
  • Professional Ethics in Hospitality — Confidentiality, integrity, punctuality, and professional appearance standards
  • Safety and Security Basics — Emergency procedures, guest safety protocols, and security awareness
  • Types of Hotel Guests — Understanding different guest categories: corporate, leisure, families, and long-stay guests
  • Indian Hospitality Standards — Following NRAI guidelines, local regulations, and government hospitality standards

Module 2: Guest Reception & Check-In Procedures

📚 Topics Covered

  • Pre-Arrival Guest Communication — Confirming bookings, sending welcome messages, special request handling
  • Guest Greeting & Welcome Protocol — First impression techniques, greeting in multiple languages, warm hospitality
  • Check-In Process Step-by-Step — Registration, document verification, room key assignment, rate confirmation
  • Guest Information Form Completion — Collecting accurate guest data, address verification, contact details
  • Room Allocation & Assignment — Assigning appropriate rooms based on guest preference, occupancy, and special needs
  • Payment Methods & Security — Cash handling, card payments, digital wallets, GST compliance in India
  • Special Requests Management — Handling room preferences, dietary requirements, transportation, tour bookings
  • VIP & Corporate Guest Handling — Extra courtesy protocols, complimentary services, executive lounge access
  • Group Check-In Procedures — Managing large group arrivals, batch registration, coordination with tour operators
  • Documentation & Record Keeping — Maintaining guest records, ID verification (Aadhaar/Passport), compliance with FEMA rules

Module 3: Reservation Management & Room Inventory

📚 Topics Covered

  • Reservation Systems Overview — Understanding PMS (Property Management System), online booking platforms, OTA channels
  • Taking Phone & Email Reservations — Accurate booking details, rate confirmation, special requests, cancellation policies
  • Online Booking Platforms — Managing OYO, Booking.com, MakeMyTrip, Trivago, and hotel website reservations
  • Room Inventory Management — Room types, occupancy tracking, availability status, rate management
  • Overbooking & Walk-In Management — Handling overbooking situations, walk-in guests, last-minute bookings
  • Cancellation & Modification Policies — Understanding cancellation rules, refund procedures, policy communication
  • Group Bookings & Corporate Contracts — Managing group reservations, contract rates, advance payments
  • Rate Management & Pricing — Understanding rack rates, discounted rates, seasonal pricing, promotional offers
  • Reservation Confirmation & Communication — Sending confirmation emails, reminder calls, pre-arrival information
  • No-Show & Late Cancellation Handling — Recording no-shows, revenue management, guest communication

Module 4: Billing, Payments & Financial Transactions

📚 Topics Covered

  • Guest Folio Management — Understanding room charges, restaurant bills, service charges, taxes
  • GST Compliance for Hotels — Calculating SGST/CGST on room rent, food, and services in India
  • Cash Handling Procedures — Cash register management, daily reconciliation, cash security protocols
  • Card Payment Processing — Credit card, debit card, and digital payment handling, PCI compliance
  • Digital Payment Methods — UPI, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and digital wallet transactions
  • Invoice & Bill Generation — Creating accurate bills, itemization, rate breakdown, payment terms
  • Advance Payment & Deposits — Collecting advance payments, security deposits, refund procedures
  • Currency Exchange & Foreign Guest Payments — Handling forex transactions, exchange rates, compliance with RBI rules
  • Billing Disputes & Adjustments — Resolving billing issues, offering complimentary services, discount authorization
  • Checkout Procedures & Final Settlement — Express checkout, late checkout charges, settlement verification, guest feedback

Module 5: Communication Skills & Guest Problem Resolution

📚 Topics Covered

  • Professional Communication Basics — Clear speech, active listening, non-verbal communication, body language
  • Telephone Etiquette & Skills — Answering calls professionally, transferring calls, taking messages, managing hold time
  • Email Communication Standards — Professional email writing, response time, tone, formal and informal language
  • Multilingual Communication — Greeting guests in Hindi, English, regional languages, and basic international phrases
  • Handling Difficult Guests — Remaining calm, empathy, de-escalation techniques, finding solutions
  • Complaint Resolution Process — Listening, acknowledging, apologizing, taking action, follow-up
  • Common Guest Complaints — Room cleanliness, noise, temperature, Wi-Fi issues, service delays, billing errors
  • Feedback Collection & Management — Guest satisfaction surveys, online reviews, continuous improvement
  • Conflict Resolution Techniques — Win-win solutions, involving management, compensation offers, guest retention
  • Guest Privacy & Confidentiality — Never sharing guest information, security of personal data, GDPR awareness

Module 6: Front Office Technology & Practical Operations

📚 Topics Covered

  • Property Management System (PMS) Training — Logging in, navigating modules, room status updates, guest profiles
  • PMS Reservation Module — Creating bookings, modifying reservations, cancellations, group management
  • PMS Front Office Operations — Check-in/check-out processes, room assignment, guest communication
  • PMS Billing & Payment Module — Creating folios, posting charges, processing payments, generating invoices
  • Hotel Management Software Alternatives — InnKey, Hotelogix, Cloudbeds, and other Indian hotel software
  • Wi-Fi & Internet Connectivity — Guest Wi-Fi management, login procedures, troubleshooting, bandwidth management
  • Phone Systems & PBX — Using hotel telephone systems, call routing, voicemail, wake-up calls
  • Email & Communication Tools — Managing hotel email, guest communication platforms, instant messaging
  • Security Systems & Access Control — Key card systems, CCTV monitoring, alarm systems, emergency protocols
  • Point of Sale (POS) Integration — Understanding POS systems, posting restaurant and bar charges to guest folios
  • Housekeeping Communication — Room status updates, maintenance requests, special cleaning, turndown service coordination
  • Practical Front Office Simulation — Mock check-ins, reservations, billing, complaint handling, real-world scenarios

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass (or equivalent)
  • No prior experience required
  • Currently employed in hospitality? Apply for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) exam
  • Valid identity proof required (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, or Voter ID)
  • Applicable for all Indian citizens and foreign nationals on valid visa
  • Physical fitness required for standing and walking during shifts
  • No criminal background required

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Perform professional guest reception and check-in procedures with accuracy and courtesy
  • Manage hotel reservations using Property Management Systems and online booking platforms
  • Handle guest payments, billing, and financial transactions with GST compliance
  • Resolve guest complaints and problems using effective communication and empathy
  • Operate front office technology including PMS, phone systems, and hotel software
  • Maintain guest privacy, security, and confidentiality according to hospitality standards
  • Coordinate with other hotel departments to ensure seamless guest experience
  • Demonstrate professional ethics, appearance, and customer service excellence
