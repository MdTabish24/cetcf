Domestic Helper Etiquette & Training

📂 Sector: Child Care & Domestic Services 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 1 Month 🕐 Hours: 60 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Yeh course aapko ek professional domestic helper banata hai jo ghar ke kaam ko sahi tareeke se aur respect ke saath karega. Aap seekhenge proper etiquette, child care basics, cleaning standards aur employer ke saath communication skills. Certified hone ke baad aap achche ghar mein kaam kar sakte ho aur achcha salary earn kar sakte ho.

Module 1: Professional Etiquette aur Workplace Behavior

📚 Topics Covered

  • Professional dress code aur personal grooming — sahi kapde, saaf-safai, aur appearance kaise rakhni chahiye ghar mein kaam karte hue
  • Communication skills aur polite behavior — employer aur family members ke saath kaise baat karni chahiye, respect dikhana
  • Time management aur punctuality — sahi time par kaam karna, schedule follow karna, aur reliability dikhaana
  • Confidentiality aur privacy — ghar ke private matters ko bahar nahi bolna, family secrets rakhna
  • Problem-solving aur patience — mushkil situations mein kaise react karna chahiye, calm rehna
  • Cultural sensitivity aur respect — different families ke customs aur traditions ko samjhna aur respect karna
  • Workplace safety aur accident prevention — ghar mein safe kaise kaam karna, injuries se bachna
  • Customer service mindset — employer ke expectations ko samjhna aur exceed karna, feedback lena
  • Conflict resolution — agar koi misunderstanding ho to kaise solve karna chahiye
  • Documentation aur record keeping — work timings, duties, aur responsibilities ka record rakhna

Module 2: Child Care Fundamentals aur Safety

📚 Topics Covered

  • Child development stages — babies, toddlers, aur school-age children ki zaruri cheezein aur behavior
  • Basic child care routine — feeding, bathing, diaper changing, sleeping schedule ko properly manage karna
  • Nutrition aur healthy eating — bacchon ke liye kaunse khane se health badhti hai, balanced diet kya hota hai
  • Hygiene practices for children — bacchon ko kaise saaf-suthre rakhna chahiye, germs se bachana
  • Child safety aur accident prevention — ghar mein kaunse cheezein dangerous hain bacchon ke liye, unhe protect karna
  • First aid basics — minor injuries, burns, choking jaise emergencies mein kya karna chahiye
  • Emotional support aur child psychology — bacchon ke emotions ko samjhna, unhe comfort dena, tantrums handle karna
  • Play aur learning activities — bacchon ke mental development ke liye kaunse activities achche hain
  • Vaccination aur health checkups — bacchon ki health schedule ko track karna, doctor ke instructions follow karna
  • Communication with parents — bacchon ke baare mein parents ko regular updates dena, concerns share karna
  • Handling emergencies — agar bachcha sick ho jaye ya accident ho to kya karna chahiye, doctor ko kaise call karna
  • Age-appropriate discipline — bacchon ko sahi tareeke se discipline karna, punishment nahi balki guidance dena

Module 3: Home Cleaning aur Maintenance

📚 Topics Covered

  • Kitchen cleaning aur hygiene — bartan dhona, counters saaf karna, food safety standards follow karna
  • Bathroom cleaning aur sanitation — toilet, sink, aur tiles ko properly clean aur disinfect karna
  • Floor cleaning techniques — different types ke floors (tile, marble, wooden) ko kaise clean karna
  • Dusting aur decluttering — furniture, shelves, aur decorative items ko properly dust karna aur organize karna
  • Laundry management — clothes dhona, ironing, folding aur proper storage
  • Waste management aur recycling — garbage ko properly segregate karna, waste ko responsibly handle karna
  • Cleaning supplies aur their proper use — different chemicals aur tools ka sahi use, safety precautions
  • Stain removal aur fabric care — different types ke stains ko remove karna, fabrics ko damage na karna
  • Organization aur storage — ghar ko neat aur organized rakhna, things ko proper place mein rakhna
  • Seasonal cleaning — seasonal changes ke according ghar ko maintain karna, deep cleaning kab karna
  • Pest control basics — insects aur rodents se ghar ko protect karna, cleanliness se prevention

Module 4: Basic Cooking aur Food Preparation

📚 Topics Covered

  • Kitchen safety aur food hygiene — kitchen mein accidents se bachna, food poisoning se prevent karna
  • Basic cooking techniques — boiling, frying, baking, steaming jaise basic methods
  • Indian cuisine basics — common Indian dishes, spices, aur traditional cooking methods
  • Meal planning — family ke liye balanced meals plan karna, nutritious khana prepare karna
  • Food storage aur preservation — khane ko properly store karna, freshness maintain karna, expiry dates check karna
  • Cooking for children — bacchon ke liye soft, nutritious, aur tasty khana kaise banate hain
  • Dietary restrictions — agar family members ko koi allergy ya dietary restriction ho to kaise handle karna
  • Cooking utensils aur equipment care — bartan, cooker, aur kitchen equipment ko properly maintain karna
  • Budget-friendly cooking — saste mein achha khana kaise banate hain, waste na karna
  • Cooking measurements aur recipes — proper quantities samjhna, recipes ko follow karna

Module 5: Health, Hygiene aur Personal Care

📚 Topics Covered

  • Personal hygiene standards — apne aap ko kaise clean aur healthy rakhni chahiye, germ prevention
  • Infectious diseases aur prevention — common diseases kaunse hain, unhe kaise prevent karna, symptoms kya hain
  • Respiratory health aur mask usage — COVID ke baad mask aur sanitization ke norms, respiratory safety
  • Handwashing aur sanitation protocols — proper handwashing technique, kab wash karna chahiye
  • Waste handling aur disposal — hazardous waste, medical waste ko safely handle karna
  • Health monitoring — apni health ko track karna, regular checkups karana, vaccinations update rakhna
  • Mental health aur stress management — kaam ke stress ko handle karna, healthy mindset maintain karna
  • Common health issues aur remedies — fever, cough, stomach issues jaise common problems ka basic knowledge
  • Hygiene practices in food handling — khana banate time kaunse hygiene rules follow karna chahiye

Module 6: Legal Rights, Responsibilities aur Career Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Employment rights aur responsibilities — domestic helper ke kya rights hain, kya duties hain, contract kya hota hai
  • Wages aur payment terms — salary structure, payment methods, deductions kya ho sakte hain
  • Working hours aur leave entitlements — kitne hours kaam karna padta hai, leave policy kya hai
  • Safety aur welfare schemes — government schemes jo domestic workers ke liye available hain, insurance options
  • Handling disputes aur grievances — agar koi issue ho to kaise resolve karna, complaint kaise file karna
  • Documentation aur record keeping — apne documents ko safely rakhna, employment records maintain karna
  • Career growth opportunities — domestic helper se kya-kya positions mein promotion ho sakta hai, further training options
  • Skill upgradation aur specialization — specialized skills seekhne ke options, income increase ke ways

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years (18 years preferred for child care roles)
  • Minimum education: Class 5th pass (Class 8th pass preferred)
  • No prior experience needed - beginners welcome
  • Already working as domestic helper? Apply for RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam directly
  • Valid ID proof required at registration (Aadhar, Voter ID, PAN, Driving License)
  • Medical fitness certificate recommended for child care positions
  • Applicable for all genders aur all social backgrounds
  • No criminal record (background verification may be required by employers)

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Candidate professional aur ethical behavior demonstrate kar sakta hai, proper dress code aur grooming maintain kar sakta hai, aur employer ke saath respectful communication kar sakta hai
  • Candidate child care basics samajhta hai - feeding, bathing, hygiene, safety precautions, aur age-appropriate activities provide kar sakta hai
  • Candidate ghar ke sab areas ko systematically clean aur organize kar sakta hai - kitchen, bathroom, bedroom, living areas - professional standards ke according
  • Candidate basic Indian cooking skills use karke nutritious aur tasty meals prepare kar sakta hai, food safety follow kar sakta hai, aur dietary restrictions respect kar sakta hai
  • Candidate personal health aur hygiene maintain karta hai, common health issues ko recognize karta hai, aur first aid basics apply kar sakta hai
  • Candidate apne employment rights aur responsibilities samajhta hai, workplace disputes handle kar sakta hai, aur career growth opportunities identify kar sakta hai
