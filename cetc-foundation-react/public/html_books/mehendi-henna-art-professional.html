Mehendi / Henna Art Professional

📂 Sector: Beauty & Lifestyle 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months 🕐 Hours: 120 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the ancient art of Mehendi (Henna) and turn it into a profitable career. This foundation course teaches you professional mehendi design, application techniques, and business skills to work at weddings, events, and beauty salons across India. Learn from industry experts and build a client base from day one.

Module 1: Introduction to Mehendi Art & Industry Overview

📚 Topics Covered

  • History and cultural significance of mehendi in India — Understanding mehendi's role in Indian weddings and festivals across regions
  • Types of mehendi (natural, herbal, chemical-based) — Composition, quality differences, and how to choose the best
  • Indian mehendi market trends and opportunities — Current demand in weddings, events, salons, and online services
  • Career opportunities in mehendi art — Employment in salons, event management, freelancing, and starting own business
  • Safety and hygiene standards — FSSAI guidelines for henna products, allergy testing, and client safety protocols
  • Tools and equipment overview — Introduction to cones, applicators, stencils, and workspace setup
  • Client communication basics — Understanding client expectations, taking bookings, and managing appointments
  • Introduction to business fundamentals — Pricing strategies, cost calculation, and profit margins in mehendi business

Module 2: Henna Paste Preparation & Product Knowledge

📚 Topics Covered

  • Henna plant and extraction process — Where henna grows, harvesting, drying, and powder quality assessment
  • Natural henna paste preparation from scratch — Step-by-step recipe using henna powder, oils, lemon juice, and additives
  • Chemical composition and stain development — Understanding PPD (para-phenylenediamine), dye molecules, and color intensity
  • Paste consistency and texture management — Achieving perfect thickness for smooth application without breaking
  • Storage and shelf-life management — Proper storage conditions, preventing paste degradation, and freshness indicators
  • Quality testing and color prediction — Testing henna on skin patch, predicting final color shade, and managing client expectations
  • Additives and enhancers — Use of eucalyptus oil, lemon juice, sugar, gum arabic for better stain and durability
  • Troubleshooting common paste problems — Fixing too thick, too thin, low stain, or uneven color issues
  • Allergies and patch testing protocol — Identifying allergic reactions, performing 24-hour patch tests, and maintaining safety records
  • Comparing commercial vs. homemade paste — Cost analysis, quality differences, and when to use each type

Module 3: Basic Mehendi Design Patterns & Techniques

📚 Topics Covered

  • Fundamental design elements — Dots, lines, curves, flowers, leaves, and how to combine them into patterns
  • Regional mehendi styles — Rajasthani (bold, geometric), Gujarati (intricate), Marathi, Bengali, and South Indian styles
  • Hand mapping and space planning — Understanding hand anatomy, dividing space proportionally, and balancing designs
  • Cone holding and grip techniques — Proper hand position, wrist movement, and pressure control for consistent lines
  • Basic floral patterns — Rose, lotus, jasmine, and how to create them with simple strokes
  • Geometric and abstract designs — Creating borders, fillings, and modern patterns for contemporary clients
  • Finger and palm designs — Special techniques for highlighting fingers, creating focal points on palm
  • Practice exercises on paper and synthetic skin — Building muscle memory before working on actual clients
  • Speed and efficiency drills — Completing designs within time limits (15-30 minutes for quick designs)
  • Design sketching and planning — Drawing designs on hand with pencil before applying henna
  • Creating personal design portfolio — Documenting your best work for client reference and social media
  • Adapting designs for different hand sizes and skin types — Scaling patterns and adjusting for individual client needs

Module 4: Bridal & Occasion Mehendi Specialization

📚 Topics Covered

  • Bridal mehendi design standards — Understanding bride's expectations, creating elaborate full-hand designs, and incorporating groom's name
  • Full-hand coverage techniques — Working from wrist to fingertips, creating continuous flow, and filling empty spaces
  • Advanced floral compositions — Creating realistic flowers, shading effects, and three-dimensional appearance
  • Peacock and bird motifs — Drawing peacocks, parrots, and other birds which are popular in bridal mehendi
  • Groom's name incorporation — Creatively hiding initials or full name within design patterns
  • Foot and leg mehendi — Designing for ankles, feet, and extending to calves for complete bridal look
  • Back-hand and arm extension — Creating seamless flow from hand to forearm and upper arm
  • Bridal mehendi timing and aftercare — Understanding stain darkening process (24-48 hours) and advising clients on care
  • Festival mehendi designs — Specific patterns for Diwali, Eid, Karwa Chauth, and other occasions
  • Quick mehendi for office events — Corporate mehendi designs that are professional yet festive (15-20 minutes)
  • Kids' mehendi designs — Child-friendly patterns, smaller sizes, and faster application
  • Mehendi for different age groups — Adapting designs for teenagers, young women, and older clients

Module 5: Client Management, Hygiene & Professional Practice

📚 Topics Covered

  • Client consultation process — Taking detailed notes, understanding preferences, discussing budget and timeline
  • Workspace setup and sanitation — Organizing tools, maintaining cleanliness, preventing cross-contamination
  • Personal hygiene and appearance standards — Dress code, hand washing, nail care, and maintaining professional image
  • Infection control and sterilization — Sterilizing cones, applicators, and reusable tools using proper methods
  • Handling difficult clients and complaints — Managing expectations, resolving issues, and maintaining relationships
  • Documentation and record-keeping — Maintaining client information, design preferences, and allergy records
  • Pricing models and negotiation — Per-hand pricing, hourly rates, package deals, and seasonal pricing
  • Time management during events — Managing multiple clients, prioritizing work, and meeting deadlines
  • Creating contracts and agreements — Simple booking forms, cancellation policies, and payment terms
  • Building client loyalty and repeat business — Follow-up communication, referral programs, and loyalty discounts
  • Professional conduct and ethics — Respecting client privacy, maintaining confidentiality, and professional behavior
  • Health and safety compliance — Following local health department regulations and maintaining certification

Module 6: Business Setup, Marketing & Career Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Starting your mehendi business — Registration, licensing, GST compliance, and legal requirements in India
  • Salon vs. freelance vs. home-based business — Comparing models, pros-cons, and startup costs for each
  • Initial investment and cost analysis — Tools, materials, workspace setup, and break-even calculation
  • Pricing strategy and profit margins — Understanding costs, setting competitive prices, and maximizing earnings
  • Social media marketing for mehendi artists — Instagram, Facebook, YouTube strategies, posting mehendi photos and videos

  • Networking and referral generation — Building relationships with wedding planners, photographers, and other vendors
  • Wedding season strategy and planning — Preparing for peak seasons (Oct-Dec, Apr-May), bulk bookings, and team management
  • Scaling your business — Hiring assistants, training others, and expanding to multiple locations
  • Continuous learning and skill upgradation — Attending workshops, learning new designs, and staying updated with trends
  • Financial management and record-keeping — Maintaining income-expense records, tax filing, and business planning

  • Minimum age: 14 years (parental consent required if below 18)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior experience needed - open to all candidates
  • Already working as mehendi artist? Apply for RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid ID proof required at registration (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID, or Passport)
  • Applicable for all candidates regardless of gender, caste, or religion
  • Medical fitness certificate recommended (no serious hand injuries or skin conditions)
  • Willingness to work with natural and chemical products

💼 Career Paths

Professional Mehendi Artist (Freelance)
Work independently at weddings, festivals, and events. Flexibility to choose clients and set rates.
Salon Mehendi Specialist
Work at beauty salons, spas, or unisex parlors with fixed salary plus commission on bookings.
Mehendi Business Owner
Own mehendi studio or chain, hire artists, manage multiple bookings, and build brand presence.
Event Management Mehendi Artist
Work with wedding planners, event management companies, and organize mehendi ceremonies.
Bridal Mehendi Specialist
Specialize in high-end bridal mehendi with premium pricing during wedding seasons.
Online Mehendi Training Instructor
Create online courses, YouTube tutorials, and conduct virtual workshops to teach mehendi art.
Mehendi Product Supplier
Manufacture and supply henna paste, cones, and tools to other artists and salons.
Mehendi Designer & Consultant
Create custom designs, provide consultations, and collaborate with photographers and wedding planners.
