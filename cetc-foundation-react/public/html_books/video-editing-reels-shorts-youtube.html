✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years (parental consent required for under-18)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior video editing experience required
  • Basic computer literacy essential (file management, internet browsing)
  • Access to a computer with minimum 4GB RAM (8GB+ recommended)
  • Already working as a video editor? Apply for RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid ID proof required at registration (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID, or Passport)
  • Applicable for all Indian citizens and foreign nationals with valid visa
  • No age limit for working professionals seeking certification

💼 Career Paths

Freelance Video Editor
Edit videos for content creators, small businesses, and agencies on platforms like Fiverr, Upwork, and local Indian platforms. Flexible hours, multiple clients, high earning potential.
Content Creator / YouTuber
Create and edit your own video content for YouTube, Instagram, TikTok. Earn through AdSense, sponsorships, and brand collaborations. Scale to team-based production.
In-House Video Editor (Agency/Company)
Work for digital marketing agencies, production houses, or corporate media teams. Stable income, team environment, benefits, and skill growth opportunities.
Social Media Manager / Content Strategist
Manage social media accounts for brands and businesses. Edit and optimize videos for multiple platforms. Combine editing with strategy and analytics.
Video Production House Owner
Start your own video production company. Offer editing, shooting, and post-production services to corporates, events, and creators. Build a team and scale operations.
Editing Trainer / Online Course Creator
Create and sell video editing courses on Udemy, YouTube, or your own platform. Build passive income while helping others learn. Combine with freelance work.
Corporate Video Editor
Edit promotional videos, training content, and internal communications for large corporations. Steady work, professional environment, benefits, and growth potential.
E-Learning Content Editor
Edit educational videos for online platforms, coaching centers, and universities. Growing demand in Indian ed-tech sector. Flexible remote work options.
