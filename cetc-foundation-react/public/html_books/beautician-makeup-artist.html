Beautician & Makeup Artist

📂 Sector: Beauty & Lifestyle 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 3 Months 🕐 Hours: 180 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Yeh Beautician & Makeup Artist course aapko beauty industry mein entry level skills provide karega. Aapko basic skincare, hair removal, nail care aur everyday makeup techniques sikhaye jayenge, jisse aap ek confident beauty professional ban saken.

Module 1: Fundamentals of Beauty & Salon Management

📚 Topics Covered

  • Beauty Industry ka Introduction & Career Scope in India — Indian beauty market, jobs aur growth ke mauke.
  • Personal Grooming & Professional Etiquette — Kaise presentable dikhein aur clients se professional tareeke se baat karein.
  • Hygiene, Sanitation & Sterilization — Beauty tools aur workspace ko saaf aur germ-free kaise rakhein.
  • Salon Safety & First Aid Basics — Salon mein safety rules aur emergency situations ke liye basic first aid.
  • Client Consultation & Communication Skills — Client ki needs samajhna, unko services explain karna aur achhe se communicate karna.
  • Skin & Hair Anatomy ka Basic Gyaan — Skin types, hair growth cycle aur body parts ka fundamental knowledge.
  • Product Knowledge & Storage — Different beauty products ko pehchanna, unke uses aur unhe sahi se store karna.
  • Salon Environment & Workflow Management — Salon operations ko samajhna, appointments aur time manage karna.
  • Beauty Tools aur Equipment ka Use & Maintenance — Common beauty tools ko operate karna aur unki care karna.
  • Basic Customer Service & Client Retention Tips — Clients ko khush rakhna aur unhe wapas aane ke liye encourage karna.

Module 2: Skincare Basics & Facial Treatments

📚 Topics Covered

  • Skin Types & Conditions ko Pehchanna — Oily, dry, combination, sensitive skin aur common skin problems (pimples, pigmentation).
  • Daily Skincare Routine & Product Selection — Cleansing, toning, moisturizing (CTM) ka importance aur sahi products chunna.
  • Basic Facial Steps: Cleansing, Toning, Exfoliation — Face ko saaf karna, dead skin hatana aur skin ko refresh karna.
  • Face Massage Techniques (Basic) — Blood circulation improve karna aur client ko relax karna.
  • Steam & Extraction ka Sahi Tareeka — Blackheads aur whiteheads ko safely aur hygienically remove karna.
  • Face Packs & Masks ka Application — Different skin types ke liye suitable face packs aur masks lagana.
  • Cleanup Facial (Normal Skin) ka Practical — Ghar par ya salon mein basic cleanup ka hands-on practice.
  • Fruit Facial (Dry/Dehydrated Skin) ka Practical — Natural ingredients se skin ko nourish aur hydrate karna.
  • Eye Care & Lip Care Treatments — Dark circles, puffy eyes aur chapped lips ke liye special care.
  • Aftercare Advice for Clients — Facial ke baad skin ki care kaise karein, iske liye clients ko guidance dena.

Module 3: Hair Removal Techniques

📚 Topics Covered

  • Hair Growth Cycle & Types of Hair — Hair growth patterns aur body hair ke different types ko samajhna.
  • Threading (Eyebrows, Upper Lip, Forehead) ka Practical — Dhage se unwanted hair remove karne ki technique.
  • Waxing ka Introduction & Types (Hot, Cold, Rica) — Different types of wax aur unke specific uses.
  • Waxing Preparation & Aftercare — Skin ko wax ke liye ready karna aur waxing ke baad care tips.
  • Full Arm & Half Leg Waxing ka Practical — Hands-on practice for waxing arms aur legs.
  • Underarm & Stomach/Back Waxing ka Practical — Sensitive areas par waxing ki technique.
  • Bleaching ka Sahi Tareeka (Face & Body) — Hair ko lighten karne ke liye bleaching process.
  • Hygiene & Safety while Hair Removal — Hair removal ke dauran infections se bachne ke tareeke.
  • Ingrown Hair & Skin Irritation se Bachao — Waxing ke side effects ko prevent aur treat karna.
  • Client Comfort & Pain Management Tips — Hair removal ke dauran client ko comfortable feel karana.

  • Pedicure ka Sahi Tareeka (Classic Pedicure) — Feet ko clean, smooth aur fresh banane ki complete process.
  • Nail Shaping & Filing Techniques — Different nail shapes (square, oval, round) aur filing methods.
  • Cuticle Care & Nail Buffing — Cuticles ko safely push back karna aur nails ko shine karna.
  • Hand & Foot Massage Techniques — Relaxation aur blood circulation improve karne ke liye massage.
  • Nail Polish Application (Basic) — Perfect aur long-lasting nail polish lagane ka tareeka.
  • Basic Nail Art Designs (e.g., French, polka dots) — Simple aur attractive nail art designs banana.
  • Paraffin Wax Treatment (Hands & Feet) ka Introduction — Deep moisturizing aur relaxation ke liye paraffin wax.
  • Hygiene & Sterilization for Manicure/Pedicure Tools — Manicure aur pedicure tools ko saaf aur sterilize karna.
