Plastic Recycling & Upcycling

📂 Sector: Environmental & Green Skills 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 1 Month 🕐 Hours: 60 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Learn the skills to transform waste plastic into valuable products while protecting our environment. This hands-on course teaches you plastic sorting, processing, and upcycling techniques that are in high demand across India's growing recycling industry. Start your green career today with practical, job-ready training.

Module 1: Fundamentals of Plastic & Environmental Impact

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to plastics — types, properties, and common uses in India — Understanding PET, HDPE, PVC, LDPE, PP, PS, and other plastic polymers
  • Plastic waste crisis in India — statistics, environmental problems, and government initiatives like Swachh Bharat and plastic ban regulations
  • Types of plastic waste — single-use plastics, industrial waste, household waste, and e-waste plastics
  • Environmental impact of plastic pollution — soil contamination, water pollution, marine life harm, and health hazards
  • Indian regulations and policies — Plastic Waste Management Rules 2016, Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR), and state-level bans
  • Circular economy concept — how recycling fits into sustainable development and resource conservation
  • Benefits of plastic recycling — economic value, job creation, energy savings, and carbon footprint reduction
  • Introduction to upcycling — definition, difference from recycling, and creative possibilities
  • Market opportunities in plastic recycling in India — current trends and growth projections

Module 2: Plastic Identification, Sorting & Collection

📚 Topics Covered

  • Plastic identification techniques — visual inspection, resin codes, density testing, and burn tests for identifying plastic types
  • Understanding resin identification codes (RIC) — symbols 1-7 and their meanings on plastic products
  • Sorting methods — manual sorting, mechanical sorting, and advanced technology (optical sorters)
  • Collection systems in India — municipal collection, door-to-door collection, collection centers, and dump yards
  • Contamination management — removing labels, adhesives, food residue, and other contaminants from plastic waste
  • Safety protocols during collection and handling — protective equipment, hygiene, and injury prevention
  • Role of waste pickers and informal sector — understanding the waste management ecosystem in Indian cities
  • Setting up collection points — location selection, storage, and hygiene standards
  • Documentation and tracking — maintaining records of plastic waste collected and segregated
  • Quality control in sorted plastic — grading and checking for reusability

Module 3: Plastic Recycling Processes & Machinery

📚 Topics Covered

  • Recycling process overview — collection, sorting, cleaning, shredding, melting, and pelletizing steps
  • Shredding and grinding equipment — types of shredders, operation, maintenance, and safety features
  • Washing and cleaning systems — water-based cleaning, drying techniques, and contamination removal
  • Melting and extrusion process — plastic melting temperatures for different types, extrusion basics, and quality control
  • Pelletizing machines — converting melted plastic into pellets, specifications, and output rates
  • Injection molding basics — how recycled plastic is converted into new products
  • Equipment operation and maintenance — daily checks, troubleshooting, and preventive maintenance
  • Safety in recycling facilities — electrical hazards, thermal hazards, chemical exposure, and emergency procedures
  • Cost analysis of recycling operations — input costs, output value, and profitability factors
  • Scaling up operations — from small-scale to commercial recycling units
  • Technology trends — IoT-enabled sorting, AI-based quality control, and automation in Indian recycling industry
  • Environmental compliance — emission control, waste water treatment, and regulatory certifications

Module 4: Upcycling Techniques & Product Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Upcycling vs recycling — understanding the difference and value addition in upcycling
  • Creative upcycling ideas — converting plastic waste into useful household items, fashion products, and decorative items
  • Plastic bag upcycling — creating bags, mats, rugs, and storage solutions from waste plastic bags
  • Bottle upcycling — making planters, organizers, lamps, and craft items from plastic bottles
  • Plastic container and packaging upcycling — converting into furniture, storage boxes, and organizational items
  • Textile and fashion upcycling — creating yarn from plastic waste, making bags, wallets, and apparel
  • Home decor upcycling projects — wall art, furniture, lighting solutions, and garden installations
  • Design thinking for upcycling — identifying market demand, creating prototypes, and cost estimation
  • Tools and techniques for upcycling — cutting, joining, painting, and finishing methods
  • Quality and durability in upcycled products — ensuring safety, functionality, and longevity

Module 5: Business Operations & Entrepreneurship

📚 Topics Covered

  • Starting a plastic recycling business — business plan, capital requirements, and location selection in India
  • Licensing and registration — obtaining necessary permits, MSME registration, GST compliance, and environmental clearances
  • Supply chain management — sourcing waste plastic, supplier relationships, and inventory management
  • Pricing and profitability — calculating costs, setting prices, and understanding market rates for recycled plastic
  • Marketing recycled and upcycled products — online and offline channels, social media, and B2B partnerships
  • Financial management — budgeting, accounting, managing cash flow, and understanding profit margins
  • Scaling your business — expanding operations, hiring staff, and quality consistency
  • Building partnerships — connecting with municipal corporations, NGOs, corporate CSR programs, and retailers
  • Government schemes and subsidies — PMEGP, MUDRA loans, and state-level incentives for recycling businesses
  • Sustainability reporting — documenting environmental impact and social responsibility

Module 6: Safety, Quality & Practical Skills Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Workplace safety standards — PPE requirements, hazard identification, and risk assessment in recycling facilities
  • Health and hygiene protocols — sanitation, disease prevention, and worker health monitoring
  • Accident prevention and first aid — common accidents, emergency response, and basic first aid knowledge
  • Quality assurance in recycled products — testing standards, material specifications, and customer satisfaction
  • Documentation and record-keeping — batch tracking, quality logs, and regulatory compliance records
  • Practical hands-on training — sorting plastic waste, operating basic equipment, and creating upcycled products
  • Case studies from Indian recycling industry — success stories, lessons learned, and best practices
  • Problem-solving in recycling operations — troubleshooting equipment, managing contamination, and improving efficiency
  • Networking and industry connections — industry associations, trade shows, and professional development
  • Continuous learning — staying updated with new technologies, market trends, and environmental regulations

  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass (or equivalent)
  • No prior experience required
  • Already working in waste management? Apply for direct RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam

  • Applicable for all candidates including persons with disabilities
  • No upper age limit — suitable for career changers and retirees

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and classify different types of plastic waste using resin codes and physical properties
  • Operate basic plastic recycling equipment safely and perform routine maintenance
  • Execute proper waste collection, segregation, and contamination removal procedures
  • Create functional upcycled products from waste plastic materials
  • Understand and comply with Indian plastic waste management regulations and safety standards
  • Develop a basic business plan for a plastic recycling or upcycling enterprise and identify market opportunities
