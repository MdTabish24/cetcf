Laundry & Dry Cleaning Operations

📂 Sector: Child Care & Domestic Services 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 1 Month 🕐 Hours: 60 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the essential skills of professional laundry and dry cleaning operations in just 4 weeks. This foundation course teaches you fabric care, stain removal, pressing techniques, and customer service to start a rewarding career in the growing laundry industry. Perfect for beginners looking to work in commercial laundries, hotels, or start their own cleaning business.

Module 1: Fundamentals of Laundry & Fabric Care

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to laundry industry in India — career opportunities, employment sectors, and business potential
  • Fabric types and fiber identification — cotton, silk, wool, polyester, blended fabrics, and their properties
  • Understanding fabric care labels — interpretation of washing symbols, temperature codes, and bleach instructions
  • Water quality and its impact — hardness, pH levels, and how water affects washing results
  • Detergents and washing agents — types, composition, and selection for different fabrics and stains
  • Health and safety in laundry operations — chemical handling, personal protective equipment (PPE), and workplace safety
  • Basic laundry equipment overview — washing machines, dryers, pressing equipment, and their functions
  • Environmental practices in laundry — water conservation, waste management, and eco-friendly operations
  • Customer communication basics — handling inquiries, understanding customer needs, and managing expectations

Module 2: Washing & Stain Removal Techniques

📚 Topics Covered

  • Stain identification and classification — organic, inorganic, combination stains, and aging effects on stains
  • Pre-treatment methods — soaking, scrubbing, and chemical application for different stain types
  • Stain removal for common substances — blood, oil, wine, ink, chocolate, grass, rust, and food stains
  • Bleaching and whitening agents — oxygen bleach, chlorine bleach, and their safe application on different fabrics
  • Washing cycle selection — normal, delicate, heavy-duty cycles and temperature settings for various garments
  • Fabric sorting and segregation — color separation, weight distribution, and preventing color bleeding
  • Detergent dosing calculations — measuring correct amounts based on load size, water hardness, and soil level
  • Washing machine operation — loading techniques, cycle monitoring, and troubleshooting common issues
  • Drying methods — air drying, tumble drying, and fabric-specific drying techniques to prevent damage
  • Pressing and ironing basics — equipment setup, temperature control, and technique for different fabrics
  • Quality checking after washing — inspection for cleanliness, damage, and color consistency

Module 3: Dry Cleaning Operations & Advanced Techniques

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to dry cleaning process — advantages, limitations, and when to use dry cleaning vs wet washing
  • Dry cleaning solvents — perchloroethylene (PCE), hydrocarbon, and eco-friendly solvent options available in India
  • Garment inspection before dry cleaning — identifying delicate trims, buttons, embroidery, and potential problem areas
  • Machine operation for dry cleaning — loading, solvent selection, cycle timing, and temperature control
  • Spot cleaning and pre-treatment for dry cleaning — specialized stain removal before machine processing
  • Post-dry cleaning finishing — pressing, steaming, and final inspection of dry-cleaned garments
  • Specialty garment handling — wedding dresses, formal wear, leather, suede, and embroidered pieces
  • Odor removal techniques — airing, steaming, and chemical deodorization methods
  • Damage assessment and repair — identifying repairable vs non-repairable damage and cost estimation
  • Solvent recovery and environmental compliance — safe handling, storage, and disposal of cleaning solvents
  • Quality assurance in dry cleaning — final checks, packaging, and documentation

Module 4: Equipment Operation & Maintenance

📚 Topics Covered

  • Industrial washing machine operation — capacity, cycle programming, water temperature, and spin settings
  • Tumble dryer operation and safety — loading capacity, temperature selection, and lint management
  • Press machines and steamers — operation, temperature control, and garment placement techniques
  • Ironing boards and hand pressing tools — proper setup, heat regulation, and technique for different fabrics
  • Dry cleaning machine basics — solvent circulation, filtration systems, and basic troubleshooting
  • Equipment maintenance schedules — daily cleaning, weekly checks, and preventive maintenance routines
  • Troubleshooting common equipment problems — water leaks, heating issues, and mechanical failures
  • Safety protocols for all equipment — emergency stops, lockout procedures, and accident prevention
  • Record keeping for equipment — maintenance logs, repair history, and performance tracking
  • Cost calculation for equipment use — determining per-garment costs and service pricing

Module 5: Customer Service & Business Operations

📚 Topics Covered

  • Customer intake and order documentation — recording garment details, special instructions, and delivery dates
  • Pricing and quotation — calculating service costs based on fabric type, garment condition, and complexity
  • Complaint handling and resolution — addressing quality issues, stain removal failures, and damage claims
  • Delivery and collection management — scheduling, packaging, and ensuring customer satisfaction
  • Hygiene and sanitation standards — maintaining clean work environment and preventing cross-contamination
  • Record management — maintaining customer files, order tracking, and payment records
  • Building customer loyalty — follow-up communication, quality consistency, and value-added services
  • Introduction to laundry business setup — home-based operations, small shop requirements, and licensing in India

Module 6: Practical Integration & Certification Preparation

📚 Topics Covered

  • Practical application of stain removal — hands-on practice with various stain types on different fabric samples
  • Complete washing cycle execution — selecting appropriate cycle, loading garments, and monitoring results
  • Pressing and finishing demonstration — pressing different garment types with proper technique and care
  • Quality inspection checklist — systematic checking of cleanliness, color, damage, and presentation
  • Mock assessment practice — simulating exam conditions with sample garments and evaluation criteria
  • Time management in laundry operations — processing multiple garments efficiently without compromising quality
  • Problem-solving scenarios — responding to challenging stains, fabric damage, and customer complaints
  • Certification exam preparation — review of key concepts, practical skills, and confidence building for final assessment

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior experience required
  • Valid identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Passport) required at registration
  • Physical fitness to stand for extended periods and handle equipment
  • Already working in laundry sector? Apply for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) exam directly
  • Open for all genders and all Indian citizens
  • Candidates with disabilities can apply with reasonable accommodations

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and classify different fabric types and understand their care requirements based on fiber content and construction
  • Perform effective stain removal using appropriate pre-treatment methods and chemical agents for various stain types
  • Operate industrial washing machines, dryers, and pressing equipment safely and efficiently following manufacturer guidelines
  • Execute complete dry cleaning operations including solvent selection, garment processing, and finishing techniques
  • Maintain quality standards through systematic inspection, defect identification, and corrective action implementation
  • Provide excellent customer service including order documentation, complaint resolution, and delivery management
  • Apply health, safety, and environmental best practices in laundry operations including PPE usage and waste management
  • Calculate service costs, manage inventory, and perform basic business operations for laundry enterprises
