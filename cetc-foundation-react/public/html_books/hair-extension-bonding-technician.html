Hair Extension & Bonding Technician

📂 Sector: Advanced Beauty, Cosmetology & Aesthetics 📊 Level: Intermediate ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months 🕐 Hours: 120 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1200
Master the art and science of hair extensions and bonding techniques to become a highly sought-after specialist in the Indian beauty industry. This comprehensive course covers all major extension methods, from tape-in and clip-in to fusion and micro-bonding, equipping you with skills that can generate ₹50,000+ monthly income as a freelancer or salon professional.

Module 1: Hair Extension Fundamentals & Hair Science

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to hair extension industry in India — market size, demand, career opportunities
  • Hair structure and anatomy — cuticle, cortex, medulla, and how extensions bond
  • Hair classification — texture types (straight, wavy, curly, coily), density grades, and quality assessment
  • Hair sourcing and origins — Remy vs non-Remy hair, Indian temple hair, synthetic vs human hair
  • Hair condition assessment — porosity, elasticity, breakage detection, and suitability for extensions
  • Common hair problems — damage, color-treated hair, fine hair, and extension compatibility
  • Safety and hygiene standards for hair extension work — infection prevention, sterilization
  • Client consultation framework — assessing scalp health, hair loss, allergies, and realistic expectations
  • Legal and ethical considerations — client agreements, liability, product warranties

Module 2: Tape-In & Clip-In Extension Techniques

📚 Topics Covered

  • Tape-in extensions overview — advantages, limitations, cost-benefit analysis for Indian market
  • Tape-in application process — sectioning, placement, direction, spacing, and pressure application
  • Tape-in removal and reapplication — safe removal techniques, adhesive residue management, hair safety
  • Maintenance protocols for tape-in extensions — washing, conditioning, styling, sleep care
  • Clip-in extensions fundamentals — types, weights, quality indicators, and durability
  • Clip-in application techniques — proper placement, weight distribution, and security checks
  • Styling and blending clip-in extensions — seamless integration, color matching, texture blending
  • Common issues with tape-in and clip-in — slipping, matting, breakage, and troubleshooting
  • Product recommendations — adhesives, removers, shampoos, and conditioners suitable for Indian climate
  • Pricing strategy for tape-in and clip-in services in Indian salons — cost calculation, profit margins

Module 3: Fusion & Micro-Bonding Extension Methods

📚 Topics Covered

  • Fusion extensions overview — keratin fusion, ultrasonic fusion, and application benefits
  • Pre-bonded fusion extension application — sectioning protocol, heat application, timing, pressure
  • Keratin-based fusion process — temperature control, bonding strength, cooling time, safety precautions
  • Micro-bonding technique — bead placement, sizing, application tools, and precision methods
  • Micro-loop extensions — installation process, weight considerations, and suitability for different hair types
  • Fusion extension maintenance — washing techniques, product restrictions, heat styling safety
  • Fusion extension removal — dissolution methods, heat removal, chemical removal, and hair protection
  • Troubleshooting fusion bonds — slipping bonds, premature shedding, breakage, and correction methods
  • Cost analysis and pricing for fusion services — material costs, time investment, pricing in Indian markets
  • Safety protocols — heat management, ventilation requirements, protective equipment, and health considerations
  • Comparing fusion methods — durability, comfort, cost, and suitability for Indian climate and hair types
  • Advanced fusion variations — pre-bonded vs hand-tied, wefted vs individual strands

Module 4: Hand-Tied Weft & Sew-In Extension Techniques

📚 Topics Covered

  • Hand-tied weft extensions overview — benefits, durability, and premium positioning in Indian market
  • Weft construction and quality assessment — knots, density, weight distribution, and durability indicators
  • Braiding techniques for sew-in preparation — cornrow braiding, braid pattern design, tension management
  • Hand-tied weft application — sewing methods, thread selection, knot security, and placement precision
  • Closure and frontal installation — seamless blending, hairline protection, and natural appearance
  • Maintenance of sew-in extensions — washing, conditioning, drying, and longevity care
  • Sew-in removal process — careful unstitching, hair protection, scalp care, and aftercare
  • Troubleshooting sew-in issues — loose stitches, braid tension problems, breakage, and repositioning
  • Pricing and business strategy for premium sew-in services in India
  • Client communication for long-term sew-in maintenance — restitching schedules, scalp health monitoring

Module 5: Styling, Blending & Client Management

📚 Topics Covered

  • Color matching and blending techniques — undertone assessment, shade selection, highlighting, and lowlighting
  • Texture blending — curly with straight, wavy with coily, and seamless integration strategies
  • Cutting and styling extensions — layering techniques, point cutting, blending with natural hair, safety measures
  • Heat styling for extensions — blow-drying, straightening, curling, and heat damage prevention
  • Protective styling — updos, braids, and styles that protect extensions and natural hair
  • Client consultation and expectation management — realistic timelines, maintenance requirements, cost transparency
  • Before-and-after documentation — photography techniques, client consent, portfolio building
  • Handling difficult clients — managing expectations, addressing complaints, refund policies
  • Building client loyalty — follow-up care, maintenance reminders, referral programs, retention strategies
  • Social media marketing for extension services — Instagram portfolio, before-and-afters, testimonials, and engagement
  • Upselling and cross-selling — suggesting complementary services, product recommendations, package deals

Module 6: Business Management, Entrepreneurship & Professional Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Starting a hair extension business in India — registration, licensing, GST compliance, and legal requirements
  • Salon setup requirements — space planning, equipment investment, hygiene standards, and sanitation protocols
  • Pricing models for Indian markets — service rates across metros (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Pune), tier-2 cities, and towns
  • Cost structure analysis — material costs, labor, overhead, rent, and profit margin calculation
  • Inventory management — stock tracking, supplier relationships, bulk purchasing, and waste management
  • Equipment and tools investment — essential tools, quality indicators, maintenance, and replacement schedules
  • Product selection and supplier management — identifying reliable suppliers, quality checks, and vendor relationships
  • Financial management — bookkeeping, tax filing, expense tracking, and profitability analysis
  • Marketing strategies for extension services — local advertising, referral programs, partnerships with bridal studios
  • Building a brand identity — logo design, salon branding, online presence, and professional reputation
  • Customer relationship management — appointment scheduling, follow-ups, feedback collection, and loyalty programs
  • Continuing education and skill updates — staying current with new techniques, attending workshops, and professional networking

  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior beauty industry experience required

  • Applicable for all candidates regardless of gender, caste, or religion
  • No upper age limit — career changers welcome
