Logo & Brand Identity Design

📂 Sector: Media, Photography & Content Creation 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months 🕐 Hours: 120 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the art of creating memorable logos and complete brand identity systems that make businesses stand out. This foundation course teaches you design principles, software skills, and industry practices to launch a career in branding and visual identity design across India's growing creative industry.

Module 1: Design Fundamentals & Brand Identity Basics

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to branding and visual identity — What is branding, importance in Indian market, types of brands (corporate, startup, NGO, retail)
  • Design principles: Balance, contrast, emphasis, movement, pattern, unity, proportion — How these apply to logo and brand design
  • Color theory fundamentals — Color psychology, color harmony, cultural significance of colors in Indian context (red, saffron, green meanings)
  • Typography basics — Font families, serif vs sans-serif, font pairing, readability principles for Hindi and English
  • Shape and form in design — Geometric shapes, organic forms, symbolism, how shapes communicate meaning
  • Visual hierarchy and composition — Arranging elements for impact, focal points, rule of thirds application
  • Brand strategy introduction — Target audience, brand personality, positioning, competitive analysis
  • Logo types and classifications — Wordmarks, lettermarks, pictorial marks, abstract marks, mascots, combination marks
  • Case study analysis — Indian brands (Flipkart, Zomato, Paytm, Reliance), international brands, what makes them effective
  • Design inspiration and mood boards — Creating inspiration collections, Pinterest, design blogs, ethical inspiration gathering
  • Industry trends in 2024-2025 — Minimalism, sustainability design, inclusive design, digital-first branding
  • Professional vocabulary — Design terminology, client communication language, industry jargon in English and Hindi

Module 2: Software Skills & Digital Tools Mastery

📚 Topics Covered

  • Adobe Illustrator fundamentals — Interface, workspace setup, tools overview, preferences, file management
  • Drawing tools in Illustrator — Pen tool, pencil, brush, eraser, shape tools, bezier curves mastery
  • Working with text in Illustrator — Text tool, character formatting, paragraph styles, text on path, converting text to outlines
  • Color and fill management — Color modes (RGB, CMYK), swatches, gradients, patterns, color management for print
  • Layers and organization — Layer panels, grouping, locking, naming conventions, working with complex documents
  • Transformations and effects — Scale, rotate, reflect, skew, distort, blend modes, opacity, shadow effects
  • Adobe Photoshop basics for branding — Image preparation, color correction, creating brand mockups, exporting for web
  • Adobe InDesign for brand guidelines — Document setup, master pages, creating brand books, template design
  • Free alternatives overview — Canva Pro, Affinity Designer, Inkscape for budget-conscious freelancers
  • File formats and export — EPS, PDF, PNG, SVG, JPG, TIFF, understanding when to use which format
  • Cloud storage and collaboration — Google Drive, Dropbox, Adobe Cloud, sharing files with clients securely
  • Keyboard shortcuts and productivity — Essential shortcuts for speed, customizing workspace, time-saving techniques

Module 3: Logo Design Process & Execution

📚 Topics Covered

  • Logo design brief understanding — Client questionnaire, requirements gathering, budget discussion, timeline planning
  • Research and concept development — Competitor analysis, market research, brainstorming techniques, sketching concepts
  • Sketching and ideation — Hand sketching, rapid prototyping, 10-10-10 method, developing multiple directions
  • Digitizing sketches — Scanning, tracing, converting hand drawings to digital format using Illustrator
  • Creating wordmark logos — Typography-based design, letter spacing, custom letterforms, monogram design
  • Creating pictorial logos — Icon design, symbolism, simplification techniques, scalability testing
  • Creating abstract logos — Geometric design, negative space usage, optical balance, modern aesthetic
  • Creating mascot and character logos — Character design, personality expression, versatility across applications
  • Refinement and iteration — Client feedback incorporation, design refinement, multiple variations development
  • Scalability and versatility testing — Minimum size testing, single-color version, reverse (white on black), different backgrounds
  • Presentation to clients — Mockups, presentation decks, explaining design choices, handling feedback professionally
  • Logo variations — Horizontal, vertical, stacked, icon-only, responsive versions for different media

Module 4: Complete Brand Identity System Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Brand identity components — Logo, color palette, typography system, imagery style, icons, patterns, textures
  • Color palette creation — Primary, secondary, accent colors, accessibility compliance, WCAG standards, cultural appropriateness
  • Typography system development — Choosing typeface families, weight variations, sizing scale, line spacing guidelines
  • Brand guidelines document creation — Structure, logo usage rules, color specifications (Pantone, CMYK, RGB, HEX), typography rules
  • Business card design — Format standards (Indian size 90x50mm), layout, material considerations, printing specifications
  • Letterhead and stationery design — Professional standards, alignment, spacing, bleeds, production specifications
  • Email signature design — Web-safe fonts, responsive design, link formatting, professional appearance
  • Envelope and packaging design — Size specifications, placement of logo, regulatory compliance, material considerations
  • Social media profile design — Profile picture templates, cover images, story templates, consistency across platforms
  • Website brand application — Brand colors in web context, typography on screen, responsive design considerations
  • Presentation templates — PowerPoint, Google Slides, PDF templates maintaining brand consistency
  • Brand pattern and texture development — Creating repeating patterns, texture libraries, decorative elements, background options

Module 5: Client Management, Pitching & Portfolio Building

📚 Topics Covered

  • Client communication essentials — Email etiquette, meeting preparation, presenting ideas professionally, handling objections
  • Project management basics — Timeline creation, milestone setting, deliverable tracking, status updates, documentation
  • Pricing and quotation — Understanding market rates in India (₹5,000-50,000 per logo), value-based pricing, quote template creation
  • Contracts and agreements — Scope of work, revision limits, payment terms, intellectual property rights, dispute resolution
  • Feedback incorporation process — Constructive criticism handling, revision rounds, client satisfaction, scope creep prevention
  • Presenting design concepts — Creating compelling presentations, mockup design, storytelling, design rationale explanation
  • Building your portfolio — Case studies, before-after comparisons, process documentation, personal branding, website creation
  • Freelance platforms navigation — Fiverr, Upwork, 99designs, local platforms like Design Pickle India, profile optimization
  • Networking in design community — Design meetups in Indian cities, online communities, Instagram strategy, LinkedIn presence
  • Handling difficult clients — Setting boundaries, communication templates, conflict resolution, knowing when to walk away
  • Testimonials and reviews — Requesting client feedback, managing online reputation, case study permission, referral generation
  • Continuous learning and trends — Following design blogs, attending webinars, design inspiration sources, skill upgradation

Module 6: Real-World Projects & Industry Practice

📚 Topics Covered

  • Project 1: Startup brand identity — Complete branding for a tech startup, logo design, color palette, guidelines document (3-4 days)
  • Project 2: Local business rebranding — Redesigning existing brand for small business, maintaining recognition while modernizing (3-4 days)
  • Project 3: NGO or social enterprise branding — Creating meaningful brand for non-profit, cultural sensitivity, impact-driven design (2-3 days)
  • Project 4: Personal brand development — Creating your own logo, brand identity, portfolio website mockup (2-3 days)
  • Project 5: Competitive brand analysis — Analyzing 5 competitors, creating improvement recommendations, presenting alternatives (2 days)
  • Project 6: Brand guidelines document creation — Creating professional brand book in InDesign with all specifications (2 days)
  • Mock client presentations — Presenting work to instructors as clients, handling feedback, revising based on critique (2 days)
  • Portfolio website project — Creating showcase website (Wix, WordPress, or portfolio platform) with 4-5 best projects (3 days)
  • Industry guest lecture integration — Learning from working designers, understanding real-world challenges and solutions
  • Freelance simulation exercise — Creating profiles on freelance platforms, bidding on real projects under guidance, understanding market dynamics
  • Peer feedback sessions — Constructive criticism from classmates, learning from others' work, collaborative improvement
  • Final portfolio review and refinement — Instructor feedback, preparing portfolio for job applications or freelance launch

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass (or equivalent)
  • No prior design experience required
  • Basic computer literacy needed (able to use mouse, keyboard, navigate files)
  • Own laptop/computer for practice (Windows/Mac, minimum 4GB RAM, Adobe Creative Suite access or free alternatives)
  • Passion for design and creativity essential
  • Already working as a designer? Apply for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) exam
  • Valid ID proof required at registration (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID)
  • Applicable for all Indian citizens and registered foreign residents
  • No upper age limit, career changers welcome

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Design and create professional logos using industry-standard software (Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop) and design principles
  • Develop complete brand identity systems including color palettes, typography, and brand guidelines documents
  • Understand and apply design psychology, color theory, and visual communication principles in branding
  • Conduct client research, gather requirements, and translate business objectives into visual brand solutions
  • Present design concepts professionally to clients and incorporate feedback constructively
  • Build professional portfolio, establish freelance presence, and secure design projects in Indian market
  • Apply scalability testing and ensure logos work across all media (print, digital, merchandise)
  • Create comprehensive brand guidelines documents that communicate design standards and usage rules
  • Understand pricing, contracts, and business aspects of freelance design work in India
  • Stay updated with current design trends and continuously improve design skills and industry knowledge
