Home Appliance Repair Technician

📂 Sector: Electrical & Electronics Trades 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months 🕐 Hours: 120 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Learn to repair and maintain common household appliances like refrigerators, washing machines, water heaters, and microwave ovens. This foundation-level course equips you with practical skills to diagnose faults, replace components, and provide customer service. Start earning within 2 months with in-demand repair skills.

Module 1: Electrical Safety & Basic Electronics Fundamentals

📚 Topics Covered

  • Electrical safety rules and regulations — understanding voltage, current, and resistance; how to work safely with electricity; importance of earthing and neutral
  • AC and DC electrical systems — difference between alternating and direct current; frequency (50Hz in India); voltage standards (230V single-phase, 415V three-phase)
  • Electrical tools and equipment — safe use of multimeter, screwdrivers, pliers, wire cutters, soldering iron, and testing equipment
  • Circuit basics and components — understanding switches, fuses, circuit breakers, relays, capacitors, and resistors used in appliances
  • Reading electrical diagrams and wiring schematics — how to interpret circuit diagrams; understanding symbols used in Indian appliance manuals
  • Common electrical hazards and prevention — shock prevention, fire hazards, proper grounding, use of safety gear and PPE
  • Power supply systems in Indian homes — single-phase vs three-phase; load capacity; earthing standards as per IS codes
  • Basic troubleshooting principles — systematic approach to identifying electrical problems; using logical deduction to narrow down faults

Module 2: Refrigerator & Air Conditioner Repair

📚 Topics Covered

  • Refrigeration cycle and components — how refrigerants work; compressor, condenser, evaporator, and expansion valve functions; cooling principles
  • Refrigerator types and models — top-load, bottom-load, side-by-side, frost-free, and direct-cool models common in India; understanding IEC standards
  • Common refrigerator faults and solutions — not cooling, ice formation, leaking water, compressor not starting, noise issues, thermostat problems
  • Compressor diagnosis and replacement — identifying compressor failures; checking relay and capacitor; replacement procedure and safety precautions
  • Cooling system maintenance — cleaning condenser coils, checking refrigerant levels, identifying leaks using dye or soapy water method
  • Air conditioner fundamentals — window AC, split AC, and inverter AC systems; understanding BTU and tonnage for Indian climate zones
  • AC troubleshooting — refrigerant leaks, compressor failure, fan motor issues, thermostat malfunction, ice formation, water leakage from indoor unit
  • AC servicing and maintenance — filter cleaning, drain pipe clearing, gas refilling, capacitor testing, and seasonal maintenance checklist
  • Safety procedures for refrigerant handling — understanding CFC and HFC regulations; proper disposal of old refrigerants; certification requirements

Module 3: Washing Machine & Water Heater Repair

📚 Topics Covered

  • Washing machine types and mechanisms — front-load vs top-load; semi-automatic vs fully automatic; drum, agitator, and motor systems
  • Washing machine components — motor, pump, inlet valve, drain valve, door lock, timer, and control board; understanding Indian brand specifications
  • Common washing machine faults — not spinning, not draining, water leakage, strange noises, not filling with water, cloth damage issues
  • Motor and pump diagnosis — testing motor windings with multimeter; identifying pump blockages; bearing wear signs; seal replacement procedure
  • Inlet and drain valve repair — checking water pressure, valve replacement, solenoid testing, hose connection issues
  • Washing machine electrical control system — timer mechanism, selector switch, safety switches, thermal overload protection
  • Water heater types in India — geyser (electric), solar water heater, gas water heater; understanding capacity and wattage ratings
  • Electric geyser repair and maintenance — heating element replacement, thermostat adjustment, pressure relief valve function, rust and corrosion prevention
  • Water heater troubleshooting — not heating, leaking, slow heating, thermostat failure, element burnout, water quality issues affecting performance

Module 4: Microwave, Oven & Cooking Appliances Repair

📚 Topics Covered

  • Microwave oven working principle — magnetron tube function, high-voltage circuit, waveguide, turntable motor, and safety interlocks
  • Microwave oven components and circuits — transformer, capacitor, diode, high-voltage components; understanding Indian microwave models and wattage
  • Common microwave problems — not heating, not turning on, sparking inside, turntable not rotating, door latch issues, uneven heating
  • Microwave magnetron testing and replacement — identifying dead magnetron; safety precautions with high-voltage components; proper disposal
  • Microwave capacitor and high-voltage circuit diagnosis — testing capacitor with multimeter; identifying burnt components; circuit board inspection
  • Electric cooktop and oven repair — heating coil replacement, thermostat calibration, temperature sensor issues, control knob problems
  • Induction cooktop fundamentals — IGBT technology, coil testing, sensor malfunction, power supply issues specific to Indian brands
  • Mixer-grinder and food processor repair — motor issues, blade assembly, seal replacement, overload protection, speed control problems
  • Safety protocols for microwave and high-voltage appliances — proper discharge procedures, avoiding electric shock, component handling precautions

Module 5: Small Appliances & Electrical Components Repair

📚 Topics Covered

  • Electric fan repair and maintenance — motor winding, capacitor replacement, blade assembly, speed regulator issues, bearing lubrication
  • Water pump and motor systems — submersible pump repair, motor coupling, seal replacement, pressure switch function, dry-run protection
  • Vacuum cleaner and dust suction appliances — motor function, filter maintenance, suction loss diagnosis, hose blockage clearing, power cord issues
  • Iron and heating appliances — heating element testing, thermostat calibration, steam generator issues, water leakage prevention
  • Television and entertainment appliance basics — power supply troubleshooting, backlight issues, display problems, remote receiver function
  • Power cord and plug safety — identifying damaged cords, proper rewiring, grounding verification, fuse selection and replacement
  • Capacitor types and testing — electrolytic, ceramic, film capacitors; using multimeter for testing; identifying capacitor failure signs
  • Motor types and testing — single-phase induction motors, split-phase motors, capacitor-start motors; winding resistance measurement; bearing condition assessment
  • Component soldering and desoldering — proper soldering technique, identifying cold solder joints, component replacement on circuit boards, heat management

Module 6: Diagnostic Tools, Documentation & Professional Practice

📚 Topics Covered

  • Multimeter usage and interpretation — measuring voltage (AC/DC), current, resistance; continuity testing; reading digital and analog displays; safety while measuring
  • Clamp meter and power measurement — measuring AC current without breaking circuit; understanding power consumption calculations; energy audit basics
  • Insulation tester (megohmmeter) — testing wire insulation, identifying leakage paths, safety standards for earthing verification
  • Thermal imaging and visual inspection — identifying overheating components, recognizing burnt components, moisture damage assessment
  • Appliance manual reading and interpretation — understanding manufacturer documentation; finding spare parts information; warranty and service guidelines
  • Service record documentation — maintaining repair logs, noting replacement parts, customer feedback recording, follow-up maintenance scheduling
  • Spare parts identification and sourcing — finding genuine parts, understanding part numbers, quality vs counterfeit identification, supplier relationships
  • Customer communication and service etiquette — explaining faults in simple terms, providing cost estimates, managing customer expectations, after-sales support
  • Business basics for self-employed technicians — pricing repair services, marketing repair services locally, maintaining tools and workspace, record-keeping for taxes
  • Quality assurance and testing after repair — functional testing procedures, safety verification, warranty period documentation, customer handover checklist
  • Waste management and environmental responsibility — proper disposal of old components, refrigerant recovery procedures, e-waste handling, environmental compliance
  • Continuing education and skill updates — staying current with new appliance models, attending manufacturer training, online learning resources, certification maintenance

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years (with parental consent if below 18)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior experience required
  • Physical fitness to lift appliances and work in standing position
  • Ability to read and understand basic English/Hindi technical terms
  • Already working as appliance technician? Apply for RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) direct exam
  • Valid government ID proof required (Aadhar, Voter ID, Passport, or School Certificate)
  • Applicable for all Indian citizens and registered foreign nationals
  • No upper age limit — senior citizens welcome

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Understand electrical safety principles and safely work with electrical appliances without risk of shock or fire
  • Diagnose common faults in refrigerators, air conditioners, washing machines, and water heaters using systematic troubleshooting methods
  • Perform repairs and component replacements (compressor, motor, pump, heating element, capacitor) with confidence and precision
  • Use electrical testing tools (multimeter, clamp meter, insulation tester) accurately to identify electrical problems
  • Read and interpret electrical diagrams, wiring schematics, and manufacturer service manuals
  • Provide professional customer service, maintain repair documentation, and build a sustainable repair business or secure employment
