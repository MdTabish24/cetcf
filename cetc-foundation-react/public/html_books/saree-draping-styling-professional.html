Saree Draping & Styling Professional

📂 Sector: Fashion, Tailoring & Textiles 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 1 Month 🕐 Hours: 60 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the ancient art of saree draping with modern styling techniques. This hands-on course teaches you traditional and contemporary saree draping methods, body type styling, and professional finishing touches. Perfect for aspiring fashion consultants, wedding coordinators, and self-employed styling professionals in India.

Module 1: Saree Fundamentals & Fabric Knowledge

📚 Topics Covered

  • History and cultural significance of Indian sarees — Understanding regional variations and traditional importance of sarees across India
  • Saree anatomy and measurements — Learning pallu, border, body, blouse piece, and standard saree dimensions (5.5m length, 1.2m width)
  • Fabric types and their properties — Cotton, silk, georgette, chiffon, linen, crepe, and synthetic blends; weight, drape, and care instructions
  • Saree quality assessment — Checking weave, thread count, dye quality, and identifying authentic vs counterfeit fabrics
  • Regional saree styles overview — Kanjivaram, Banarasi, Chanderi, Tussar, Pochampalli, and contemporary designer sarees
  • Saree price ranges and market segments — Budget (₹500-2000), mid-range (₹2000-10000), premium (₹10000-50000), and luxury (₹50000+)
  • Seasonal and occasion-based saree selection — Summer, winter, wedding season, festival, office, and casual wear choices
  • Care, maintenance and storage of sarees — Washing, drying, ironing, and proper folding techniques to prevent damage

Module 2: Body Type Analysis & Personal Styling

📚 Topics Covered

  • Body type identification — Apple, pear, hourglass, rectangle, and inverted triangle shapes; measuring and categorizing clients
  • Height and proportion analysis — Petite, average, and tall body types; adjusting saree length and pallu placement accordingly
  • Skin tone and undertone assessment — Warm, cool, and neutral undertones; identifying best colors for each skin type
  • Face shape and saree styling — Round, oval, square, rectangular, and diamond faces; choosing appropriate blouse necklines and pallu drapes
  • Age-appropriate styling — Styling sarees for teenagers, young women, middle-aged, and senior women with modern and traditional options
  • Complexion and color coordination — Creating flattering color palettes; understanding contrast and saturation for different skin tones
  • Personality-based styling — Classic, trendy, bohemian, and minimalist styles; matching saree choices to client personality
  • Virtual consultation and mood boards — Creating digital styling presentations and before-after references for clients

Module 3: Traditional Saree Draping Techniques

📚 Topics Covered

  • Nivi drape (Andhra Pradesh style) — Step-by-step pleating, pallu placement over shoulder, and finishing for everyday and formal wear
  • Bengali drape — Distinctive left shoulder pallu placement, pleating style, and aanchal arrangement for traditional look
  • Gujarati drape — Front-facing style with decorative pleats, side pallu placement, and traditional jewelry coordination
  • Marathi drape (Nauvari) — Nine-yard saree technique, dhoti-style lower portion, and pallu arrangement for traditional occasions
  • Maharashtrian Paithani drape — Specific pleating and pallu positioning with traditional border display for festive wear
  • Tamil Kanjeevaram drape — Formal temple and wedding style with prominent border display and traditional aanchal placement
  • Kashmiri and North Indian drapes — Dupatta-style variations and contemporary folds for formal and wedding functions
  • Fusion and modern drapes — Draped, wrapped, and asymmetrical styles; saree-gown hybrids and contemporary western fusion

Module 4: Occasion-Based Styling & Accessorizing

📚 Topics Covered

  • Wedding and bridal saree styling — Choosing heavy fabrics, embellished designs, color psychology; coordinating jewelry and makeup
  • Festival and celebration styling — Diwali, Holi, Durga Puja, Navratri; color trends, traditional patterns, and regional preferences
  • Office and professional saree styling — Lightweight fabrics, neutral colors, minimal jewelry; creating corporate-appropriate looks
  • Casual and everyday styling — Cotton sarees, simple drapes, comfortable styling for daily wear and social gatherings
  • Party and evening wear styling — Silk, georgette, and designer sarees; glamorous draping and statement jewelry pairing
  • Jewelry coordination — Necklaces, earrings, bangles, bindis, and traditional ornaments; matching metals and stone colors
  • Makeup and hair styling for sarees — Hairstyles (bun, braids, side-swept), makeup intensity, and bindi placement for different occasions
  • Footwear selection — Traditional juttis, modern heels, flats, and ethnic shoes; matching footwear to saree style and occasion

Module 5: Advanced Styling & Professional Services

📚 Topics Covered

  • Blouse design and customization — Selecting necklines (V-neck, boat, halter, backless), sleeve styles, and embroidery matching saree
  • Petticoat selection and fitting — Choosing correct length, fabric, and color; adjusting for different body types and drape styles
  • Saree length and hemming — Calculating correct length, marking, and basic hemming techniques; adjusting for heels vs flat shoes
  • Pallu styling and securing — Using safety pins, hooks, and brooches; creating decorative pleats and preventing slipping during wear
  • Layering and draping with dupattas — Adding additional fabrics for modesty, style, and traditional looks; managing multiple layers
  • Styling for different seasons — Summer fabrics and light colors, winter heavy silks, monsoon-friendly materials and colors
  • Bridal party coordination — Styling multiple family members; color coordination and complementary designs for wedding events
  • Portfolio development and client documentation — Taking styling photos, maintaining client records, and building professional portfolio

Module 6: Professional Practice & Business Skills

📚 Topics Covered

  • Client consultation and communication — Taking measurements, understanding preferences, budget, and occasion requirements professionally
  • Styling session management — Time management, preparing workstation, handling multiple clients, and maintaining hygiene standards
  • Pricing and service packages — Offering individual styling (₹500-2000), event styling (₹2000-5000), and wedding packages (₹5000-15000)
  • Building client base and reputation — Word-of-mouth marketing, social media presence, and creating referral networks in local community
  • Safety and hygiene practices — Sanitizing hands and tools, preventing skin infections, and maintaining professional environment
  • Conflict resolution and customer satisfaction — Handling difficult clients, managing expectations, and ensuring repeat business
  • Business registration and GST compliance — Understanding MSME registration, maintaining records, and tax filing for self-employed professionals
  • Building online presence — Instagram portfolio, WhatsApp business, local listing optimization, and online booking systems for styling services

  • Create complete styled looks for different occasions with coordinated accessories, jewelry, and makeup suggestions
  • Provide professional styling consultations with proper client communication and documentation
  • Establish and manage a professional saree styling business with proper pricing, branding, and customer service
