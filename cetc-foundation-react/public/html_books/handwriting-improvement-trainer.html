✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years (16 years recommended)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass (Class 10th preferred)
  • No prior handwriting training experience required
  • Basic English and Hindi reading-writing ability required
  • Already working as teacher or trainer? Apply for RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid government ID proof required at time of registration (Aadhaar, PAN, or Voter ID)
  • Medical fitness: No severe hand/finger disability (assessment done during admission)
  • Applicable for all candidates including school/college students, homemakers, and working professionals
