Nail Art & Extension Technician

📂 Sector: Beauty & Lifestyle 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months 🕐 Hours: 120 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the art and science of nail care, design, and extensions in just 8 weeks. This hands-on certification program trains you to become a professional Nail Art & Extension Technician, ready to work in salons, spas, or start your own beauty business across India. Learn modern techniques, trending designs, and client management skills demanded by the booming Indian beauty industry.

Module 1: Nail Anatomy, Health & Sanitation

📚 Topics Covered

  • Nail anatomy and structure — Understanding nail plate, nail bed, cuticle, and surrounding tissues
  • Nail growth cycle and health indicators — Normal growth rate, healthy vs unhealthy nails, common nail conditions
  • Nail disorders and infections — Onychomycosis, psoriasis, brittle nails, nail discoloration, when to refer clients to dermatologists
  • Personal hygiene and professional appearance — Dress code, grooming standards, hand care for technicians
  • Salon sanitation and sterilization — Autoclave use, chemical disinfectants, tool cleaning protocols, workspace setup
  • Client consultation and health screening — Patch tests, allergy identification, contraindications for nail services
  • Safety equipment and PPE — Gloves, masks, aprons, ventilation requirements, chemical safety handling
  • Salon licensing and legal requirements — GST registration, salon permits, insurance, record-keeping for Indian salons

Module 2: Basic Manicure & Pedicure Services

📚 Topics Covered

  • Manicure process step-by-step — Soaking, cuticle work, filing, buffing, moisturizing, polish application
  • Pedicure process and foot care — Foot soak, callus removal, nail care, massage techniques, polish application
  • Nail filing techniques — Straight cut, round, oval, square, almond shapes; correct angle and pressure
  • Cuticle management — Pushing back vs removal, cuticle oil application, safe handling of cuticle tools
  • Hand and foot massage basics — Pressure points, circulation benefits, relaxation techniques for clients
  • Nail polish application techniques — Base coat, color application, top coat, preventing bubbles and streaks
  • French manicure and ombre designs — Traditional and modern variations, application methods
  • Color theory and skin tone matching — Choosing polish colors based on Indian skin tones, seasonal trends
  • Client communication and comfort — Managing expectations, handling nervous clients, timing management
  • Pricing and service packages — Standard rates in Indian market, combo packages, service documentation

Module 3: Nail Art Design Fundamentals

📚 Topics Covered

  • Nail art tools and materials — Brushes, dotting tools, stamping plates, nail stickers, rhinestones, foils, glitter
  • Freehand nail art basics — Brush strokes, line work, simple patterns, flower designs, geometric shapes
  • Stamping technique — Choosing plates, ink application, transfer methods, design variations, troubleshooting
  • Dotting and stippling designs — Creating dots of various sizes, mandala patterns, floral arrangements
  • Gradient and ombre nail art — Color blending techniques, sponge application, watercolor effects
  • Decal and sticker application — Placement techniques, adhesion methods, sealing with top coat
  • Rhinestone and embellishment placement — Crystal application, gold foil accents, 3D nail art basics
  • Trending designs in Indian market — Bridal nail art, festival designs, Bollywood-inspired patterns, minimalist trends
  • Seasonal and occasion-based designs — Wedding nails, festival designs, casual wear, party nails
  • Design portfolio and customer reference book — Building your design collection, photography tips, client portfolio management
  • Time management for nail art — Efficiency without compromising quality, production speed, batch processing

Module 4: Acrylic Nail Extensions

📚 Topics Covered

  • Acrylic chemistry and materials — Monomer and polymer chemistry, liquid-to-powder ratio, product selection
  • Acrylic application techniques — Bead placement, shaping, blending, creating proper apex and arch
  • Nail tip selection and sizing — Tip sizing chart, full-cover vs half-cover tips, custom sizing for Indian nail beds
  • Acrylic over tips vs full-set application — Differences, advantages, client suitability, application steps
  • Nail shape and design creation — Square, round, coffin, almond, stiletto, ballerina shapes using acrylics
  • Acrylic art and embellishments — Applying designs on acrylic, rhinestone placement, chrome powder application
  • Maintenance and filling — Growth management, fill-in appointments, timeline for fills, cost calculation
  • Acrylic removal process — Safe removal techniques, client education on damage prevention, post-removal care
  • Troubleshooting common issues — Lifting, cracking, yellowing, thickness problems, bubble formation
  • Product chemistry and brands — Indian market products, quality comparison, cost-effective options, supplier relationships
  • Health and safety with acrylics — Fume management, ventilation requirements, respiratory protection, chemical storage

Module 5: Gel Nail Extensions & UV/LED Technology

📚 Topics Covered

  • Gel chemistry and formulations — UV vs LED gels, hard gels vs soft gels, glossy vs matte finishes
  • UV and LED lamp technology — Lamp types, wattage, curing times, lamp maintenance, safety precautions
  • Gel base coat and primer application — Nail dehydration, primer use, proper base coat thickness
  • Gel color application and curing — Thin layers, curing times, preventing shrinkage, color accuracy
  • Gel top coat and finishes — High gloss, matte, glitter, chrome finishes, proper sealing
  • Gel extension tips and overlays — Tip application, overlay on natural nails, length and shape options
  • Gel nail art techniques — Gel painting, stamping on gel, embellishments, reverse stamping, marbling effects
  • Gel removal and aftercare — Acetone soaking, electric file removal, cuticle care post-removal
  • LED lamp safety and UV exposure — Sunscreen use, lamp safety standards, long-term exposure concerns
  • Gel vs acrylic comparison — Cost, durability, appearance, client preference, application time
  • Popular gel brands in India — Local and international brands, pricing, availability, quality assessment

Module 6: Professional Practice, Business Skills & Career Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Professional client communication — Consultation techniques, listening skills, managing difficult clients, setting boundaries
  • Building client relationships — Retention strategies, loyalty programs, referral systems, follow-up communication
  • Salon hygiene standards and compliance — Daily cleaning schedules, disinfection protocols, record maintenance, health department compliance
  • Appointment management and scheduling — Booking systems, time estimation, managing no-shows, cancellation policies
  • Pricing strategy and cost calculation — Material cost, labor cost, profit margin, competitive pricing in Indian market
  • Business documentation and billing — Invoice creation, GST compliance, payment methods, financial record-keeping
  • Self-employment and salon setup — Registering as self-employed, salon space requirements, equipment investment, licensing
  • Marketing and social media presence — Instagram content creation, before-after photography, hashtag strategies, local marketing
  • Building a nail art portfolio — Photography techniques, portfolio presentation, client testimonials, online presence
  • Continuing education and trend awareness — Fashion weeks, nail art competitions, online courses, staying updated with global trends
  • Health and wellness for technicians — Posture and ergonomics, repetitive strain prevention, mental health, work-life balance
  • Entrepreneurship and franchise opportunities — Starting your own salon, franchise models in India, investment requirements, scaling business

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior experience needed - open to all candidates
  • Already working as nail technician? Apply for RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) direct exam
  • Valid photo ID proof required at registration (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID, Passport, Driving License)
  • Medical fitness certificate recommended (especially for those with respiratory sensitivity)
  • Applicable for all candidates across India, both male and female

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Understand nail anatomy, health conditions, and when to refer clients to medical professionals
  • Perform professional manicure and pedicure services following Indian salon standards and hygiene protocols
  • Create diverse nail art designs using freehand, stamping, and embellishment techniques trending in Indian market
  • Apply acrylic extensions with proper technique, shaping, and design customization for various client preferences
  • Apply gel extensions using UV/LED technology with correct curing and finishing for durable, professional results
  • Manage client relationships, maintain salon hygiene, handle business operations, and build a successful nail care career as employee or entrepreneur
