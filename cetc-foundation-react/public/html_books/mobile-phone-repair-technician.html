Mobile Phone Repair Technician

📂 Sector: Electrical & Electronics Trades 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months 🕐 Hours: 120 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the essential skills to repair and maintain mobile phones professionally. This foundation course equips you with hands-on knowledge of smartphone hardware, software troubleshooting, and common repair techniques used in Indian mobile service centers. Start your career in one of India's fastest-growing electronics repair sectors.

Module 1: Mobile Phone Fundamentals & Safety Practices

📚 Topics Covered

  • Mobile phone evolution and types — History from 2G to 5G smartphones, different phone categories (budget, mid-range, flagship)
  • Basic mobile phone anatomy — Understanding motherboard, display, battery, charging port, antenna, and internal components
  • Safety protocols in mobile repair — Electrical safety, electrostatic discharge (ESD) prevention, tool safety, and workspace organization
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE) — Anti-static wrist bands, gloves, safety glasses, and proper handling techniques
  • Common hazards and prevention — Battery explosion risks, chemical exposure, electrical shocks, and safe disposal methods
  • Workplace setup and organization — Proper workbench arrangement, tool storage, component management, and cleanliness standards
  • Mobile phone operating systems overview — Android architecture, iOS basics, and differences between systems
  • Introduction to mobile repair tools — Screwdrivers, opening tools, tweezers, multimeters, and diagnostic equipment

Module 2: Mobile Phone Hardware Components & Diagnostics

📚 Topics Covered

  • Display technology and types — LCD, AMOLED, IPS screens, resolution standards, and common display issues in Indian market phones
  • Battery technology and specifications — Lithium-ion, capacity measurement (mAh), voltage ratings, and battery health assessment
  • Charging systems and ports — USB Type-C, Micro USB, Lightning connectors, wireless charging, and fast charging technologies
  • Processor and RAM function — Understanding CPU, GPU, RAM types (LPDDR), and their role in phone performance
  • Storage systems — Internal storage types (eMMC, UFS), microSD cards, and storage-related problems
  • Camera modules and sensors — Front and rear camera components, image sensors, and common camera malfunctions
  • Audio systems — Speaker types, microphone placement, audio jack components, and sound-related issues
  • Diagnostic tools and testing — Multimeters, thermal imaging, battery testers, and software diagnostic apps
  • Using diagnostic software — AIDA64, CPU-Z, phone manufacturer diagnostic tools, and interpreting test results
  • Identifying hardware faults — Visual inspection techniques, fault detection methods, and creating repair estimates
  • Component-level identification — Reading circuit board labels, understanding component placement, and recognizing IC chips
  • Power delivery systems — Understanding voltage regulation, power distribution on motherboards, and short circuit detection

Module 3: Screen Replacement & Display Repairs

📚 Topics Covered

  • Display disassembly techniques — Safe screen removal using heat guns, opening tools, and adhesive solvents
  • Screen glass removal and replacement — Separating digitizer from LCD, handling fragile components, and avoiding further damage
  • LCD panel repair procedures — Checking for internal damage, cleaning LCD surfaces, and replacing faulty panels
  • Touch screen calibration — Post-replacement calibration, sensitivity adjustments, and testing touch response
  • Screen adhesive application — Using proper adhesives (B7000, LOCA), applying even pressure, and curing times
  • Common display problems — Dead pixels, color bleeding, unresponsive touch, and ghost touches
  • Waterproofing after screen replacement — Proper sealing techniques, gasket placement, and testing water resistance
  • Popular phone models screen replacement — Samsung Galaxy series, Redmi, Realme, OnePlus, and iPhone screen replacement procedures
  • Screen quality assessment — Identifying original vs. refurbished screens, quality testing, and customer communication
  • Troubleshooting display issues — Brightness problems, flickering screens, color distortion, and connector issues
  • Curved and notch display handling — Special techniques for curved edges, notch displays, and edge-to-edge screens
  • Cost estimation for screen repairs — Understanding pricing in Indian market, supplier relationships, and profit margins

Module 4: Battery, Charging & Power System Repairs

📚 Topics Covered

  • Battery removal and installation — Proper battery extraction, handling precautions, and safe installation procedures
  • Battery health assessment — Using battery diagnostic apps, understanding battery health percentage, and degradation signs
  • Charging port repair and replacement — Cleaning corroded ports, micro-soldering damaged ports, and replacing USB connectors
  • Charging cable issues — Identifying faulty cables, testing with multimeters, and understanding charging standards
  • Power button repair — Cleaning, replacement, and troubleshooting power-related issues
  • Battery swelling and explosion prevention — Identifying swollen batteries, safe removal procedures, and disposal methods
  • Wireless charging troubleshooting — Coil testing, alignment issues, and foreign object detection
  • Fast charging problems — Recognizing incompatible chargers, voltage regulation issues, and device temperature management
  • Battery replacement for popular models — Samsung, Xiaomi, Oppo, Vivo, Apple, and other Indian market phones
  • Power management IC (PMIC) understanding — Role of power management chips, common failures, and heat-related issues
  • Thermal management — Heat dissipation techniques, thermal paste application, and preventing overheating
  • Testing charging systems — Using multimeters, testing voltage output, and current measurement for safety verification

Module 5: Software Troubleshooting & Mobile OS Management

📚 Topics Covered

  • Android operating system basics — File structure, system partitions, and boot process understanding
  • iOS operating system basics — iOS architecture, file management, and iOS-specific features
  • Bootloader and recovery mode — Accessing recovery, fastboot mode, and safe mode on different phones
  • Factory reset procedures — Complete data wipe, factory reset methods, and data backup importance
  • ROM flashing and custom ROMs — Understanding ROM files, flashing procedures, and risks of custom ROMs
  • Stock ROM installation — Finding official ROMs, flashing stock firmware, and reverting from custom ROMs
  • Common software issues — Freezing, lagging, app crashes, and system errors troubleshooting
  • Malware and virus removal — Identifying infected devices, removing malware safely, and preventing infections
  • Software update procedures — System updates, security patches, and update-related issues
  • Backup and data recovery — Cloud backups, local backups, and data recovery from damaged devices
  • Performance optimization — Clearing cache, managing storage, disabling unnecessary apps, and RAM management
  • IMEI and software authentication — Understanding IMEI numbers, device verification, and avoiding counterfeit devices

Module 6: Business Operations & Customer Service Excellence

📚 Topics Covered

  • Mobile repair shop setup — Workspace design, tool investment, spare parts sourcing, and initial capital requirements in India
  • Supplier relationships and sourcing — Finding reliable spare parts suppliers, quality verification, and cost management
  • Inventory management — Tracking spare parts, managing stock levels, and reducing wastage
  • Pricing strategies — Cost calculation, competitive pricing in Indian markets, and profit margin management
  • Customer communication skills — Explaining technical issues in simple language, managing expectations, and building trust
  • Warranty and after-sales service — Providing warranties, handling complaints, and maintaining customer relationships
  • Documentation and record keeping — Service records, warranty cards, customer data management, and GST compliance
  • Diagnostic and repair estimates — Creating accurate estimates, communicating delays, and transparent pricing
  • Quality assurance and testing — Final testing procedures, ensuring repairs work properly, and customer satisfaction
  • Handling difficult customers — Problem resolution, refund policies, and maintaining professionalism
  • Building reputation online — Google reviews, social media presence, and word-of-mouth marketing
  • Legal and compliance requirements — GST registration, warranty laws in India, consumer protection, and insurance considerations
  • Time management and efficiency — Scheduling repairs, managing workload, and meeting customer deadlines

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years (or as per local regulations)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior mobile repair experience required
  • Already working as mobile technician? Apply for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) direct exam
  • Valid photo ID proof required (Aadhaar, Voter ID, Passport, or PAN)
  • Open for all Indian citizens and foreign nationals with valid visa
  • Medical fitness: No serious hand tremors or vision problems that prevent detailed work
  • Basic English/Hindi language understanding for safety and technical documentation

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and understand all major mobile phone components and their functions correctly
  • Perform safe disassembly and reassembly of mobile phones following proper procedures
  • Diagnose hardware faults using appropriate tools and diagnostic methods accurately
  • Execute screen replacement, battery replacement, and charging port repairs professionally
  • Troubleshoot software issues and manage mobile operating systems effectively
  • Provide excellent customer service and maintain proper repair documentation and records
  • Apply safety protocols to prevent electrical hazards and component damage consistently
  • Create accurate repair estimates and manage mobile repair shop operations efficiently
