Skin Care & Facial Therapist

📂 Sector: Beauty & Lifestyle 📊 Level: Intermediate ⏱️ Duration: 4 Months 🕐 Hours: 240 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the art and science of professional skin care and facial treatments. This intermediate certification program trains you to become a skilled facial therapist capable of providing advanced skincare services, performing facials, and managing client relationships in salons, spas, and wellness centers across India.

Module 1: Skin Anatomy, Types & Conditions

📚 Topics Covered

  • Layers of skin (epidermis, dermis, hypodermis) — understand skin structure and functions
  • Skin types classification (oily, dry, combination, sensitive, normal) — identify and assess client skin
  • Common skin conditions (acne, eczema, psoriasis, rosacea, hyperpigmentation) — recognize problems
  • Skin pH balance and importance — understand acid mantle protection
  • Age-related skin changes — learn aging process and prevention
  • Skin sensitivity and allergic reactions — identify triggers and contraindications
  • Hair growth cycles and facial hair — understand threading and hair removal basics
  • Skin care for different climates — adapt routines for Indian weather conditions
  • Seasonal skin changes in India — manage dry winters and humid summers
  • Skin barrier function and repair — maintain healthy skin integrity

Module 2: Professional Facial Treatment Techniques

📚 Topics Covered

  • Basic facial procedure steps (cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, massage, mask, moisturizing) — perform complete facial
  • Facial massage techniques and pressure points — improve circulation and relaxation
  • European facial treatment protocols — follow international standards
  • HydraFacial and microdermabrasion basics — understand advanced treatments
  • Chemical peels (AHA, BHA, TCA) — safe application and post-care
  • Threading technique for eyebrows and upper lip — precision hair removal
  • Facial steam and extraction methods — deep cleansing procedures
  • Face packs and masks application (clay, gel, cream masks) — treatment variations
  • Anti-aging facial treatments — collagen and elastin boost techniques
  • Brightening and whitening facials for Indian skin tones — specialized treatments
  • Post-facial care instructions — client guidance for results
  • Contraindications and when to refer clients — safety awareness

Module 3: Skincare Products & Ingredients Knowledge

📚 Topics Covered

  • Active ingredients (retinol, vitamin C, hyaluronic acid, niacinamide) — benefits and applications
  • Natural ingredients (aloe vera, turmeric, neem, rose, charcoal) — Indian traditional skincare
  • Sunscreen types and SPF ratings — UV protection importance
  • Moisturizers and serums selection — hydration solutions
  • Cleansers (gel, foam, oil-based) — appropriate selection by skin type
  • Toners and essences — balancing skin pH
  • Face oils and their benefits — nourishing treatments
  • Exfoliants (physical and chemical) — safe usage guidelines
  • Anti-acne products (benzoyl peroxide, salicylic acid) — acne management
  • Brightening agents and melanin inhibitors — dealing with hyperpigmentation
  • Product shelf life and storage — maintain product quality
  • Reading product labels and certifications — understand FSSAI and dermatologist approval

Module 4: Client Consultation & Skin Analysis

📚 Topics Covered

  • Client intake forms and questionnaires — gather complete information
  • Skin analysis using magnifying lamp — assess skin condition accurately
  • Patch test procedures — check for allergies before treatment
  • Consultation communication skills — listen actively and build trust
  • Identifying skin concerns and goals — understand client expectations
  • Medical history assessment — recognize contraindications
  • Photography and documentation — track client progress
  • Recommending treatment plans — customize services
  • Managing difficult clients professionally — handle complaints gracefully
  • Follow-up and retention strategies — build long-term relationships

Module 5: Hygiene, Safety & Professional Standards

📚 Topics Covered

  • Salon hygiene standards and cleanliness protocols — prevent infections
  • Sterilization and disinfection methods — sanitize tools properly
  • Personal hygiene for therapists — maintain professional appearance
  • Infection control and cross-contamination prevention — safety measures
  • Handling and disposal of waste materials — environmental responsibility
  • First aid for skin reactions — manage emergencies
  • Contra-indications and when to refuse service — know your limits
  • Professional ethics and client confidentiality — maintain trust
  • FSSAI and beauty industry regulations in India — legal compliance
  • Insurance and liability awareness — protect your business
  • Workplace safety and ergonomics — prevent repetitive strain injuries
  • Chemical safety and handling hazardous materials — proper storage

Module 6: Business Skills & Career Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Starting a beauty business in India — registration and licensing requirements
  • Pricing strategies and service packages — competitive rates
  • Client management and appointment scheduling — CRM basics
  • Marketing and social media for beauty services — Instagram and Facebook strategies
  • Building a professional portfolio — showcase your work
  • Customer service excellence — create loyal clients
  • Managing finances and invoicing — basic bookkeeping
  • Professional development and continuing education — stay updated with trends
  • Networking in beauty industry — build connections
  • Online presence and booking platforms (Practo, Urban Company model) — digital presence

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years (16 years recommended)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior experience needed - beginners welcome
  • Already working as a beauty therapist? Apply for direct RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid ID proof required at registration (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID, Passport)
  • Physical fitness required - standing for long hours during practical sessions
  • Applicable for all candidates irrespective of gender, caste, religion, or background

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Understand skin anatomy, types, and conditions to assess client needs accurately
  • Perform professional facial treatments including cleansing, massage, extraction, and mask application with proper technique
  • Apply knowledge of skincare ingredients and products to recommend suitable treatments for different skin types
  • Conduct professional client consultations using intake forms, skin analysis, and patch testing
  • Maintain highest standards of hygiene, sterilization, and infection control in salon environment
  • Build client relationships, manage appointments, and develop business skills for self-employment or salon employment
