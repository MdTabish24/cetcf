Podcast Production Basics

📂 Sector: Media, Photography & Content Creation 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 1 Month 🕐 Hours: 60 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the complete process of podcast production from concept to distribution. This foundation course teaches you how to plan, record, edit, and publish professional podcasts using industry-standard tools and techniques. Perfect for aspiring content creators, journalists, and entrepreneurs looking to build their audio presence in India's rapidly growing podcast market.

Module 1: Podcast Fundamentals & Industry Overview

📚 Topics Covered

  • What is podcasting? — Definition, history, and evolution of podcasts in India and globally
  • Types of podcasts — Solo shows, co-hosted shows, narrative fiction, interview-based, educational, and niche genre podcasts
  • Indian podcast market landscape — Growth statistics, popular Indian podcasts, listener demographics, and market opportunities
  • Podcast vs other audio content — Differences between podcasts, audiobooks, radio shows, and YouTube videos
  • Career opportunities in podcasting — Jobs as podcast producer, editor, host, sound engineer, and podcast manager in India
  • Equipment overview — Basic understanding of microphones, headphones, audio interfaces, and recording software
  • Understanding podcast formats — Episode structure, show format, series planning, and content pillars
  • Audience analysis — How to identify target listeners, understand listener behavior, and build listener personas
  • Podcast platforms in India — Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, JioSaavn, Gaana, and local platforms
  • Monetization models — Sponsorships, affiliate marketing, listener support, premium content, and advertising rates in India

Module 2: Audio Recording & Technical Setup

📚 Topics Covered

  • Microphone types & selection — Dynamic vs condenser microphones, USB microphones, lavalier mics, and budget options under ₹5000
  • Setting up recording space — Home studio basics, soundproofing with affordable materials, room acoustics, and noise reduction
  • Audio interface fundamentals — What is an audio interface, why it matters, popular affordable options in India
  • Headphones & monitoring — Importance of good headphones, closed-back vs open-back, monitoring during recording
  • Recording software setup — Introduction to Audacity (free), GarageBand, Adobe Audition, and other DAWs available in India
  • Microphone technique & positioning — Proper mic placement, distance from mouth, pop filter usage, and avoiding handling noise
  • Levels & gain staging — Understanding decibels, setting proper input levels, avoiding clipping and distortion
  • Recording quality standards — Bitrate, sample rate, file formats (MP3, WAV, AAC), and quality benchmarks for podcasts
  • Remote recording setup — Recording guests via Skype, Zoom, or local apps, using recording software like SquadCast or Riverside
  • Testing & troubleshooting — Sound checks, latency issues, echo problems, and fixing common recording problems
  • File management — Organizing recordings, naming conventions, backup strategies, and cloud storage solutions in India
  • Hardware maintenance — Cleaning microphones, cable care, storage, and longevity of equipment

Module 3: Audio Editing & Post-Production

📚 Topics Covered

  • Audio editing basics — Cutting, trimming, splitting, and joining audio tracks in editing software
  • Noise reduction techniques — Removing background hum, wind noise, room echo, and using noise gates effectively
  • Equalization (EQ) — Understanding frequency ranges, improving voice clarity, removing unwanted frequencies
  • Compression & dynamics — Making audio sound professional, evening out volume levels, and preventing clipping
  • Audio normalization — Standardizing volume levels across episodes and segments for consistency
  • Fading & transitions — Creating smooth fade-ins and fade-outs, crossfading between clips, and transition effects
  • Adding intro & outro music — Sourcing royalty-free music, copyright considerations, and integrating music into podcasts
  • Creating audio effects — Adding reverb, delay, panning, and special effects for creative podcasts
  • Mixing multiple tracks — Balancing host voice, guest audio, music, and sound effects together
  • Multitrack editing workflow — Managing multiple audio sources, syncing, and creating polished final output
  • Exporting & file formats — Choosing correct export settings, creating different versions for different platforms
  • Quality assurance — Listening for errors, checking audio levels, and final review before publishing

Module 4: Podcast Planning & Content Strategy

📚 Topics Covered

  • Podcast concept development — Defining your podcast idea, unique angle, and target audience in Indian market
  • Show format & structure — Creating episode templates, deciding episode length (15-60 minutes based on content type)
  • Content calendar planning — Planning 4-12 weeks of episodes, scheduling consistency, and maintaining quality
  • Script writing for podcasts — Writing conversational scripts, creating outlines, and interviewing techniques
  • Guest management — Finding guests, scheduling interviews, preparing guest briefs, and follow-up strategies
  • Branding & positioning — Creating podcast name, tagline, brand voice, and positioning against competitors
  • SEO for podcasts — Using keywords in titles, descriptions, show notes, and optimizing for discovery
  • Engagement strategies — Encouraging listener feedback, creating calls-to-action, and building community
  • Analytics & metrics — Understanding downloads, listener retention, audience growth, and measuring success
  • Consistency & publishing schedule — Setting realistic publishing frequency and maintaining listener expectations

Module 5: Podcast Distribution & Publishing

📚 Topics Covered

  • Podcast RSS feeds — Understanding RSS technology, creating feeds, and submitting to directories
  • Submitting to major platforms — Step-by-step submission to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, JioSaavn, Gaana
  • Podcast hosting platforms — Buzzsprout, Anchor, Podbean, Transistor, and free hosting options in India
  • Writing effective descriptions — Crafting podcast and episode descriptions that improve discoverability and attract listeners
  • Show notes & transcripts — Creating comprehensive show notes, timestamps, and transcription benefits for accessibility
  • Cover art & branding assets — Designing podcast cover art (3000x3000px), creating visual assets, and brand consistency
  • Publishing workflow — Scheduling episodes, timing releases, and coordinating with social media
  • Distribution to social media — Sharing podcast clips on Instagram, YouTube Shorts, Twitter, and LinkedIn
  • Email marketing integration — Building email lists, sending podcast updates, and newsletter strategies
  • Cross-platform promotion — Leveraging YouTube, blogs, social media, and partnerships for distribution
  • Podcast directories & discovery — Understanding how podcasts are discovered, featured placements, and trending strategies
  • Monetization setup — Adding sponsorship information, setting up affiliate links, and enabling listener support features

Module 6: Professional Skills & Industry Practice

📚 Topics Covered

  • Copyright & intellectual property — Understanding music licensing, creative commons, fair use, and podcast rights in India
  • Podcast ethics & standards — Fact-checking, source credibility, handling controversial topics, and journalistic integrity
  • Interviewing techniques — Asking good questions, active listening, handling difficult guests, and creating engaging conversations
  • Podcast marketing basics — Promoting your podcast, building audience, using social media, and growth hacking strategies
  • Building a podcast portfolio — Creating demo episodes, showcasing your work, and applying for podcast jobs in India
  • Freelance podcasting — Finding clients on Upwork, Fiverr, and Indian platforms, pricing your services, and managing clients
  • Podcast production workflow — Managing end-to-end production from planning to publishing, time management, and efficiency
  • Troubleshooting common issues — Handling technical problems, improving audio quality, and solving production challenges
  • Industry trends & tools — Staying updated with podcast trends, new software, emerging platforms, and continuous learning
  • Building your personal brand — Creating an online presence, networking in podcast community, and establishing credibility

  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior podcasting experience required
  • Interest in audio content creation and media is beneficial
  • Basic computer literacy required (ability to use Windows/Mac)
  • Access to computer/laptop for practical training

  • Students, unemployed, and working professionals all eligible
