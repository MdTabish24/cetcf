Fashion Styling & Personal Shopping

📂 Sector: Apparel Design, Pattern Making & Fashion Technology 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months 🕐 Hours: 120 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the art of transforming appearances through expert styling and personalized shopping services. This foundation course equips you with practical skills in color theory, body type analysis, wardrobe curation, and client management to launch a successful career in India's growing fashion styling industry.

Module 1: Fashion Industry Fundamentals & Styling Basics

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to Fashion Styling — definition, scope, and career opportunities in India
  • Difference between styling, personal shopping, and fashion consulting
  • Indian fashion industry overview — traditional wear, fusion, and contemporary trends
  • Professional ethics and client confidentiality in styling practice
  • Types of styling services — personal, editorial, bridal, corporate, and event styling
  • Role of a fashion stylist in Indian retail and e-commerce sectors
  • Fashion terminology and vocabulary used in Indian fashion industry
  • Introduction to fashion research tools and trend forecasting platforms

Module 2: Body Analysis, Proportions & Personal Color Theory

📚 Topics Covered

  • Understanding body shapes — apple, pear, hourglass, rectangle, inverted triangle (Indian context)
  • Body measurements and proportion analysis techniques
  • Dressing for different body types — practical styling solutions
  • Color theory fundamentals — primary, secondary, and tertiary colors
  • Seasonal color analysis — determining warm, cool, and neutral undertones
  • Indian skin tones and color consultation for diverse complexions
  • Creating personal color palettes for different clients
  • Fabric and texture selection based on skin tone and body type
  • Psychology of colors in Indian culture and fashion choices
  • Virtual color consultation methods and tools

Module 3: Wardrobe Planning, Curation & Capsule Building

📚 Topics Covered

  • Wardrobe assessment and audit techniques
  • Understanding lifestyle and daily requirements of clients
  • Building capsule wardrobes for Indian professionals — office, casual, festive
  • Essential basics and foundation pieces for every wardrobe
  • Mix-and-match styling strategies to maximize outfit combinations
  • Seasonal wardrobe planning for Indian climate
  • Budget-friendly wardrobe building and smart shopping practices
  • Sustainable fashion and ethical shopping guidelines
  • Care and maintenance of clothing — fabric-specific tips
  • Digital wardrobe management and styling apps
  • Creating style guides and mood boards for clients

Module 4: Indian Fashion Trends, Traditional & Contemporary Styling

📚 Topics Covered

  • Indian traditional wear — sarees, salwar kameez, lehengas, and regional variations
  • Contemporary Indian fashion — fusion wear and modern interpretations
  • Bridal styling in Indian context — regional bridal aesthetics
  • Festival and occasion wear styling — Diwali, weddings, religious events
  • Corporate and formal wear styling for Indian workplaces
  • Casual and weekend wear for modern Indian lifestyle
  • Accessorizing traditional and contemporary outfits
  • Footwear selection and coordination with Indian outfits
  • Jewelry styling for different occasions and outfits
  • Current fashion trends and forecasting for Indian market
  • Influencer and celebrity styling analysis

Module 5: Personal Shopping, Client Management & Communication

📚 Topics Covered

  • Client consultation process — intake forms and questionnaires
  • Active listening and understanding client needs and preferences
  • Building client profiles and style preferences documentation
  • Shopping strategies — online, offline, and hybrid approaches
  • Navigating Indian retail landscape — malls, boutiques, local markets
  • E-commerce platforms for fashion — Myntra, Flipkart, Amazon Fashion, Ajio
  • Budget management and cost-effective shopping techniques
  • Quality assessment of fabrics, stitching, and fit
  • Return and exchange policies and customer service
  • Building long-term client relationships and loyalty
  • Professional communication — emails, calls, and messaging etiquette
  • Handling difficult clients and managing expectations professionally

Module 6: Professional Practice, Business Setup & Portfolio Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Starting a styling business — registration and legal requirements in India
  • Creating a professional brand identity and personal styling brand
  • Pricing services — consultation fees, shopping fees, and package structures
  • Building a portfolio — photography, documentation, and case studies
  • Social media presence for stylists — Instagram, Pinterest, YouTube strategies
  • Creating before-and-after transformation content
  • Networking in the Indian fashion industry
  • Collaborations with fashion brands, boutiques, and retailers
  • Customer feedback and testimonial management
  • Professional certification and continuous learning opportunities
  • Time management and scheduling for multiple clients
  • Financial management — invoicing, GST compliance, and record-keeping

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass
  • No prior experience needed — open to all fashion enthusiasts
  • Already working as a stylist or in fashion retail? Apply for direct RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid ID proof required at registration — Aadhaar, PAN, or Voter ID
  • Passion for fashion and strong communication skills preferred
  • Basic computer literacy helpful for digital wardrobe management and social media
  • Applicable for all candidates across India

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Analyze client body types, proportions, and personal color undertones to provide customized styling recommendations
  • Create comprehensive wardrobe plans and capsule wardrobes tailored to individual lifestyles, budgets, and preferences
  • Demonstrate knowledge of Indian fashion trends, traditional wear, and contemporary styling across different occasions
  • Conduct professional client consultations, manage expectations, and build long-term professional relationships
  • Execute personal shopping services effectively using both online and offline retail channels in India
  • Develop a professional styling portfolio, establish a personal brand, and manage a styling business with ethical practices
