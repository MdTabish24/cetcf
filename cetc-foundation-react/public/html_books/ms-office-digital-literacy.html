MS Office & Digital Literacy

📂 Sector: Digital & Information Technology 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 2 Months 🕐 Hours: 120 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master essential digital skills and MS Office applications to unlock employment opportunities in India's rapidly growing digital economy. This foundation-level course equips you with practical competencies in word processing, spreadsheets, presentations, and digital communication—skills demanded across all sectors and industries.

Module 1: Digital Literacy Fundamentals & Computer Basics

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to computers and digital devices — understanding hardware, software, and peripherals used in Indian offices
  • Operating System basics (Windows/Linux) — navigating desktop, file management, and folder organization
  • Computer input and output devices — keyboard shortcuts, mouse operations, and printer connectivity in professional settings
  • Internet fundamentals — understanding bandwidth, connectivity types (broadband, WiFi, mobile data) common in India
  • Cybersecurity awareness — password creation, phishing detection, and protecting personal information online
  • Digital etiquette and professional conduct — email protocols, online communication standards in Indian workplaces
  • Troubleshooting common computer issues — restarting, updating drivers, clearing cache for optimal performance
  • Accessibility features — enabling accessibility tools for differently-abled users in compliance with Indian standards

Module 2: MS Word: Document Creation & Professional Writing

📚 Topics Covered

  • MS Word interface and ribbon navigation — customizing toolbars and understanding document templates
  • Document formatting essentials — fonts, styles, alignment, spacing, and indentation for professional documents
  • Creating business letters and formal documents — letterhead design, official correspondence formats used in India
  • Tables and document structure — creating organized reports, forms, and structured business documents
  • Headers, footers, and page numbering — implementing page breaks, sections, and watermarks in long documents
  • Styles and templates — creating custom templates for recurring documents (invoices, reports, certificates)
  • Mail merge functionality — generating bulk letters, labels, and personalized documents for Indian businesses
  • Document review and collaboration — tracking changes, comments, and version control for team projects
  • Inserting images, shapes, and objects — embedding charts, logos, and visual elements in professional documents
  • Printing and PDF conversion — preparing documents for print and digital distribution with proper formatting

Module 3: MS Excel: Data Management & Spreadsheet Analysis

📚 Topics Covered

  • Excel interface and worksheet fundamentals — navigating cells, rows, columns, and multiple sheet management
  • Data entry and cell formatting — number formats, currency formatting (Indian Rupee), dates, and text alignment
  • Basic formulas and functions — SUM, AVERAGE, COUNT, MIN, MAX for quick calculations and data analysis
  • Creating professional spreadsheets — organizing sales data, inventory lists, and financial records common in Indian SMEs
  • Sorting and filtering data — arranging information alphabetically, numerically, and by custom criteria for analysis
  • Conditional formatting — highlighting cells based on values, creating visual heat maps for data interpretation
  • Charts and graphs — creating bar charts, pie charts, and line graphs for presentations and reports
  • Pivot tables — summarizing large datasets, analyzing sales by region/product in Indian business contexts
  • Advanced formulas — IF statements, VLOOKUP, INDEX-MATCH for complex data retrieval and analysis
  • Data validation and protection — restricting entries, password-protecting sheets, and preventing accidental modifications
  • Printing and sharing spreadsheets — page setup, print areas, and exporting data for stakeholder reports

Module 4: MS PowerPoint: Presentations & Visual Communication

📚 Topics Covered

  • PowerPoint interface and slide design — understanding layouts, themes, and master slides for consistent branding
  • Creating effective slides — best practices for text, bullet points, and visual hierarchy in Indian business presentations
  • Adding multimedia content — inserting images, audio, and video clips for engaging presentations
  • Slide transitions and animations — applying effects appropriately without overusing for professional impact
  • Building company presentations — creating pitch decks, product launches, and quarterly reports for Indian companies
  • Using SmartArt and shapes — creating organizational charts, process flows, and business diagrams
  • Presenter notes and speaker view — preparing notes, practicing delivery, and managing presentations effectively
  • Slide master and templates — creating branded templates for consistent organizational communication
  • Presentation delivery techniques — managing timing, handling Q&A, and engaging audience in Indian boardrooms
  • Exporting and sharing presentations — saving as PDF, video, and preparing for online distribution

Module 5: Internet, Email & Online Communication Tools

📚 Topics Covered

  • Web browsers and internet navigation — using Chrome, Firefox, Edge for efficient online research and information gathering
  • Search engines and advanced searching — Google search operators, evaluating source credibility in Indian context
  • Email fundamentals — creating accounts (Gmail, Outlook), understanding SMTP/IMAP protocols
  • Professional email composition — writing formal emails, subject lines, and maintaining professional tone in business communication
  • Email attachments and file management — sending large files, using cloud storage (Google Drive, OneDrive) for document sharing
  • Email organization and management — folders, filters, and labels for organizing high-volume correspondence in Indian offices
  • Collaborative tools — using Google Workspace, Microsoft Teams for remote work and team collaboration
  • Video conferencing basics — Zoom, Google Meet for virtual meetings, webinars, and online training sessions
  • Online document sharing and version control — managing collaborative projects using cloud-based platforms
  • Digital communication etiquette — understanding cultural nuances in Indian business email and chat communication

Module 6: Digital Safety, Ethics & Professional Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Data security and privacy — understanding GDPR, India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, and organizational data policies
  • Malware and antivirus protection — recognizing threats, installing security software, and maintaining system hygiene
  • Social media safety — managing privacy settings, avoiding oversharing, and understanding workplace social media policies
  • Online payment security — safe online transactions, recognizing fraudulent websites, and protecting financial information
  • Intellectual property and copyright — understanding licensing, plagiarism, and proper attribution in digital work
  • Workplace digital ethics — appropriate internet usage, respecting confidentiality, and understanding acceptable use policies
  • Digital footprint management — understanding personal branding and maintaining professional online presence
  • Accessibility and inclusive digital practices — creating documents accessible to all users, understanding Indian accessibility standards
  • Continuous learning and digital skills development — identifying online resources, MOOCs, and certification opportunities in India
  • Career advancement through digital skills — leveraging LinkedIn, online portfolios, and professional networks for job opportunities
  • Emerging technologies awareness — understanding AI, cloud computing, and automation trends affecting Indian job market
  • Practical capstone project — creating a comprehensive portfolio demonstrating all learned skills

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior computer experience required
  • Already working in a related field? Apply for Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) exam
  • Valid government-issued ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Passport) required at registration
  • Access to a computer and internet connection for online learning components
  • Applicable for all candidates across India, including persons with disabilities

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Demonstrate proficiency in using Windows/Linux operating systems and managing digital files and folders effectively
  • Create, format, and edit professional documents using MS Word with proper styling, templates, and mail merge functionality
  • Build and analyze spreadsheets using MS Excel with formulas, pivot tables, and data visualization techniques
  • Design and deliver engaging presentations using MS PowerPoint with multimedia elements and proper branding
  • Compose professional emails, manage digital communication, and collaborate using cloud-based tools and platforms
  • Identify and implement digital security measures, understand data protection laws, and practice ethical digital citizenship in Indian workplaces
