Module 1: Nail Anatomy, Health & Safety Fundamentals

📚 Topics Covered

  • Nail structure and anatomy — understanding nail plate, nail bed, cuticle, and nail matrix for proper extension application
  • Nail growth cycle and health indicators — recognizing healthy nails vs diseased nails to identify contraindications
  • Common nail diseases and disorders — fungal infections, psoriasis, eczema, and when to refer clients to dermatologists
  • Skin diseases and infections — identifying contagious conditions that prevent service provision
  • Sterilization and sanitation protocols — autoclave use, disinfectant solutions (bleach, phenol), tool sterilization procedures
  • Personal hygiene and PPE requirements — gloves, masks, hand washing, preventing cross-contamination
  • Salon safety standards — ventilation systems, chemical storage, fire safety, first aid basics
  • Allergies and sensitivity management — patch testing, identifying allergic reactions to products
  • Legal and regulatory compliance — FSSAI guidelines for beauty products, salon licensing in Indian states

Module 2: Gel Nail Extension Products & Chemistry

📚 Topics Covered

  • Types of gel products — hard gels, soft gels, builder gels, polygels, and their differences in application and removal
  • Gel composition and chemistry — oligomers, monomers, photoinitiators, and how UV/LED light cures gel
  • UV vs LED lamps — spectrum differences, curing times, safety considerations, and equipment comparison
  • Base coats and primers — bonding agents, their role in adhesion, and application techniques
  • Top coats and finishes — glossy, matte, gel toppers, and their protective functions
  • Gel removal methods — soaking, filing, acetone use, and preventing nail damage during removal
  • Product shelf life and storage — proper storage conditions, expiry identification, and quality maintenance
  • Brand comparison and cost analysis — Indian market brands (Nails Inc, Lakme, Colorbar) vs international brands
  • Allergenic ingredients and safety concerns — HEMA-free gels, hypoallergenic options for sensitive clients
  • Color theory and pigmentation — understanding gel color mixing, undertones, and seasonal trends

Module 3: Acrylic Nail Extension Products & Application Science

📚 Topics Covered

  • Acrylic powder and liquid chemistry — polymer beads, monomers, and polymerization process
  • Types of acrylic systems — traditional powder-liquid, self-leveling acrylics, and dip powder acrylics
  • Acrylic powder varieties — nail-colored, clear, glitter-embedded, and specialty powders
  • Liquid (monomer) types and safety — EMA vs MMA, toxicity levels, and ventilation requirements
  • Mixing ratios and consistency — achieving proper bead consistency for different techniques
  • Acrylic curing and hardening — exothermic reactions, heat management, and timing
  • Acrylic removal and damage prevention — safe filing techniques, acetone soaking, and minimizing natural nail damage
  • Acrylic vs gel comparison — durability, cost, maintenance, and suitability for different clients
  • Odor management and ventilation — air purifiers, table fans, and creating comfortable salon environment
  • Quality control and product testing — identifying defective products, checking consistency and color accuracy

📚 Topics Covered

  • Nail surface preparation — filing, buffing, dehydration, and removing shine for proper adhesion
  • Cuticle removal and management — safe cuticle pushing, removal techniques, and moisturizing
  • Nail sizing and tip selection — choosing correct tip sizes for each finger, custom sizing for irregular nails
  • Tip application techniques — proper glue application, pressure, angle, and timing for gel and acrylic
  • Building nail length and shape — apex placement, stress areas, and creating structurally sound extensions
  • Gel extension application — building with builder gel, creating forms, and achieving desired thickness
  • Acrylic application methods — brush techniques, bead placement, sculpting on forms, and free-hand sculpting
  • Sculpting and shaping — creating square, round, almond, coffin, and stiletto shapes with precision
  • Blending tips with natural nail — seamless integration, invisible seams, and professional finish
  • Thickness and weight management — avoiding heavy extensions, proper load distribution, and comfort for clients
  • Extension maintenance and infills — timing, refill techniques, and maintaining extension integrity
  • Common application mistakes — bubbles, lifting, improper adhesion, and corrective measures
