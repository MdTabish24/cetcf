HD Makeup & Airbrush Technique

📂 Sector: Advanced Beauty, Cosmetology & Aesthetics 📊 Level: Advanced ⏱️ Duration: 3 Months 🕐 Hours: 180 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1500
Master the art of High Definition (HD) makeup and professional airbrush techniques used in film, television, bridal, and fashion industries. This advanced course teaches cutting-edge makeup application methods that create flawless, camera-ready finishes with precision and durability. Become a certified HD makeup artist and airbrush specialist in just 12 weeks.

Module 1: HD Makeup Fundamentals & Skin Science

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to HD Makeup — What is HD makeup, difference from traditional makeup, camera angles and lighting considerations
  • Skin anatomy and physiology — Skin layers, pores, texture, and how HD makeup works on different skin types
  • Skin types and conditions — Oily, dry, combination, sensitive, acne-prone skin; identifying and analyzing skin conditions
  • Skin care basics for makeup artists — Cleansing, toning, moisturizing, sunscreen application before makeup
  • Undertones and skin tone classification — Warm, cool, neutral undertones; Indian skin tone categories (fair, medium, olive, deep)
  • Face shape analysis — Round, oval, square, heart, oblong faces; contouring and highlighting principles for each
  • Hygiene and sanitation standards — Sterilization of tools, single-use applicators, cross-contamination prevention
  • Professional ethics and client consultation — Understanding client needs, managing expectations, confidentiality
  • Product knowledge basics — Foundation types, concealers, powders, primers; reading product labels and ingredients
  • Tools and brushes fundamentals — Types of brushes, bristle materials, proper care and cleaning techniques

Module 2: Color Theory & Foundation Matching

📚 Topics Covered

  • Color wheel and color theory — Primary, secondary, tertiary colors; complementary and analogous color combinations
  • Undertone matching for foundations — Finding perfect match for Indian skin tones, avoiding orange and ashy finishes
  • Foundation types and formulations — Liquid, cream, powder, stick foundations; coverage levels and finishes (matte, dewy, satin)
  • HD foundation application techniques — Stippling, buffing, and blending methods for seamless coverage
  • Concealer selection and application — Under-eye, blemish, and color-correcting concealer techniques
  • Contouring and highlighting — Sculpting face with cream and powder products; creating dimension
  • Color correction for skin concerns — Using green, yellow, pink, and red correctors for blemishes, redness, and dark circles
  • Shade matching for diverse skin tones — Avoiding white cast, matching neck and jaw, seasonal shade variations
  • Mixing and customizing shades — Creating custom foundation shades for perfect match
  • Blending and setting techniques — Powder selection, setting spray application, longevity of makeup
  • Light and shadow in HD makeup — Understanding how light reflects on different textures and finishes
  • Corrective makeup — Disguising imperfections, scars, and tattoos with proper techniques

Module 3: Airbrush Equipment, Maintenance & Operation

📚 Topics Covered

  • Airbrush equipment overview — Types of airbrushes (single-action, double-action), components and parts identification
  • Air compressor selection and operation — PSI settings, tank capacity, moisture trap, pressure regulators
  • Airbrush guns and nozzles — Different nozzle sizes (0.35mm, 0.4mm, 0.5mm), needle types, and their applications
  • Airbrush hose and connections — Air hose selection, quick-disconnect couplings, pressure line setup
  • Paint cup and fluid systems — Gravity-fed vs siphon-fed systems, cup capacity, fluid consistency requirements
  • Setting up airbrush workstation — Spray booth, ventilation, lighting, work surface, safety equipment
  • Cleaning and maintenance protocols — Daily cleaning, deep cleaning, needle care, preventing clogs
  • Troubleshooting common airbrush problems — Spitting, clogging, uneven spray, air leaks, pressure issues
  • Safety protocols and ventilation — Proper PPE, respiratory protection, spray booth requirements, ventilation standards
  • Product preparation for airbrush — Thinning makeup products, achieving correct viscosity, strain-free application
  • Testing spray patterns — Practicing spray control, distance adjustment, pressure modulation
  • Airbrush hygiene and sterilization — Preventing bacterial contamination, proper storage, equipment sanitization

Module 4: Airbrush Application Techniques & HD Makeup Execution

📚 Topics Covered

  • Airbrush spray techniques — Gun angles, distance from face, trigger control, pressure modulation for even coverage
  • Foundation application with airbrush — Building coverage gradually, blending edges, achieving flawless finish
  • Contouring with airbrush — Creating shadows, depth, and dimension; blending contour seamlessly
  • Highlighting with airbrush — Applying highlights, creating glow, strategic placement for face lift effect
  • Blush and bronzer application — Achieving natural flush, bronzing techniques, blending for dimension
  • Airbrush eyeshadow techniques — Creating gradients, blending multiple colors, special effects on lids
  • Eyeliner and eyebrow airbrushing — Precision application, defining brows, liquid eyeliner effects
  • Lip color application with airbrush — Even coverage, ombre lips, gradient effects, lip lining techniques
  • HD makeup for different occasions — Bridal makeup, party makeup, everyday makeup, professional looks
  • Correcting airbrush mistakes — Fixing uneven coverage, over-spray, color correction mid-application
  • Layering and building coverage — Strategic layering for HD effect, avoiding caking, maintaining natural look
  • Setting airbrush makeup — Using setting sprays, powders, and sealers for longevity and photography

Module 5: Bridal & Special Occasion HD Makeup

📚 Topics Covered

  • Bridal makeup consultation — Understanding bride's vision, skin tone, outfit color, wedding theme, cultural preferences
  • Bridal skin preparation — Pre-wedding skincare timeline, addressing skin concerns, primer selection for longevity
  • Bridal makeup application — Creating timeless, elegant looks that photograph well under different lighting
  • Bridal eye makeup — Dramatic yet elegant eyeshadow, eye-opening techniques, waterproof products for tears
  • Bridal lips and cheeks — Long-lasting lip color, cheek color that complements outfit, maintaining vibrancy
  • Regional Indian bridal makeup — North Indian, South Indian, East Indian, West Indian bridal makeup styles
  • Makeup for different skin tones — Bridal looks for fair, medium, olive, and deep skin tones
  • Makeup for different wedding themes — Traditional, modern, fusion, destination wedding makeup
  • Party and festive makeup — Diwali, Holi, festivals, evening parties; bold colors and dramatic looks
  • Makeup for special events — Engagements, mehendi, sangeet, reception, haldi ceremonies
  • Touch-up and maintenance during events — Products for touch-ups, long-lasting formulations, quick fixes
  • Photography and videography considerations — Makeup under different lighting, camera angles, video-ready finishes
