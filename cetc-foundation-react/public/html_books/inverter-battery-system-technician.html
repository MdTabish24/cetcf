Inverter & Battery System Technician

📂 Sector: Electrical & Electronics Trades 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 1 Month 🕐 Hours: 60 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Learn to install, maintain, and repair inverter and battery systems that power homes and businesses across India. This hands-on course trains you to become a skilled technician in one of India's fastest-growing sectors, with guaranteed job opportunities in the renewable energy and power backup industry.

Module 1: Basics of Electricity & Electrical Safety

📚 Topics Covered

  • Fundamentals of electricity — voltage, current, resistance, and power explained in simple terms
  • AC vs DC current — understanding alternating and direct current and their applications
  • Ohm's Law and electrical calculations — practical formulas for technicians
  • Electrical safety standards — IS 1866 and IEC standards for Indian installations
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE) — proper use of gloves, goggles, and safety tools
  • Earthing and grounding basics — why earthing is critical for inverter systems
  • Common electrical hazards — shock, fire, and electrocution prevention
  • First aid for electrical injuries — CPR and emergency response procedures

Module 2: Inverter Systems & Technology

📚 Topics Covered

  • What is an inverter — converting DC to AC power for home and office use
  • Types of inverters — square wave, modified sine wave, and pure sine wave explained
  • Inverter specifications — understanding VA, watts, efficiency, and surge capacity
  • Pure sine wave vs modified sine wave — which devices need which type
  • Inverter components — IGBT, transformer, capacitors, and circuit protection devices
  • Single phase and three phase inverters — applications in Indian homes and industries
  • Smart inverters and MPPT technology — modern features and benefits
  • Inverter rating and sizing — selecting correct capacity for home loads
  • Warranty and certifications — BIS certification and quality standards in India
  • Installation guidelines — wall mounting, floor placement, and ventilation requirements

Module 3: Battery Systems & Energy Storage

📚 Topics Covered

  • Lead-acid batteries — construction, working principle, and maintenance procedures
  • Lithium-ion batteries — advantages, lifespan, and safety considerations
  • Battery capacity and rating — understanding Ah (ampere-hour) and Wh (watt-hour)
  • Battery charging cycles — float charging, bulk charging, and absorption phase
  • Battery health monitoring — specific gravity, voltage testing, and capacity checks
  • Battery safety — preventing overcharge, deep discharge, and thermal runaway
  • Battery terminals and connections — proper cable sizing and terminal cleaning
  • Tubular and flat plate batteries — differences and suitable applications
  • Battery backup time calculation — estimating runtime for appliances
  • Environmental impact and recycling — responsible battery disposal in India

Module 4: Installation & Wiring Practices

📚 Topics Covered

  • Pre-installation checklist — site assessment and customer requirements
  • Electrical load calculation — measuring total power consumption of appliances
  • Cable selection and sizing — understanding wire gauge (mm²) for safety
  • Wiring diagrams and circuit diagrams — reading and interpreting technical drawings
  • Battery bank configuration — series and parallel connections for desired voltage
  • Inverter to battery connections — correct polarity and cable routing
  • AC distribution board setup — connecting inverter output to home circuits
  • Bypass switch installation — manual switchover between grid and inverter
  • Earthing and neutral bonding — proper grounding for inverter systems
  • Cable management and labeling — professional installation practices
  • Testing before commissioning — voltage, continuity, and load testing
  • Customer handover and documentation — operation manuals and warranty cards

Module 5: Troubleshooting, Maintenance & Repair

📚 Topics Covered

  • Inverter not starting — common causes and quick fixes
  • Low backup time — battery health testing and capacity restoration
  • Overload and over-temperature shutdown — understanding protection mechanisms
  • Battery not charging — charger circuit diagnosis and repair
  • Beeping and alarm codes — interpreting inverter display messages
  • Voltage fluctuation issues — stabilizer interaction and load balancing
  • Inverter making noise — fan problems and transformer issues
  • Solar integration troubleshooting — MPPT and charge controller faults
  • Multimeter usage — practical testing of voltage, current, and resistance
  • Component replacement — changing fuses, capacitors, and relays safely
  • Preventive maintenance schedule — monthly, quarterly, and annual tasks
  • Customer communication — explaining technical issues in simple language

Module 6: Solar Integration & Advanced Features

📚 Topics Covered

  • Solar panel basics — understanding wattage, voltage, and panel types
  • MPPT charge controllers — maximum power point tracking explained
  • PWM vs MPPT — efficiency differences and cost-benefit analysis
  • Grid-tie inverters — net metering and solar integration with electricity board
  • Hybrid inverters — combining solar, battery, and grid power intelligently
  • Smart monitoring systems — mobile apps and remote monitoring features
  • Energy management — load scheduling and priority-based power distribution
  • Government subsidies and schemes — MNRE and state-level solar incentives in India

✅ Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age: 14 years (14-40 years preferred)
  • Minimum education: Class 8th pass or equivalent
  • No prior experience needed — beginners welcome
  • Already working as inverter technician? Apply for direct RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) exam
  • Valid government ID proof required at registration (Aadhaar, PAN, or Voter ID)
  • Medical fitness: No color blindness or serious hearing impairment
  • Applicable for all Indian citizens and registered foreign residents

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Understand electrical fundamentals, safety standards, and inverter technology relevant to Indian market
  • Install, configure, and commission inverter and battery systems for residential and commercial use
  • Perform accurate electrical load calculations and select appropriate inverter and battery capacity
  • Test and diagnose battery health using multimeter and industry-standard procedures
  • Troubleshoot common inverter faults and perform repairs with minimal supervision
  • Maintain inverter systems according to preventive maintenance schedules and customer service standards
