Keratin & Hair Smoothing Treatment

📂 Sector: Advanced Beauty, Cosmetology & Aesthetics 📊 Level: Foundation ⏱️ Duration: 1 Month 🕐 Hours: 60 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1000
Master the art of professional keratin and hair smoothing treatments with this comprehensive foundation course. Learn industry-standard techniques, product knowledge, and safety protocols to deliver premium hair transformation services. Perfect for aspiring beauty professionals looking to specialize in high-demand hair smoothing treatments.

Module 1: Fundamentals of Hair Structure & Chemistry

📚 Topics Covered

  • Hair anatomy and structure — understanding cortex, medulla, and cuticle layers and how they affect treatment outcomes
  • Hair protein composition — keratin, collagen, and amino acids role in hair health and smoothing treatments
  • Hair damage assessment — identifying chemical damage, heat damage, and natural texture variations
  • pH balance and hair chemistry — how pH affects keratin penetration and treatment effectiveness
  • Hair porosity classification — low, medium, and high porosity hair and treatment suitability
  • Chemical bonds in hair — hydrogen bonds, disulfide bonds, and ionic bonds relevant to smoothing treatments
  • Hair growth cycle — anagen, catagen, and telogen phases and treatment timing
  • Scalp health assessment — identifying scalp conditions that may contraindicate treatments

Module 2: Keratin Treatment Types & Product Knowledge

📚 Topics Covered

  • Brazilian keratin treatments — formulation, benefits, processing time, and expected results
  • Japanese hair straightening (Thermal reconditioning) — chemical process, heat application, and permanent results
  • Protein-based smoothing treatments — collagen, silk protein, and keratin blend products
  • Formaldehyde-free keratin treatments — safer alternatives and their effectiveness comparison
  • Organic and natural keratin products — plant-based options and market trends in India
  • Keratin treatment product brands — Indian and international brands, quality standards, and pricing
  • Understanding ingredient labels — reading product composition, concentration levels, and safety certifications
  • Product shelf life and storage — proper handling, temperature control, and expiry management
  • Patch testing protocols — allergy testing procedures and documentation requirements
  • Cost analysis and markup calculation — product cost, application time, and pricing strategy for salons

Module 3: Pre-Treatment Consultation & Hair Diagnosis

📚 Topics Covered

  • Client consultation framework — gathering information about hair history, lifestyle, and expectations
  • Hair texture analysis — straight, wavy, curly, coily classifications and treatment compatibility
  • Damage level assessment — visual and tactile methods to evaluate treatment damage
  • Scalp examination — identifying conditions like dandruff, psoriasis, dermatitis that affect treatment
  • Previous chemical treatment history — identifying prior coloring, perming, or relaxing treatments
  • Lifestyle and maintenance assessment — client's styling habits, heat tool usage, and commitment to aftercare
  • Realistic expectation setting — communicating treatment results, duration, and maintenance requirements
  • Contraindication identification — medical conditions, allergies, and pregnancy considerations
  • Before and after photography — proper lighting, angles, and documentation for portfolio
  • Treatment plan customization — selecting appropriate keratin type based on hair condition and goals
  • Consent and patch test documentation — legal requirements and client safety records

Module 4: Application Techniques & Treatment Process

📚 Topics Covered

  • Pre-treatment shampooing — clarifying shampoo selection and proper cleansing technique
  • Hair sectioning methods — dividing hair into manageable sections for even product application
  • Product application technique — brush application, saturation levels, and section-by-section methodology
  • Timing and processing — monitoring processing time based on hair type and product instructions
  • Heat application methods — blow drying, flat iron, and thermal processing for keratin activation
  • Temperature control and safety — maintaining safe heat levels to prevent hair and scalp damage
  • Sectional flat ironing technique — proper pressure, speed, and multiple passes for uniform smoothing
  • Cooling and setting phase — allowing treated hair to cool and set before rinsing
  • Post-treatment rinsing protocols — lukewarm water rinsing and residue removal
  • Final styling and finishing — blow drying, straightening, and product application for polished look
  • Treatment duration management — time management for different hair lengths and densities
  • Common mistakes and corrections — troubleshooting uneven results, frizz, and texture issues

Module 5: Aftercare, Maintenance & Client Education

📚 Topics Covered

  • Post-treatment waiting period — 48-72 hour no-wash rule and explanation to clients
  • Recommended aftercare products — sulfate-free shampoos, keratin-infused conditioners, and serums
  • Heat styling recommendations — appropriate tools, temperature settings, and frequency for treated hair
  • Home care routine — daily, weekly, and monthly maintenance schedule for prolonged results
  • Hair washing frequency — optimal washing schedule to extend treatment longevity
  • Deep conditioning treatments — protein masks and moisturizing treatments for maintenance
  • Swimming and water exposure — chlorine protection and salt water precautions
  • Sun protection for treated hair — UV protection products and outdoor care tips
  • Diet and hydration — nutritional factors affecting hair health and treatment results
  • Touch-up scheduling — when and how to schedule maintenance treatments for optimal results

Module 6: Safety, Hygiene, Business & Professional Development

📚 Topics Covered

  • Salon hygiene standards — sterilization, sanitization, and cleanliness protocols
  • Personal protective equipment (PPE) — gloves, aprons, masks, and ventilation requirements
  • Chemical safety and handling — proper storage, disposal, and spill management procedures
  • Occupational health risks — respiratory protection, skin exposure prevention, and health monitoring
  • First aid for chemical exposure — immediate response to skin contact, eye contact, and inhalation
  • Salon setup and ventilation — adequate air circulation and fume extraction systems
  • Record keeping and documentation — client files, treatment records, and safety documentation
  • Building client relationships — communication, follow-up, and loyalty programs
  • Pricing strategy and service packages — competitive pricing in Indian market and package bundling
  • Marketing and social media — Instagram, YouTube, and local advertising for keratin services
  • Starting your own salon — registration, licensing, and business setup requirements in India
  • Professional development — continuing education, advanced certifications, and industry trends
