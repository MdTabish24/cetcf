Digital Marketing Professional

📂 Sector: Digital & Information Technology 📊 Level: Intermediate ⏱️ Duration: 3 Months 🕐 Hours: 180 💰 Exam Fee: ₹1200
Master the complete digital marketing ecosystem with hands-on training in SEO, social media, content marketing, and paid advertising. This industry-aligned course prepares you for immediate employment in India's fastest-growing digital economy, with practical skills used by top brands and agencies across the country.

Module 1: Digital Marketing Fundamentals & Landscape

📚 Topics Covered

  • Introduction to Digital Marketing — Definition, evolution, and significance in India's digital economy; comparison with traditional marketing
  • Digital Marketing Channels Overview — Search engines, social media, email, content, affiliate, mobile, and voice marketing channels
  • Consumer Behavior in Digital Space — Understanding online customer journey, touchpoints, decision-making patterns, and Indian consumer preferences
  • Digital Marketing Ecosystem — Role of agencies, platforms, tools, and stakeholders in India's digital marketing industry
  • Business Models & Monetization — E-commerce, SaaS, content monetization, and affiliate models relevant to Indian market
  • Market Trends in India — Current digital marketing trends, emerging technologies (AI, AR), and industry projections for 2024-2026
  • Ethics & Compliance in Digital Marketing — GDPR basics, Indian data protection laws, advertising standards, and responsible marketing practices
  • Setting Up Your Digital Presence — Creating professional profiles on LinkedIn, Twitter, and industry platforms for personal branding
  • Introduction to Digital Marketing Tools — Overview of Google Analytics, Meta Business Suite, Mailchimp, Canva, and SEMrush
  • Career Pathways in Digital Marketing — Various roles, specializations, and growth opportunities in Indian digital marketing industry

Module 2: Search Engine Optimization (SEO) & Content Marketing

📚 Topics Covered

  • SEO Fundamentals — How search engines work, ranking factors, and importance of organic traffic for Indian businesses
  • Keyword Research & Analysis — Tools like Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, and SEMrush; identifying high-intent keywords for Indian markets
  • On-Page SEO Optimization — Title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, URL optimization, and internal linking strategies
  • Technical SEO Basics — Site speed, mobile-friendliness, XML sitemaps, robots.txt, canonical tags, and structured data markup
  • Off-Page SEO & Link Building — Backlink strategies, guest posting, local citations, and building authority for Indian websites
  • Local SEO for Indian Businesses — Google My Business optimization, local citations, reviews management, and location-based ranking
  • Content Marketing Strategy — Content pillars, buyer personas, content calendar creation, and editorial planning for Indian audiences
  • Content Creation & Copywriting — Writing SEO-friendly blog posts, product descriptions, landing pages, and engaging headlines
  • Measuring SEO Performance — Google Analytics setup, tracking rankings, monitoring traffic, and calculating ROI from organic search
  • SEO Case Studies & Best Practices — Real examples from Indian e-commerce, SaaS, and service-based companies; common mistakes to avoid

Module 3: Paid Advertising: Google Ads & Performance Marketing

📚 Topics Covered

  • Google Ads Fundamentals — Platform overview, account structure, campaign types, and bidding strategies for Indian market
  • Search Advertising (Google Search Ads) — Creating campaigns, keyword selection, ad copy writing, landing page optimization, and quality score improvement
  • Display Advertising — Google Display Network, banner creation, audience targeting, and remarketing strategies for Indian brands
  • Shopping Ads & E-commerce Advertising — Product feed setup, merchant center optimization, and driving conversions for Indian e-commerce businesses
  • Google Ads Bidding Strategies — Manual CPC, automated bidding, target CPA, ROAS optimization, and budget allocation techniques
  • Ad Copy & Creative Testing — A/B testing methodologies, writing compelling ad headlines, descriptions, and improving click-through rates
  • Landing Page Optimization — Conversion rate optimization, page speed, mobile responsiveness, and persuasive copywriting for Indian audiences
  • Budget Management & Cost Control — Daily budgets, bid adjustments, negative keywords, and maximizing ROI on limited budgets
  • Google Ads Analytics & Reporting — Conversion tracking, UTM parameters, Google Analytics integration, and performance dashboards
  • Performance Marketing Metrics — Understanding CPC, CPM, CPA, ROAS, LTV, CAC, and profitability calculations for Indian startups and SMEs

Module 4: Social Media Marketing & Community Management

📚 Topics Covered

  • Social Media Platform Overview — Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, and emerging platforms (Threads, Koo) popular in India
  • Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Marketing — Account setup, business pages, audience insights, and algorithm understanding for Indian creators
  • Meta Ads Manager — Campaign creation, audience targeting, lookalike audiences, and conversion tracking on Meta platforms
  • Instagram Marketing Strategy — Content pillars, Reels optimization, Stories strategy, hashtag research, and influencer collaboration for Indian brands
  • LinkedIn Marketing — B2B content strategy, professional networking, LinkedIn ads, and thought leadership for Indian professionals and companies
  • YouTube Marketing — Channel optimization, video SEO, thumbnail creation, engagement strategies, and monetization options for Indian creators
  • Content Calendar & Planning — Scheduling tools (Buffer, Later, Meta Business Suite), content mix, posting frequency, and seasonal planning
  • Community Management & Engagement — Responding to comments, managing negative feedback, building brand advocates, and crisis management
  • Influencer & Creator Marketing — Identifying micro-influencers, negotiation, campaign management, and measuring influencer ROI in Indian market
  • Social Media Analytics & Reporting — Understanding metrics (reach, engagement, impressions), tracking conversions, and presenting insights to stakeholders

Module 5: Email Marketing, Analytics & Marketing Automation

📚 Topics Covered

  • Email Marketing Fundamentals — Benefits, ROI potential, compliance (GDPR, India's DND rules), and best practices for Indian businesses
  • Email List Building — Lead magnets, signup forms, incentives, and growing subscriber base ethically for Indian audiences
  • Email Platform Overview — Mailchimp, ConvertKit, Active Campaign, and Brevo; choosing right tool for business needs
  • Email Campaign Creation — Welcome series, promotional campaigns, newsletter design, and automation workflows for customer retention
  • Email Copywriting & Design — Subject lines, preview text, CTA optimization, responsive design, and mobile-friendly templates
  • Segmentation & Personalization — Dividing audiences by behavior, demographics, preferences, and sending targeted messages for higher conversion
  • A/B Testing in Email — Testing subject lines, send times, content, and design; analyzing results and implementing improvements
  • Google Analytics Advanced — Setting up goals, funnels, user behavior tracking, custom dashboards, and attribution modeling
  • Marketing Automation — Workflows, trigger-based emails, lead scoring, nurturing sequences, and automating customer lifecycle marketing
  • Data Analysis & Reporting — Creating performance reports, visualizing data, calculating metrics, and presenting insights to management for decision-making

Module 6: Portfolio Development, Tools Mastery & Career Planning

📚 Topics Covered

  • Portfolio Building Essentials — Creating case studies, documenting campaigns, measuring results, and presenting work to potential employers
  • Real-World Campaign Execution — Planning, launching, and managing a complete digital marketing campaign from strategy to analysis
  • Canva & Design Basics — Creating social media graphics, email templates, infographics, and promotional materials without design experience
  • Advanced Google Analytics 4 — Understanding GA4 interface, event tracking, audience creation, and predictive analytics for Indian businesses
  • SEMrush & Competitive Analysis — Competitor keyword research, backlink analysis, content gap analysis, and benchmarking against Indian competitors
  • Tools Integration & Workflow Automation — Connecting tools (Zapier, Make), automating reporting, and streamlining marketing operations
  • Job Search & Interview Preparation — Resume building, LinkedIn optimization, portfolio presentation, and answering common digital marketing interview questions
  • Freelancing & Self-Employment — Setting up as freelancer, pricing services, finding clients, and managing projects for Indian startups and SMEs
  • Entrepreneurship in Digital Marketing — Starting digital agency, building client base, scaling operations, and creating recurring revenue models
  • Continuous Learning & Industry Updates — Staying current with trends, joining communities, certifications (Google, Meta, HubSpot), and professional development
